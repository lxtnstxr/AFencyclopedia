### AF 2018-03-21 0129
(00:08:00) Jack Burkman, a lobbyist, offered a $130,000 bounty in 2017 for information leading to the arrest or resolution in the murder of [[Seth Rich]], a [[Democratic Party|DNC]] staffer killed on July 10, 2016 in [[Washington D.C.|Washington]], D.C.

(00:09:30) Jack Burkman hired a private investigator, former Marine [[Kevin Doherty]], to look into the murder of [[Seth Rich]], but later fired him, leading to a dispute.
(00:10:40) [[Kevin Doherty]], posing as an informant, lured Jack Burkman to a parking garage with promises of emails about [[Andrew McCabe]], where he then shot Burkman twice and ran him over with an SUV.
(00:12:50) In January, Jack Burkman was assaulted outside his [[State of Virginia|Virginia]] home by a masked individual [[who]] pepper-sprayed him before fleeing in a [[black]] sports sedan.
