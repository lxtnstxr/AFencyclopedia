# Carl Williams III
#person

### AF 2020-05-11 0602
(00:50:00) In April 2017 in [[State of California|California]], [[Corey Ali Mohammed]], a [[black]] man, killed motel security guard Carl Williams III because he believed the guard was racist for asking him to pay a visitor's fee.

(00:51:00) After learning via a news app that he was wanted for the murder of Carl Williams III, [[Corey Ali Mohammed]] stated he decided to "kill as many white men as he could," leading to the killings of three more men, including [[Zachary Randles]].
