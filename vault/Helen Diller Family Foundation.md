# Helen Diller Family Foundation
#organization

### AF 2018-10-10 0258
(00:35:40) The Helen Diller Family Foundation, a supporting charity of the [[Jewish Community Federation of San Francisco]] whose board members include former [[Bank of America Corporation|Bank of America]] chairman [[Richard Rosenberg]], was found to have earmarked $100,000 for [[Canary Mission]].

(00:37:20) Funds from the Helen Diller Family Foundation were directed to [[Canary Mission]] via the [[Central Fund of Israel]] and an [[Israel|Israeli]] public benefit corporation called [[Megamot Shalom]], which was established to ensure "the national image and strength of the [[state of Israel]]" through information dissemination.
(00:40:40) The Helen Diller Family Foundation also provided funding to [[Project Veritas Action|Project Veritas]], run by [[James O'Keefe]], the [[American Freedom Defense Initiative]] founded by [[Robert Spencer]] and [[Pamela Geller]], and a foundation initiated by Dutch politician [[Geert Wilders]].
