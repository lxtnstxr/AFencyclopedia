# American Families Plan
#organization

### AF 2021-04-28 0801
(00:29:27) [[Joe Biden]] introduced the American Families Plan to address access to education, child care, paid leave, and direct payments to families.

(00:30:36) The American Families Plan guaranteed four additional years of public education for every [[person]] in [[United States|America]], including [[universal]] preschool for three- and four-year-olds and free community college.
(00:32:59) The American Families Plan guaranteed that low- and middle-income families would pay no more than 7% of their income for high-quality child care for children up to age five.
(00:33:37) The American Families Plan proposed providing up to 12 weeks of paid family and medical leave.
(00:34:05) The American Families Plan expanded the child tax credit to up to $3,000 per child under six and $3,600 for children over six, which [[Joe Biden]] stated would help cut child poverty in half.
