### AF 2017-08-28 0003
(07:10:32) At the [[Paramount Global|MTV]] Video Music Awards, Robert E. Lee IV, a descendant of General [[Robert E. Lee]], condemned his ancestor as an idol of white supremacy and racism and announced the launch of the [[Heather Heyer Foundation]] to provide scholarships for anti-racism work.

(07:49:70) [[Nicholas J. Fuentes]] condemned Robert E. Lee IV's speech as a "Marxist 101" ritual of self-condemnation, arguing that no other ethnic or religious group would publicly apologize for their ancestors' actions in a similar way.
