### AF 2020-03-10 0561
(00:03:23) [[Andrew Cuomo]], the Governor of [[New York]], deployed the [[U.S. Department of Defense|National Guard]] to establish a one-mile containment zone and lockdown in New Rochelle, a suburb of [[New York|New York City]], due to a coronavirus cluster.

(00:16:34) [[Nicholas J. Fuentes]] detailed [[Andrew Cuomo]]'s establishment of a one-mile coronavirus containment zone around New Rochelle, [[New York]], where the [[U.S. Department of Defense|National Guard]] would clean schools and deliver food, calling it the first such action in the [[United States]].
(00:17:19) [[Nicholas J. Fuentes]] reported [[New York]] had 173 active coronavirus cases, with 108 in [[Westchester County]] where New Rochelle is located, and [[New York|New York City]] had 36 confirmed cases.
(00:21:54) [[Nicholas J. Fuentes]] asserted it was impossible that New Rochelle (population 70,000) had 176 coronavirus cases while [[New York|New York City]] (population 8 million) had only 36, concluding the virus was spreading undetected nationwide.
(00:23:46) [[Nicholas J. Fuentes]] predicted the New Rochelle lockdown was only the beginning and that school closures, quarantines, and empty streets would occur across the [[United States]] in the coming weeks.
