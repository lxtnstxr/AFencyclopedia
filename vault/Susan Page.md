### AF 2020-10-07 0697
(00:04:46) The 2020 [[vice]] presidential debate between [[Mike Pence]] and [[Kamala Harris]] was held in [[City of Miami|Miami]] and moderated by Susan Page of [[Russia Today|USA Today]].

(00:18:25) Susan Page asked [[Mike Pence]] if he had an agreement with [[Donald Trump]] regarding safeguards for presidential disability, given [[Donald Trump]]'s age and recent COVID-19 hospitalization.
(00:23:32) Susan Page questioned both candidates about the transparency of presidential health information, citing [[Donald Trump]]'s doctors giving misleading answers about his COVID-19 condition.
(00:37:56) Susan Page asked [[Mike Pence]] if he believed, as the scientific community concluded, that man-made climate change made wildfires bigger and hurricanes more damaging.
(00:39:52) Susan Page noted [[Kamala Harris]] co-sponsored the Green New Deal in [[Congress]], while [[Joe Biden]] said he did not support it, asking for the [[Joe Biden|Biden-Harris]] administration's stance.
(00:42:31) Susan Page asked [[Mike Pence]] if he believed climate change posed an existential threat.
(00:47:56) Susan Page asked [[Mike Pence]] to describe the [[United States|U.S]]. relationship with [[China]] as competitors, adversaries, or enemies.
(00:49:56) Susan Page asked [[Kamala Harris]] the same question on how to describe the [[United States|U.S]]. relationship with [[China]].
(00:52:13) Susan Page asked [[Kamala Harris]] for her definition of [[United States|American]] leadership in 2020, given strains with [[China]] and traditional allies like [[North Atlantic Treaty Organization|NATO]].
(01:00:33) Susan Page enforced debate rules agreed upon by both campaigns and the [[Commission on Presidential Debates]], after [[Mike Pence]] complained about speaking [[time]].
(01:00:57) Susan Page noted the [[Senate Judiciary Committee]] would begin hearings on [[Amy Coney Barrett]]'s [[Supreme Court of the United States|Supreme Court]] nomination, asking [[Mike Pence]], if [[Roe v. Wade]] were overturned, what he would want [[India|Indiana]] to do.
(01:03:51) Susan Page asked [[Kamala Harris]], if [[Roe v. Wade]] were overturned, what she would want [[State of California|California]] to do regarding abortion restrictions.

(01:14:39) Susan Page asked [[Mike Pence]] the same question on whether justice was done for [[Breonna Taylor]].
(01:22:37) Susan Page asked [[Kamala Harris]] what steps she and [[Joe Biden]] would take if [[Donald Trump]] refused to accept a peaceful transfer of power after the election.
(01:25:04) Susan Page asked [[Mike Pence]] what his role and responsibility would be if [[Joe Biden]] were declared the winner and [[Donald Trump]] refused to accept a peaceful transfer of power.
