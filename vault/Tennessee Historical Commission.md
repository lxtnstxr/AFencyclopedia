# Tennessee Historical Commission

### AF 2020-05-14 0605
(00:24:46) The [[city]] of [[Memphis]] sold the [[parkland]] to the nonprofit [[Memphis Greenspace]] after the Tennessee Historical Commission denied a waiver to remove the [[Nathan Bedford Forrest]] statue under the [[Tennessee Heritage Protection Act]]; [[Memphis Greenspace]] subsequently removed the statue in December 2017 along with a statue of [[Jefferson Davis]].
