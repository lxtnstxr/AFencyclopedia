### AF 2023-02-14 1121
(01:08:42) [[Nicholas J. Fuentes]] reported that the [[United States|U.S.]] military shot down four high-altitude objects in February 2023: a [[China|Chinese]] spy balloon off the coast of [[South Carolina]], and then unidentified objects over [[Alaska]], [[Dominion of Canada|Canada]], and Lake Huron.

(01:15:08) [[Nicholas J. Fuentes]] pointed out that none of the three most recent objects shot down over [[Alaska]], [[Dominion of Canada|Canada]], and Lake Huron had been recovered or identified by authorities.
