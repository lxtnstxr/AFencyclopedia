# Rostov-on-Don
#city

### AF 2023-06-27 1181
(01:21:00) [[Nicholas J. Fuentes]] recounted that the head of the [[Wagner Group]], [[Yevgeny Prigozhin]], declared war on the [[Russian]] Ministry of Defense, captured military facilities in Rostov-on-Don, and began a march toward [[Moscow]] before turning back after a deal was brokered.


### AF 2023-06-24 1180
(01:00:00) [[Yevgeny Prigozhin]] led [[Wagner Group]] forces from [[Ukraine]] into [[Russia]], seizing control of the [[city]] of Rostov-on-Don, including its police headquarters and the Southern Military District Headquarters, reportedly without resistance from [[Russian]] military or [[U.S. Department of Defense|National Guard]] units.

### AF 2023-06-24 1180
(01:03:74) The [[Wagner Group]]'s advance on [[Moscow]] and seizure of Rostov-on-Don created a crisis of loyalty within the [[Russian]] military, with some reports indicating soldiers in [[Bakhmut]] and other units were sympathetic to [[Yevgeny Prigozhin]]'s criticisms of the defense ministry.
