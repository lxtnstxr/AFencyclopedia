### AF 2019-12-13 0513
(00:08:16) [[Elizabeth Warren]], [[Bernie Sanders]], [[Joe Biden]], [[Tom Steyer]], [[Peter Buttigieg|Pete Buttigieg]], and [[Amy Klobuchar]] announced they would boycott a [[Democratic Party|Democratic]] debate at Loyola Marymount University in solidarity with striking food service workers from [[Unite Here Local 11]].
