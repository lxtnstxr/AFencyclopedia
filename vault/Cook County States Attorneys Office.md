# Cook County State's Attorney's Office
#organization

### AF 2021-10-08 0890
(02:01:780) Prosecutors with the Cook County State's Attorney's Office dropped all charges against the five suspects in the [[City of Chicago|Chicago]] gang shootout, citing "mutual combat" as a reason for the rejection according to a police report obtained by the [[The Chicago Sun-Times|Chicago Sun Times]].

(02:25:240) The Cook County State's Attorney's Office issued a public statement contradicting the police report, claiming charges were dropped because evidence was insufficient to meet the burden of proof for felony charges.
