# Mike Garcia
#person

### AF 2020-05-13 0604
(00:35:51) [[Politico]] reported that [[Republican Party|Republican]] candidate Mike Garcia won a special election for [[State of California|California]]'s 25th congressional district, defeating [[Democratic Party|Democrat]] [[Christy Smith]].

(00:36:42) Mike Garcia's victory in [[State of California|California]]'s 25th district marked the first [[time]] in 22 years that a [[Republican Party|Republican]] had captured a congressional seat from [[Democratic Party|Democrats]] in the state.
(00:37:56) Mike Garcia, a former naval aviator and employee of the defense contractor [[Raytheon Technologies Corporation|Raytheon]], was nominated for the [[United States Naval Academy]] by former [[Republican Party|Republican]] congressman [[Buck McKeon]].
(00:38:34) [[Nicholas J. Fuentes]] noted that [[State of California|California]]'s 25th congressional district, which Mike Garcia won, had been carried by [[Hillary Clinton]] in the 2016 presidential election and by [[Katie Hill]] in the 2018 midterms.
