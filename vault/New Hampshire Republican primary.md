#organization

### AF 2024-01-25 1282
(01:06:00) [[Donald Trump]] won the New Hampshire Republican primary, defeating [[Nikki Haley]] by approximately eight to ten percentage points.

(01:07:00) [[Nikki Haley]] lost both the [[Iowa Republican caucuses]] and the New Hampshire Republican primary, with the next major contests scheduled for [[State of Nevada|Nevada]] on February 8 and [[South Carolina]] on February 24.
(01:09:00) In the New Hampshire Republican primary, approximately 70% of [[Nikki Haley]]'s voters were not registered [[Republican Party|Republicans]], with 13% being [[Democratic Party|Democrats]] who crossed over to vote against [[Donald Trump]].
