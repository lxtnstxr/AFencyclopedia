### AF 2020-04-17 0588
(00:01:26) Researchers in Santa Clara County, [[State of California|California]], found that the number of coronavirus cases may be 50 to 85 times higher than official tallies, suggesting a significant number of asymptomatic carriers.

(00:02:21) The antibody study in Santa Clara County indicated that the coronavirus death rate could be dramatically lower than the 5% suggested by confirmed case data, potentially as low as 0.05%.
