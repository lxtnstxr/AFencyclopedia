### AF 2020-05-14 0605
(00:03:24) [[Nicholas J. Fuentes]] announced a secondary topic for the show: the exhumation of [[Nathan Bedford Forrest]] and his wife's remains from a state park in [[City of Memphis|Memphis]], [[Tennessee]], following the removal of his monument by a private nonprofit, Memphis Greenspace.

(00:24:46) The [[city]] of [[City of Memphis|Memphis]] sold the [[parkland]] to the nonprofit Memphis Greenspace after the [[Tennessee Historical Commission]] denied a waiver to remove the [[Nathan Bedford Forrest]] statue under the [[Tennessee Heritage Protection Act]]; Memphis Greenspace subsequently removed the statue in December 2017 along with a statue of [[Jefferson Davis]].
