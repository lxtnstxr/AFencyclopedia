#organization

### AF 2020-11-02 0714
(00:42:17) The activist group Shut Down DC, part of the [[Sunrise Movement]] network, planned to fill [[Washington D.C.|Washington, D.C.]] with protesters on and after election day to shut down [[the White House]] and disrupt [[Congress]], regardless of the outcome.

(00:45:06) A spokesman for Shut Down DC stated on a leaked Zoom call that the group's first step in the post-election phase was to "start in the streets" at [[Black Lives Matter|BLM]] Plaza after 4 p.m. on election night, shut down [[the White House]] on November 5th, and shut down larger parts of D.C. on November 6th.
