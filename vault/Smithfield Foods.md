### AF 2020-04-13 0584
(11:23:01) Smithfield Foods closed its pork processing plant in Sioux Falls, [[United States|South Dakota]] indefinitely after 293 workers tested positive for coronavirus, a facility responsible for 4-5% of the [[country]]'s pork production.

(11:59:89) [[Kenneth Sullivan]], [[the President]] and CEO of Smithfield Foods, warned that the closure of his plant and others across the industry was pushing the [[United States]] "perilously close to the edge in terms of our meat supply" and that it was impossible to keep grocery stores stocked if plants were not running.
