### AF 2021-05-11 0809
(00:03:36) [[Nicholas J. Fuentes]] stated that the Colonial Pipeline, responsible for a large percentage of fuel distribution on the southeastern coast of the [[United States]], was shut down due to a ransomware attack by a criminal group called DarkSide.

### AF 2021-05-11 0809
(00:06:28) An article from [[CNBC]] reported that if the Colonial Pipeline was not restored by the weekend, prices would rise and localized fuel shortages would spread across the southeastern and mid-[[The Atlantic|Atlantic]] regions of the [[United States]].

### AF 2021-05-11 0809
(00:08:10) The DarkSide cybercrime group's ransomware attack resulted in the shutdown of 5,500 miles of the Colonial Pipeline, which supplies half of the gasoline to the East Coast of the [[United States]].

### AF 2021-05-11 0809
(00:08:30) [[Jennifer Granholm]], the [[United States]] Energy Secretary, said federal agencies were working to help the Colonial Pipeline return to normal operations.
