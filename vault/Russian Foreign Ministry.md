### AF 2021-02-19 0763
(00:28:18) [[Nicholas J. Fuentes]] reported that the Russian Foreign Ministry issued a statement condemning the [[United States]] government under [[Joe Biden]] for a persecution campaign against individuals who dispute the results of the latest presidential election.

(00:30:05) The Russian Foreign Ministry accused the [[Federal Bureau of Investigation|FBI]] of opening over 400 criminal cases and detaining around 200 people in connection with the [[January 6th Attack|January 6th]] [[United States Capitol|Capitol]] events, while also criticizing the objectivity of [[United States|U.S]]. law enforcement agencies.
(00:32:35) The Russian Foreign Ministry called on the [[United States]] to respect the basic human rights of [[Donald Trump|Trump]] supporters, accusing [[United States|U.S]]. officials of hypocritically ignoring these rights domestically while advocating for them abroad.
