## Summary

Richard Grenell served as Acting Director of National Intelligence after being Ambassador to Germany and was a former aide to John Bolton. Julian Assange's lawyer alleged former Congressman Dana Rohrabacher offered Assange a pardon to deny Russian involvement in the DNC email hack, a claim Rohrabacher and White House Press Secretary Stephanie Grisham denied. The Trump administration's economic advisors argued companies like Facebook and Google were not monopolistic problems, while Chief of Staff Mick Mulvaney advocated for increased legal immigration to fuel economic growth. Bernie Sanders was the Democratic frontrunner, with Michael Bloomberg participating in a Nevada debate despite not being on the ballot, as the state planned to use an app similar to Iowa's problematic one.
