## Summary

The United States negotiated with the Taliban to reduce troop levels in Afghanistan, where over 2,400 American soldiers had died. Donald Trump announced tariffs on Chinese imports and downplayed North Korea's missile tests, while also intervening in the Swedish assault case of rapper ASAP Rocky. The Trump administration's eight rounds of talks with the Taliban sought assurances Afghanistan would not harbor terrorists.
