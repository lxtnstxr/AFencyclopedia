## Summary

Luke O'Brien authored a Huffington Post article listing over 60 individuals and groups, arguing Twitter's inaction was tied to Section 230 legal protections. Nicholas J. Fuentes described a network where journalists compiled lists for organizations like Media Matters to pressure social media platforms, and noted Facebook banned white nationalists after the Christchurch shooting.
