## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes began hosting "The Nicholas J. Fuentes Show" at his high school television station in November 2015. His program "America First" debuted on Right Side Broadcasting Network in February 2017 after Cassie Dillon recruited him from Boston University. Fuentes later partnered with James Allsup under America First Media before establishing an independent show, which entered a "rise" phase in February 2019. He credited individuals including Savant, Jacob Seals, Joe Seals, and Mike Maloney for instrumental support in the show's development and growth.
