## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes hosts the America First program from a studio in Chicago. Donald Trump tweeted about DACA negotiations with Democratic leaders Nancy Pelosi and Chuck Schumer, prompting criticism from Ann Coulter and Sean Hannity. Fuentes argued Trump's DACA statements were a tactical pressure campaign on Republican leaders Paul Ryan and Mitch McConnell to fund a border wall.
