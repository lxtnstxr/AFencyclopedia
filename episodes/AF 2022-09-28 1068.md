## Summary

Vladimir Putin announced and held referendums in the Ukrainian territories of Luhansk, Donetsk, Zaporizhzhia, and Kherson, which subsequently voted to join Russia. The Danish and German governments concluded the Nord Stream 1 and Nord Stream 2 pipelines were sabotaged, with the United States CIA having warned Germany in advance and the U.S. having long opposed the pipelines. Antony Blinken stated the pipeline destruction was in no one's interest but presented an opportunity for Europe to transition away from Russian natural gas, which had supplied 40% of Europe's needs.
