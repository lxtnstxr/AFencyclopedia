## Summary

A BBC investigation revealed that Andrew Tate's "War Room" organization was allegedly built on the digital trafficking of women for sex work and pornography, with leaked chats showing instructions to groom and coerce victims. The report identified Miles Sonkin as the group's leader and detailed allegations of encouraged violence and a "PhD" course teaching exploitation. Separately, Florida Governor Ron DeSantis was loudly booed at a vigil for the black victims of a racially motivated mass shooting in Jacksonville, an incident that commentators used to discuss broader racial tensions.
