## Summary

Lebanese Prime Minister Saad Hariri resigned and fled to Saudi Arabia, blaming Hezbollah and Iran. Saudi Arabia closed all crossings with Yemen after Houthi rebels launched a missile at Riyadh and Crown Prince Mohammed bin Salman oversaw a major anti-corruption purge. The United States deployed three aircraft carriers to the West Pacific and the Pentagon stated disarming North Korea would require a ground invasion. The House Intelligence Committee secured indictments against Paul Manafort, Rick Gates, and George Papadopoulos related to its investigation.
