## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes announced anti-vaccine mandate rallies at Pfizer World Headquarters and Gracie Mansion in New York City. The NBA mandated COVID-19 booster shots for vaccinated players, and the CDC director expanded booster eligibility against advisory panel recommendations.
