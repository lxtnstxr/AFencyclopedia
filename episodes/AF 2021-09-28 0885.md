## Summary

Governor Kathy Hochul of New York State announced a plan to replace unvaccinated hospital staff with National Guard personnel. Rochelle Walensky, director of the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention, overruled FDA and CDC advisors to recommend Pfizer booster shots for certain occupational groups. Nicholas J. Fuentes announced an anti-vaccine mandate rally at the Illinois State Capitol and reported that 16% of New York State hospital staff faced termination for being unvaccinated.
