## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes stated investigators found evidence of a lobbying industry involving Israel during the Trump transition. Michael Flynn pleaded guilty to lying to the FBI and disclosed that a senior Trump transition official directed him to make overtures to Russia in December 2016. BuzzFeed and Newsweek reported Jared Kushner ordered Flynn to lobby against a UN resolution on Israeli settlements and failed to disclose his foundation role, which funded West Bank settlements. Donald Trump issued an unconditional endorsement to Roy Moore ahead of the Alabama Senate election, where betting markets gave Moore a 75% chance of victory.
