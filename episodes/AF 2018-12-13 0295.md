## Summary

The United States Senate passed a resolution invoking the War Powers Resolution to withdraw American support for the Saudi-led war in Yemen and unanimously passed a resolution condemning Mohammed bin Salman for the killing of Jamal Khashoggi. Nicholas J. Fuentes criticized quantum physicist Julian von Abele for political activism and warned him against interviews with figures from The Right Stuff.
