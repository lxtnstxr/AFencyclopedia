## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes attended the 2017 Charlottesville "Unite the Right" rally to protest immigration and the removal of the Robert E. Lee monument. Fuentes argued that Confederate monuments and the Confederate flag are legitimate parts of American heritage and symbols of rebellion against central government overreach. Sneako asserted that the Confederate flag represents secession and slavery, making it anti-American and unworthy of public honor. Fuentes posited that national guilt over slavery and the Confederacy leads to detrimental policies like the removal of historical monuments.
