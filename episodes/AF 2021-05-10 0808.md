## Summary

Axios reported that Donald Trump's political ideology and policy positions on trade and immigration had become institutionalized within the Republican Party. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued that figures like Kevin McCarthy and Mitch McConnell were not genuine Trumpists and that the party was co-opting the "America First" brand. Fuentes stated that Trump had endorsed candidates like Tim Scott and Marco Rubio, whom he characterized as insufficiently aligned with a Trumpian agenda on issues like immigration and foreign policy.
