## Summary

Senator Mike Lee sponsored an immigration bill to eliminate per-country caps on high-skilled visas, a measure passed by the Republican-controlled Senate via unanimous consent. The National Defense Authorization Act contained an amendment by Senator Elizabeth Warren to rename U.S. military bases with Confederate names, while Republican senators refused to include President Donald Trump's demanded repeal of Section 230. FCC Chairman Ajit Pai announced his resignation effective January 20, 2021, and the Senate advanced the nomination of Nathan Simington to the commission via a party-line committee vote. Nicholas J. Fuentes traveled to multiple states for political events related to election fraud allegations and advocated for Republicans to withhold votes in the Georgia Senate runoff elections.
