## Summary

Glenn Youngkin won the 2021 Virginia gubernatorial election, defeating Terry McAuliffe in a state Joe Biden had carried in 2020. Nicholas J. Fuentes asserted the 2020 presidential election was stolen through systemic fraud in six key swing states. Residents of Minneapolis voted down a referendum to abolish the city's police department following the death of George Floyd. Fuentes argued that Republican victories on cultural issues demonstrated a winning strategy for the party.
