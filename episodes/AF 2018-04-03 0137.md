## Summary

Donald Trump threatened to cancel NAFTA and revoke aid to Honduras if Mexico did not stop a migrant caravan from reaching the United States border. The Mexican government subsequently announced it would disband the caravan, claiming the decision was not made under pressure from Trump. Nicholas J. Fuentes disavowed his association with Paul Nealon, citing campaign finance irregularities and the doxxing of Ricky Vaughn. Fuentes also declined a job offer from an immigration think tank after forming America First Media with James Allsup and Matt.
