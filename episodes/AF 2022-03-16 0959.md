## Summary

Volodymyr Zelensky stated Ukraine is unlikely to ever join NATO in addresses to the Canadian Parliament and the UK's Joint Expeditionary Force. The United States imposed sanctions banning Russian oil imports and barring Russia from the SWIFT payment system. India is planning to purchase discounted Russian oil and Saudi Arabia is in talks with China to price oil sales in Yuan, defying U.S. pressure. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued the U.S. sanctions have accelerated a de-dollarization trend that strengthens a Russia-China alliance.
