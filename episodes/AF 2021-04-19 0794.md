## Summary

Fuentes stated Democrats, led by Steny Hoyer, planned a vote on Washington, D.C. statehood legislation, a bill that had the support of 44 Democratic senators according to Tom Carper. Fuentes argued Democrats were pursuing systemic changes including Supreme Court expansion and immigration reform, contrasting this with the Republican Party's restraint under unified government in 2016. He also described Donald Trump's 2016 campaign as a unique, iconoclastic political movement distinct from standard Republican politics.
