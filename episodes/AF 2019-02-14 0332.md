## Summary

A proposed immigration appropriations bill passed the United States Senate by a vote of 83 to 16. The bill allocated $1.375 billion for pedestrian fencing in the Rio Grande Valley Sector with numerous restrictions on its construction. Nicholas J. Fuentes characterized provisions within the bill, such as Section 224's deportation immunity for sponsors of unaccompanied minors, as a form of amnesty.
