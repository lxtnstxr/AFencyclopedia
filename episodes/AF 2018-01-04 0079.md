## Summary

Laura Loomer asserted that the FBI and Las Vegas police had been caught lying about the official timeline of the 2017 Las Vegas shooting. Loomer and Nicholas J. Fuentes detailed Jewish leadership at numerous media companies, including CNN, The New York Times, and NBC. Loomer distinguished between "globalist Jews" who fund globalist policies and "nationalist Jews" who are pro-Israel and anti-Islam. Fuentes cited General Social Survey data showing Jewish Americans voted 71% for Hillary Clinton and are more likely to hold liberal social positions.
