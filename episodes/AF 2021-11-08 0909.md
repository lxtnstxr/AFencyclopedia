## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes broadcast from New York City for anti-vaccine mandate protests after traveling by train due to being on a federal no-fly list. Steve Bannon predicted the Republican Party would win 50% of the black vote in the 2022 elections, a claim Fuentes contested by citing a Virginia election result. At the COP26 summit in Glasgow, Barack Obama urged young activists to fight climate change and criticized Donald Trump's stance on climate science. During his trial, Kyle Rittenhouse faced homicide charges for shootings in Kenosha, Wisconsin, where a prosecution witness testified he pointed a gun at Rittenhouse before being shot.
