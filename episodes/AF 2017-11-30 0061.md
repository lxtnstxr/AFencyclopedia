## Summary

Roy Moore led in polls for the Alabama special Senate election following his primary defeat of Luther Strange, despite allegations published by the Washington Post and calls for his withdrawal from figures like Mitch McConnell and Paul Ryan. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued that Republican leaders and mainstream media lost credibility for opposing Roy Moore, who maintained polling leads. Donald Trump's administration experienced high turnover, with officials like Rex Tillerson slated to be replaced by Mike Pompeo. Nicholas J. Fuentes hosts the nightly program *America First* and faced criticism for a tweet stating workplace harassment would not occur if women were at home raising children.
