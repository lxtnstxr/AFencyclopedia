## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes contrasted the 55% audience score for *Star Wars: The Last Jedi* on Rotten Tomatoes with its 93% critic score. He asserted Disney's acquisitions of Marvel, Lucasfilm, and Pixar gave it dominant control over children's entertainment. Fuentes claimed the film presented an anti-white narrative by depicting a Resistance of women and minorities against a villainous First Order of white men. He stated actor Mark Hamill was unhappy with director Rian Johnson's portrayal of Luke Skywalker as a bitter hermit.
