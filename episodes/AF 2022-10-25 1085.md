## Summary

Ari Emanuel called for a business boycott of Kanye West, leading Endeavor-owned William Morris Endeavor and rival agencies CAA and UTA to do the same. Russian officials Sergei Shoigu and Valery Gerasimov alleged Ukraine planned a dirty bomb false flag, claims rejected by the British Ministry of Defence. The Biden administration and NATO, led by Jens Stoltenberg, rejected Ukraine's expedited membership application and calls for direct military intervention.
