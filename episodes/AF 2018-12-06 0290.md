## Summary

Neil deGrasse Tyson faced multiple sexual misconduct allegations, prompting Fox and *Cosmos* producers to open an investigation. Marc Lamont Hill was fired from CNN following comments about Palestine and Israel. Senators Ted Cruz, Mike Rounds, Jim Inhofe, and John Kennedy introduced the WALL Act to fund a border wall by closing loopholes for illegal immigrants. Conservative networks The Blaze and CRTV merged after The Blaze faced financial difficulties and laid off staff.
