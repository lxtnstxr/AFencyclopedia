## Summary

Marjorie Taylor Greene apologized to the Republican Jewish Coalition for comparing COVID-19 vaccine mandates to the Holocaust, after condemnations from Kevin McCarthy and Mitch McConnell. Nicholas J. Fuentes criticized Greene and Matt Gaetz for excluding figures like himself from their movement and attended the 2017 Charlottesville rally protesting the removal of Confederate monuments. The Charlottesville City Council voted to remove statues of Robert E. Lee and Stonewall Jackson, following a deadly 2017 rally that Joe Biden later cited in his presidential campaign announcement.
