## Summary

Bernie Sanders argued that providing abortion and birth control access in developing countries is a necessary response to population growth and climate change. Donald Trump replaced Middle East envoy Jason Greenblatt with Avi Berkowitz and thanked Greenblatt for his dedication to Israel. Attorney Deborah Katz stated Christine Blasey Ford's testimony against Brett Kavanaugh was intended to influence public perception ahead of potential Supreme Court rulings on abortion.
