## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes recorded a 2018 election podcast episode covering Paul Ryan and white working-class voting patterns. Following a chemical weapons attack in Syria, Israel launched retaliatory missile strikes, and Donald Trump vowed a joint response with French President Emmanuel Macron and British Prime Minister Theresa May. Russia threatened to shoot down any missiles fired at Syria and urged the United States not to take action. U.S. Defense Secretary Jim Mattis stated the United States had no intention of engaging in Syria's civil war and lacked confirmation of responsibility for the chemical attack.
