## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes previously hosted his show on Right Side Broadcasting Network until its cancellation following the Charlottesville rally. Fuentes argued that Donald Trump's political success tapped into a public sentiment that America had lost its former greatness. He criticized Ariana Grande for renouncing her Roman Catholic faith and for her song "God is a Woman," which he characterized as blasphemous. Fuentes agreed with Patrick Little's factual case regarding Israel and the USS Liberty attack but lamented that his rhetoric undermined the argument.
