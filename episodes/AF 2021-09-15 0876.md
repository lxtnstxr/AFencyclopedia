## Summary

Mark Milley, Chairman of the Joint Chiefs under Donald Trump, contacted Chinese General Li Zuocheng in 2020 and 2021 to assure him the United States would not attack. A study by Harvard Medical School and others found over 50% of COVID-19 hospitalizations during the Delta surge were for mild or asymptomatic cases. Nicholas J. Fuentes announced demonstrations against vaccine mandates and promoted a film recut from his "White Boy Summer" vlog series. The Washington Post first reported the details of Milley's calls in a book by Bob Woodward and Robert Costa.
