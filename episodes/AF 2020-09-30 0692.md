## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes hosted a live stream covering the first presidential debate between Donald Trump and Joe Biden. Donald Trump refused to condemn the Proud Boys when prompted by moderator Chris Wallace, telling the group to "stand back and stand by." Approximately a dozen media outlets, including the Washington Post and Time Magazine, called for the cancellation of the remaining presidential debates. The Biden campaign committed to participating in the debates scheduled for October 15th and 22nd.
