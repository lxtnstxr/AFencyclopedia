## Summary

Donald Trump imposed a travel ban on several countries including Somalia, Iran, and Cuba. John McCain voted against repealing the Affordable Care Act in 2017, and his daughter Meghan McCain responded to Trump's criticism of him from the set of *The View*. Following Trump's order for a withdrawal from Syria, Defense Secretary Jim Mattis resigned and the timeline was later extended after a visit from Senator Lindsey Graham.
