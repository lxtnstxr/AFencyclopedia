## Summary

Over 600 rockets were launched at Israel from the Gaza Strip, resulting in Israeli and Palestinian deaths before a ceasefire was negotiated. The Trump administration authorized a cumulative 60,000 additional H-2B visas in 2019 through DHS Secretaries Kirstjen Nielsen and Kevin McAleenan. Facebook and Instagram banned figures including Laura Loomer, Paul Joseph Watson, and Alex Jones from their platforms. In 2003, Ben Shapiro wrote a Townhall article advocating for the transfer of Palestinian Arabs from Israel, a position he later disavowed.
