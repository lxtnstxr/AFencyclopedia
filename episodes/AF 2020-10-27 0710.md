## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes described riots in Philadelphia following the police shooting of Walter Wallace, which led to 91 arrests and a Pennsylvania National Guard deployment. Fuentes stated that Twitter introduced prompts to warn users about potential election misinformation regarding topics like Hunter Biden and mail-in voting. He expressed confidence in Donald Trump's 2020 election chances but argued that social media platforms like Facebook, Google, and Twitter systematically censored his supporters. Fuentes attributed racial disparities in policing and business practices to behavioral issues within black communities, criticizing figures like Martin Luther King Jr.
