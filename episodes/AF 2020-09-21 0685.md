## Summary

Trump issued and then delayed an executive order to ban TikTok, leading ByteDance to negotiate a sale of its U.S. operations to Oracle. Republican senators Susan Collins and Lisa Murkowski stated they would not confirm a nominee before the 2020 election, while a confirmation would establish a 6-3 conservative Court majority. Nicholas J. Fuentes, banned from Amazon's Twitch and Google's YouTube, identified major tech companies as hostile and declared war on mainstream conservative organizations.
