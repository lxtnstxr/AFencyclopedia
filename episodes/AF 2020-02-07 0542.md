## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes streamed coverage of the Democratic debate after discovering its schedule on Twitter. Joe Biden argued Donald Trump would label the Democratic ticket as socialist if Bernie Sanders were the nominee. Bernie Sanders claimed he won the Iowa caucus popular vote by 6,000 votes and advocated for Medicare for All to save money over the current system. Amy Klobuchar stated two-thirds of Senate Democrats did not support Sanders's Medicare for All bill, which she argued would displace 149 million Americans from their insurance.
