## Summary

Stephen Paddock killed 59 people in the 2017 Las Vegas shooting from the Mandalay Bay hotel. Nicholas J. Fuentes cited Ted Kaczynski, Adam Lanza, and James Holmes as examples of isolated individuals, while recommending authors like Julius Evola and Pat Buchanan. Fuentes proposed policy goals of restricting immigration and abolishing the Federal Reserve, and argued a cultural movement must precede political organization.
