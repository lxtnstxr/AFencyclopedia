## Summary

The Supreme Court of the United States declined to hear a Republican challenge to Pennsylvania's extended mail-in ballot deadline and declined to block a New York subpoena for Donald Trump's tax returns. Nicholas J. Fuentes announced the speaker list for the second annual America First Political Action Conference, which included Michelle Malkin and former Congressman Steve King. A Suffolk University/USA Today poll found that 46% of Republicans would follow Donald Trump if he started a new party, while only 27% would remain with the Republican Party. Donald Trump criticized Senate Minority Leader Mitch McConnell as a "dour and unsmiling political hack" in a statement.
