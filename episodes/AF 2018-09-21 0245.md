## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes characterized Cody Wilson as an enemy of the state for his work with Defense Distributed, following Wilson's arrest in Taiwan for extradition to Texas. Fuentes argued that Protestantism's individualist underpinnings underlie many ideological problems in America and contrasted the global history of the Catholic Church with the Eastern Orthodox Church's contested authority. He described himself as an integralist who believes America's laws should be informed by Catholic values and suggested Vladimir Putin's rule represents a modern form of monarchy.
