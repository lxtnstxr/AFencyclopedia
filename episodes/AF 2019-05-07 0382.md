## Summary

Twitter suspended Mike Morrison for a parody account of Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez and temporarily banned David Horowitz. Alex Jones was banned from Twitter after harassing a reporter at a congressional hearing attended by Jack Dorsey. Antifa members clashed with Joey Gibson's Patriot Prayer group in Portland, where Andy Ngo documented acts of vandalism and police inaction. Following the Charlottesville rally, participants like Sean McCaffrey were doxed and barred from employment, unlike participants in the Portland Antifa rally. Pennsylvania State Representative Brian Sims harassed teenagers and an elderly woman outside a Philadelphia Planned Parenthood clinic and offered money to dox them. Virginia Governor Ralph Northam defended post-birth abortion and remained in office despite controversy over past KKK imagery. Jared Kushner led the development of a Trump administration immigration bill, which Donald Trump approved after a meeting with GOP senators.
