## Summary

Michael Flynn pleaded guilty to lying to the FBI about a December 2016 phone call with Russian ambassador Sergey Kislyak, during which he requested Russia delay a UN Security Council vote condemning Israel. The Obama administration obtained a FISA warrant to surveil the Trump campaign using a dossier compiled by Christopher Steele, who was hired by Fusion GPS through law firm Perkins Coie, which was retained by the Hillary Clinton campaign and the DNC. Israel receives at least $3.8 billion in annual U.S. foreign aid and has conducted airstrikes in Syria, while Saudi Arabia considers Lebanon a government at war due to its cooperation with Hezbollah. Nicholas J. Fuentes advocated for revoking dual citizenship and criticized the term "Judeo-Christian," citing Thomas Aquinas's *Summa Theologica* as a demonstration for God's existence.
