## Summary

Matt Brainerd organized a September 18 rally at the U.S. Capitol in support of January 6 defendants. Donald Trump opposed the rally and planned to be at his New Jersey golf resort that day, while Representatives Marjorie Taylor Greene and Madison Cawthorn canceled their speaking appearances. The Department of Homeland Security estimated approximately 700 attendees for the event.
