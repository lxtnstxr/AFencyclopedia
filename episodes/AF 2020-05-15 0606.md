## Summary

The United States Department of Justice and several state attorneys general prepared antitrust lawsuits against Google in 2020. Donald Trump launched Operation Warp Speed, appointing Moncef Slaoui and General Gustav Perna to accelerate coronavirus vaccine development. Governors including Andrew Cuomo held the authority to reopen their states, while Anthony Fauci contradicted Trump's push to lift restrictions. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued that access to social media was essential for dissident movements and that the Democratic Party focused on acquiring political power through institutional changes.
