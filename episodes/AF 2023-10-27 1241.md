## Summary

The United States conducted airstrikes on Iranian positions in Syria and deployed additional troops to the Middle East in response to attacks on American bases by Iranian-backed militias. The episode extensively analyzed the Israel-Hamas war, with commentator Nicholas J. Fuentes arguing that U.S. support for Israel draws America into broader regional conflicts and that pro-Israel lobbying heavily influences U.S. policy. Fuentes also appeared in a contentious interview on InfoWars, where he debated these topics and claimed that domestic pro-Palestine activism represented the primary opposition to such lobbying efforts.
