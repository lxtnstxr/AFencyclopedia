## Summary

Bill de Blasio faced protests from New York City workers over a strict vaccine mandate without a testing opt-out. Facebook rebranded its parent company to Meta, which owns Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, and Messenger. Mark Zuckerberg is heavily investing Meta's future in developing the metaverse, a planned digital environment. Nicholas J.
