## Summary

Ron DeSantis proposed anti-big tech legislation in Florida targeting Amazon, Apple, Facebook, Google, and Twitter with fines and user lawsuit provisions. Major anti-lockdown protests occurred across Europe, including in the Netherlands, Belgium, Austria, Hungary, the Czech Republic, and Poland. Alexei Navalny was sentenced to three and a half years in prison in Russia. Nicholas J. Fuentes criticized several U.S. politicians and identified COVID lockdowns, Big Tech censorship, and election fraud as urgent populist issues.
