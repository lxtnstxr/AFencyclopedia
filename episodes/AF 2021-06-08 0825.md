## Summary

ProPublica obtained IRS records showing the top 25 richest Americans paid an effective tax rate as low as 0.1% on true income, with Warren Buffett paying a 0.1% rate on $24.3 billion in wealth growth. The FBI seized approximately $2.3 million in Bitcoin from the DarkSide hacking group, which had executed a ransomware attack against Colonial Pipeline. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued the Colonial Pipeline attack was a false flag operation and criticized Donald Trump for betraying his supporters and endorsing establishment Republicans.
