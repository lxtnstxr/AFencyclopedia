## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes announced a one-year anniversary call-in special for his show and referenced previous technical difficulties. The Philadelphia Eagles defeated the New England Patriots in the Super Bowl, after which riots involving property damage occurred in Philadelphia. United States troops began a withdrawal from Iraq, with some forces being redeployed to Afghanistan. The United States invaded Iraq in 2003 based on intelligence from Israel and other sources that was later found to be faulty.
