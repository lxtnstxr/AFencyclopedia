## Summary

The commentary focused on the Israel-Hamas war that began in October 2023, detailing Hamas's initial attack and Israel's military response, including a ground offensive and a complete blockade of the Gaza Strip. It argued that reports of atrocities were used as propaganda to galvanize support and warned that the conflict risked escalating into a wider regional war involving Hezbollah, Iran, and potentially the United States. The discussion also placed the conflict within a broader geopolitical framework, suggesting that U.S. support for Israel's actions could accelerate a shift toward a multipolar world order involving China and Russia.
