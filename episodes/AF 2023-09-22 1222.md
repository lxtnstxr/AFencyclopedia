## Summary

The Biden administration extended Temporary Protected Status to approximately 500,000 Venezuelan nationals, allowing them to work and deferring deportation. Concurrently, a faction of House Republicans, including Marjorie Taylor Greene and members of the Freedom Caucus, sought to force a government shutdown to demand stronger border security measures, opposing a procedural vote on a Pentagon funding bill. The episode also detailed the ongoing strain on major U.S. cities from migrant arrivals and reviewed past immigration policy shifts and funding battles during the Trump administration.
