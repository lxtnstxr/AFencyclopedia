## Summary

Donald Trump advocated for a border wall in an Oval Office address and later met with Chuck Schumer and Nancy Pelosi, who refused to fund it. A Politico/Morning Consult poll showed 44% of Americans supported a border wall, while 49% supported Trump's proposed withdrawal from Syria. John Bolton was refused a meeting with Recep Tayyip Erdoğan in Turkey, and a secret regional meeting concluded Turkey was a greater threat than Iran.
