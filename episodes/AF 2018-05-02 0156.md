## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes recently participated in a stream with Richard Spencer and was a guest on J.F. Gharapi's show *The Public Space*. Alex Witteslawski began his political background as a Ron Paul supporter and worked as a political operative for the Leadership Institute, where he helped defeat campus speech codes. Witteslawski characterized the 2017 Charlottesville rally as a disaster and argued U.S. strikes in Syria harmed the Assad government instead of helping destroy ISIS. He contended Donald Trump requires dissident right support to win states like Michigan and Pennsylvania, and that activists should infiltrate local Republican Party apparatuses.
