## Summary

Stripe forced the closure of Nicholas J. Fuentes's credit card donation option on his Streamlabs account. The Chinese military inserted microchips onto motherboards in China that were shipped to Supermicro in California for assembly into servers sold to Apple, the Department of Defense, and the CIA. Republican Senator Jeff Flake stated an FBI report contained no corroborating information for claims against Supreme Court nominee Brett Kavanaugh, while Democratic Senators Heidi Heitkamp and Joe Donnelly announced they would not vote to confirm him. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued China was the primary great-power rival to the United States and that the two nations were in a state of war conducted through economic and cyber means.
