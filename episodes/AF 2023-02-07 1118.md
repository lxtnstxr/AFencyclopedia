## Summary

Joe Biden signed the bipartisan Infrastructure Investment and Jobs Act, the largest such investment since the Eisenhower interstate system. Biden's administration committed $1.6 billion to repair the Brent Spence Bridge and launched a national effort to replace lead pipes. The Inflation Reduction Act, also signed by Biden, caps insulin costs and Medicare drug spending while representing a historic climate investment. Biden's policies reduced bank overdraft fees, banned non-compete agreements for millions, and cut the deficit by over $1.7 trillion.
