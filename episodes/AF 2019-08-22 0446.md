## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes hosts the political commentary program America First. Donald Trump intervened diplomatically to secure the release of ASAP Rocky from Swedish detention in early August 2019. Breitbart News drew attention to past offensive tweets by New York Times editor Tom Wright-Piersanti, for which he apologized. Nicholas J. Fuentes characterized Congressman Dan Crenshaw as a "gentile Zionist neocon" and argued Hasan Piker should be permanently suspended from Twitch.
