## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes hosts the America First program, which began on Right Side Broadcasting Network from a Boston University dorm. A high school shooting at Marshall County High School in Benton, Kentucky, resulted in two deaths and 17 injuries. Peter Strzok and Lisa Page were involved in 50,000 missing text messages from December 2016 to May 2017 that the FBI attributed to technical issues with Samsung phones. Paul Nealon received a seven-day suspension from Twitter following a public theological debate with Ben Shapiro, Ali Stuckey, Cassie Dillon, and Elliot Hamilton.
