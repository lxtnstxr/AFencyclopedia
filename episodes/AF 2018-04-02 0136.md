## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes announced the return of his premium podcast America First World Report and identified William Nardi as the author of a Vice article labeling him a white supremacist. A BuzzFeed report described a caravan of 1,000 to 1,500 migrants, organized by Pueblos Sin Fronteras, traveling through Mexico toward the United States. Donald Trump tweeted that Mexico must stop the caravan or he would terminate NAFTA, calling on Congress for tougher immigration laws. Israel had a population of 42,000 African migrants and later canceled a United Nations resettlement deal due to domestic political pressure.
