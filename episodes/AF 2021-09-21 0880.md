## Summary

The Food and Drug Administration voted against recommending COVID-19 booster shots for the general population, citing myocarditis risks for young men. France recalled its ambassadors from the United States and Australia after Australia canceled a submarine contract with France to instead acquire nuclear submarines through the AUKUS pact with the United States and United Kingdom. Nicholas J. Fuentes announced plans for an anti-vaccine rally in Illinois and argued that American dissidents could find common cause with adversarial states like China and Russia.
