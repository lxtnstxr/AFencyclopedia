## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes advocated for joining the Republican Party and local political campaigns as the most effective form of activism. Fuentes defended the right to own semi-automatic firearms and recommended the Gun Owners of America for advocacy. He argued the Republican Party must reorient itself to be more labor-oriented to secure working-class voters. Fuentes cited analytics showing his "America First" audience was 95% male and 73% based in the United States.
