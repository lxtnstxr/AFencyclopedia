## Summary

PewDiePie became the first individual creator to surpass 100 million subscribers on YouTube. Donald Trump fired National Security Advisor John Bolton, whom he had appointed in 2018. Nicholas J. Fuentes announced he would host live commentary for a Democratic primary debate on DLive, a platform PewDiePie supported.

(00:10:00) [[PewDiePie]] surpassed 100 million subscribers, becoming the first individual creator to reach that milestone on [[YouTube]].

(00:21:00) [[Donald Trump]] fired National Security Advisor [[John Bolton]] after a series of disagreements, including over negotiations with the [[Taliban]] and trade talks with [[China]].

(00:21:00) [[John Bolton]] was appointed National Security Advisor in April 2018, a move widely attributed to pressure from major donor [[Sheldon Adelson]].
