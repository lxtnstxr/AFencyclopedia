## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes interpreted Donald Trump's "tired of winning" campaign statement as being directed at Israel and Jewish people. The Donald Trump administration moved toward officially recognizing the Golan Heights as sovereign Israeli territory, an important issue for Benjamin Netanyahu and the Likud party. Andrew Yang's universal basic income proposal, the "freedom dividend," was central to his presidential platform and reportedly appealed to figures including Richard Spencer and Nicholas J. Fuentes. Nicholas J. Fuentes cited United States Census Bureau data to argue that forms of universal basic income already existed for significant percentages of senior, black, and Hispanic Americans.
