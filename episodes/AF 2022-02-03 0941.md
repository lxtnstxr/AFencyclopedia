## Summary

The Canadian government mandated vaccination for cross-border truckers, prompting a "Freedom Convoy" protest that locked down Ottawa and disrupted a key Alberta border crossing. Ottawa police reported significant U.S. involvement in the protest, which included the defacement of monuments. Airbnb banned Michelle Malkin and Nicholas J. Fuentes, with Malkin citing SPLC designations and planning a lawsuit under California consumer protection laws. Major digital platforms like Airbnb, Uber, and Twitter outsource content moderation decisions to groups such as the SPLC and ADL.
