## Summary

The Federal Bureau of Investigation confirmed an active criminal investigation into Hunter Biden for money laundering involving Ukraine, China, and Kazakhstan. The Supreme Court of the United States allowed North Carolina to extend its absentee ballot receipt deadline, a decision dissented by Justices Clarence Thomas, Samuel Alito, and Neil Gorsuch. Nicholas J. Fuentes criticized Madison Cawthorn's policy priorities and characterized Donald Trump's political identity by opposition to globalism and support for border security.
