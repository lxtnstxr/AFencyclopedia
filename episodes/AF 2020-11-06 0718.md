## Summary

Donald Trump won the 2020 presidential election, though vote counts remained ongoing in Arizona and Pennsylvania as of November 6th. A software error in Antrim County, Michigan, initially allocated 6,000 votes from Donald Trump to Joe Biden, and that software was deployed in over 40 Michigan counties and several other states. Nicholas J. Fuentes asserted Donald Trump won the election and accused major media outlets and social media platforms of censoring claims of fraud, while organizing protests at state capitols for November 7th, 2020. Justice Alito ordered Pennsylvania to segregate mail-in ballots received after Election Day, and the U.S. Supreme Court deadlocked 4-4 on blocking a Pennsylvania Supreme Court decision that had extended the mail-in ballot receipt deadline.
