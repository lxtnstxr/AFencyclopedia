## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes created a comprehensive election analysis resource for the 2018 midterms, planning to cover numerous congressional, Senate, and gubernatorial races. He reported on diplomatic developments involving North Korea, including Mike Pompeo's meeting with Kim Jong-un and North Korea's commitment to denuclearization. Fuentes and guest Beardly Beardson advocated for removing women from video games through a movement called "GamerGate HD." Fuentes also stated the Democratic National Committee sued the Trump campaign and Russia, to which Donald Trump suggested a counter-suit.
