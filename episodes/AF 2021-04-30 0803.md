## Summary

The Florida State House passed a bill imposing daily fines on social media companies that ban statewide political candidates. The Barack Obama administration implemented a fair housing rule requiring predominantly white suburbs to diversify, which the Donald Trump administration later withdrew. The Joe Biden administration drafted a new rule to reintroduce subsidized housing into suburbs and proposed infrastructure grants tied to zoning changes for multifamily units. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued these housing policies would destroy suburban property values and that increased immigration under Biden exacerbated a housing crisis.
