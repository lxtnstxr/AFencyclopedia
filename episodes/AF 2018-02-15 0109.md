## Summary

Nikolas Cruz killed 17 people and injured approximately 50 others in a shooting at a Florida school after the FBI failed to identify him from a tip about a threatening YouTube comment. The United States Senate saw all four immigration proposals fail, including a bipartisan plan offering a pathway to citizenship for 1.8 million DACA recipients in exchange for border wall funding. Donald Trump proposed amnesty for 1.8 million individuals in exchange for ending the diversity visa lottery and building a border wall, while also addressing the Florida shooting as a mental health issue.
