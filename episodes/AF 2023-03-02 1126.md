## Summary

A House committee advanced a bill granting President Joe Biden authority to ban TikTok in the United States. Texas State Representative Brian Slayton proposed a bill granting property tax cuts to married, never-divorced heterosexual couples with four or more children. Canada and the European Union have banned TikTok on government-issued devices, a policy also enacted by the White House for federal agencies. Nicholas J. Fuentes participated in interviews with Pearl and Alex Jones, and advocates for mandatory E-Verify to prevent employment of illegal immigrants.
