## Summary

A 2019 photograph of Baraboo High School students giving a Roman salute prompted investigations and responses from the Anti-Defamation League and the Southern Poverty Law Center. Following the 2018 Florida elections, Rick Scott and Ron DeSantis won Senate and gubernatorial races, respectively, amid vote-counting discrepancies in Broward County that drew lawsuits and monitors like Roger Stone. Donald Trump promoted the RAISE Act to reduce legal immigration, while his administration considered replacing officials like John Kelly with candidates including Nick Ayers or Chris Kobach. Nicholas J. Fuentes planned to attend the 2019 Conservative Political Action Conference with supporters to confront journalists and opponents.
