## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes was permanently banned from YouTube on February 14, 2020, following community guideline strikes. Fuentes appeared on InfoWars with Alex Jones that same day and argued his ban resulted from pressure related to the "Groyper Wars" confrontation with Turning Point USA. He announced the America First Political Action Conference (AFPAC) for February 28, 2020, during CPAC, with Michelle Malkin as the keynote speaker. Fuentes identified historical figures like Patrick Buchanan and Sam Francis as predecessors in his conflict with the conservative establishment.
