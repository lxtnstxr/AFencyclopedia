## Summary

The Robert Mueller investigation found no evidence of collusion between Donald Trump's campaign and Russia, though it secured indictments for unrelated offenses. A priest was stabbed at St. Joseph's Oratory in Montreal, and over 300 people were killed in attacks on Christian villages in Nigeria's Kaduna State. Andrew Yang proposed a Universal Basic Income of $5,000 for every American and later debated Ben Shapiro.
