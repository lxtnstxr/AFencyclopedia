## Summary

The episode discussed a reportedly contentious phone call between U.S. President Joe Biden and Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, in which Biden warned that Israel was losing international support due to its military campaign in Gaza, while Netanyahu defended Israel's actions by referencing historical U.S. wartime tactics. It also covered the subsequent pressure on the presidents of Harvard University, the University of Pennsylvania, and MIT over their responses to campus antisemitism, which led to leadership changes at Penn and a review of plagiarism allegations against Harvard's president. Further commentary criticized U.S. foreign policy regarding Israel, Gaza, and alliances with nations like Saudi Arabia, while also dismissing U.S. State Department claims about China's treatment of Uighurs.
