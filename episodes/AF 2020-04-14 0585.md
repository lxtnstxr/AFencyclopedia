## Summary

Gavin Newsom led a West Coast bloc with Oregon and Washington to coordinate reopening their economies, while Andrew Cuomo led an East Coast bloc. Donald Trump threatened to halt funding to the World Health Organization and initiated a review of its pandemic response. The United States recorded a single-day coronavirus death toll of approximately 2,400, contributing to a total of over 26,000 deaths. A Harvard study concluded social distancing measures would likely be necessary until 2022.
