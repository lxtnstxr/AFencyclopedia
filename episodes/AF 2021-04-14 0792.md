## Summary

The Johnson & Johnson COVID-19 vaccine was paused in the United States after reports of severe blood clots, with health authorities framing the risk as one in a million. Former Trump administration officials Brooke Rollins, Linda McMahon, and Larry Kudlow founded the America First Policy Institute, a think tank with a $20 million first-year budget and stated priorities of jobs, opportunity, security, freedom, and innovation. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued the institute's policy priorities were indistinguishable from traditional Republican agendas and criticized figures like Catalina Lauf for co-opting the America First message.
