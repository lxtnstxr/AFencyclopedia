## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes was banned from attending the Conservative Political Action Conference after registering, an exclusion he attributed to pressure from figures like Cassie Dillon and media coverage from outlets like Huffington Post. Fuentes obtained credentials under a different name and attended CPAC events before being identified by Alex Sears and escorted from the exhibition hall.
