## Summary

Donald Trump accused Google, Facebook, and Twitter of censoring conservative content and rigging search results. Facebook, YouTube, and Spotify coordinated to remove Alex Jones and InfoWars from their platforms. A United States Senate hearing scheduled for September 5th will question executives from Twitter and Facebook regarding allegations of shadow banning.
