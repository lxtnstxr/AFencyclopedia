## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes cited a Fox News report stating the coronavirus originated in a Wuhan government laboratory as part of a virology effort rivaling the United States. Fuentes claimed China suppressed data, destroyed samples, and allowed international travel from Wuhan to spread the virus globally. He also accused the World Health Organization of complicity in covering up the virus's origins. Fuentes announced California Governor Gavin Newsom created a $125 million fund, with donations from groups like the Chan Zuckerberg Initiative, to provide cash payments to undocumented immigrants.
