## Summary

Israel barred Representatives Rashida Tlaib and Ilhan Omar from visiting due to their support for the Boycott, Divestment, and Sanctions movement, though it later granted Tlaib a humanitarian exemption to visit her grandmother in the West Bank, which she declined. An inverted yield curve between Treasury bonds in 2019 signaled a potential recession, coinciding with an 800-point drop in the Dow Jones Industrial Average. Nicholas J. Fuentes characterized PragerU, The Daily Wire, and Breitbart as media outlets with a strong pro-Israel editorial stance.
