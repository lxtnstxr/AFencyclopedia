## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes argued that widespread mail-in ballot fraud by the Democratic Party and Biden campaign was stealing Donald Trump's rightful victory. Joe Biden gained leads in Wisconsin and Michigan after large batches of mail-in ballots were released from Milwaukee and Detroit. Donald Trump's campaign filed lawsuits in Georgia, Wisconsin, Michigan, and Pennsylvania to challenge vote counting procedures and results.
