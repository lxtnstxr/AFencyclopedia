## Summary

The Ukrainian Armed Forces damaged the bridge connecting Russia to Crimea through a suicide bombing attack. Vladimir Putin characterized the attack as a terrorist act and Russia subsequently launched drone and missile strikes against Ukrainian infrastructure. PayPal announced then rescinded a policy to fine users for misinformation after criticism from former president David Marcus and Elon Musk. Getter, a social media platform founded by former Trump communications director Jason Miller, bans the term "Groyper" and classifies it as hate speech.
