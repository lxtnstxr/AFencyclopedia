## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes celebrated the 100th episode of *America First* and announced its one-year anniversary for February 6th. The Obama administration's Justice Department obtained a FISA warrant to surveil Trump campaign advisor Carter Page in October 2016, relying heavily on the Steele dossier funded by the Democratic National Committee and the Hillary Clinton campaign. The Senate Judiciary Committee, led by Chuck Grassley and Lindsey Graham, referred dossier author Christopher Steele for criminal charges for making false statements. The United States economy added 200,000 jobs in January, with wages growing at their fastest pace in eight years.
