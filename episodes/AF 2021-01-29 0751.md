## Summary

The United States House of Representatives considered censure or expulsion of Representative Marjorie Taylor Greene over past social media posts. The Republican Jewish Coalition and the Conference of Presidents of Major American Jewish Organizations condemned Greene's rhetoric, with the coalition stating it was working with House Republican leadership on next steps. House Democrats Jimmy Gomez and Debbie Wasserman Schultz introduced resolutions to expel Greene and strip her committee assignments, respectively. Nicholas J. Fuentes claimed a coordinated effort by media and Jewish organizations sought to thwart Greene and right-wing populism, while the Department of Homeland Security issued a bulletin warning of a threat from domestic violent extremists.
