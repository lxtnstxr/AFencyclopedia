## Summary

The Supreme Court ruled eastern Oklahoma is a Muscogee (Creek) Nation reservation, overturning state convictions of Jimcy McGirt and Patrick Murphy. Justice Neil Gorsuch authored the majority opinion, joined by the Court's liberal justices, while Chief Justice John Roberts led the dissent. New York City Mayor Bill de Blasio authorized a "Black Lives Matter" mural on Fifth Avenue directly in front of Trump Tower. The Trump administration, through Secretary of State Mike Pompeo, considered a national ban on TikTok over data security concerns linked to China.
