## Summary

Elizabeth Warren proposed an extensive housing policy to address the Section 8 backlog and create incentives for affordable construction. Michael Bloomberg cited his record of creating 175,000 units of affordable housing in New York City and argued for bipartisan cooperation. Joe Biden outlined a plan to address racial inequities with a tax credit for first-time homebuyers and doubled funding for young entrepreneurs. Bernie Sanders pledged to legalize marijuana in every state on his first day in office and expunge all possession records.
