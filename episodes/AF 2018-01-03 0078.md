## Summary

Michael Wolff's book *Fire and Fury* contained excerpts quoting Steve Bannon describing contacts with Russia as treasonous, prompting a public disavowal from Donald Trump. Paul Manafort filed lawsuits against Special Counsel Robert Mueller, who was appointed by Rod Rosenstein to lead the Russia probe originating from a dossier funded by the DNC and Hillary Clinton's campaign. The House Intelligence Committee, led by Devin Nunes, threatened contempt charges against Mueller and Rosenstein for obstructing oversight of the investigation.
