## Summary

Darrell Brooks drove an SUV into a Christmas parade crowd in Waukesha, Wisconsin, killing at least five people. A jury acquitted Kyle Rittenhouse of all charges for shootings during Kenosha unrest, after which he gave an interview to Tucker Carlson. Nicholas J. Fuentes organized anti-vaccine mandate protests in New York City and argued that banks pressured payment processors to deny him service.
