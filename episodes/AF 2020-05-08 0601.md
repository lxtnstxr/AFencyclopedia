## Summary

Donald Trump imposed a 60-day ban on approximately 26,000 green cards per month in April 2020. The United States economy lost 20.5 million jobs that same month, raising the unemployment rate to 14.7%. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued the recession would be severe and that nationalists could gain from the crisis, while a coalition of GOP lawmakers urged Trump to expand the immigration ban to temporary work visas for up to 36 months.
