## Summary

The United States Senate failed to pass a proposed January 6th Commission modeled after the 9-11 Commission. A post-mortem examination found United States Capitol Police officer Brian Sicknick died of natural causes, leading the New York Times to retract its initial reporting. Paul Ryan warned the Republican Party would not survive if it remained the party of Donald Trump, who subsequently called Ryan a "curse" to the party. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued the conflict between figures like Ryan and Trump is a false dichotomy, criticizing Republican congressional leaders like Kevin McCarthy and Mitch McConnell.
