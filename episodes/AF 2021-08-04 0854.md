## Summary

New York City announced a "Key to NYC Pass" mandate requiring proof of vaccination for entry into public venues, with a voluntary phase beginning in August 2021. The Supreme Court indicated the CDC's eviction moratorium lacked clear legal authority from Congress, though it allowed the ban to remain temporarily in a 5-4 decision. The Biden administration reversed its position and extended the moratorium for 60 days despite publicly acknowledging the action likely lacked legal standing. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued that figures like Charlie Kirk, Steve Bannon, and Tucker Carlson promote a bankrupt ideology of economic nationalism that refuses to address racial identity.
