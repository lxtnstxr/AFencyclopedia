## Summary

Ilhan Omar made comments about Israel and AIPAC, prompting responses from AIPAC and the Anti-Defamation League. Nancy Pelosi drafted a House resolution rejecting anti-Semitism, which cited a 37% increase in anti-Jewish hate crimes and referenced the Pittsburgh synagogue shooting. Andrew Yang's presidential campaign platform included universal basic income and making Puerto Rico a state.
