## Summary

The New York Times hired columnist Sarah Jeong despite her history of posting thousands of anti-white tweets. Nicholas J. Fuentes contrasted media defenses of Jeong with the firings of individuals like Roseanne Barr and Jason Richwine. Fuentes also debated Will Chamberlain on Israel and argued that figures like Ben Shapiro fail to recognize asymmetric power dynamics in media.
