## Summary

PewDiePie rescinded a planned $50,000 donation to the Anti-Defamation League following fan backlash. A Charlottesville circuit judge ruled statues of Robert E. Lee and Stonewall Jackson must remain standing under a state law. The FBI concluded Israel was likely responsible for placing surveillance devices near the White House, but the Trump administration took no punitive action. Israel receives $3.8 billion in annual U.S. military aid and can spend a quarter domestically.

(01:27:34) [[PewDiePie]] planned to donate $50,000 to the [[Anti-Defamation League|ADL]] as part of his celebration for surpassing 100 million YouTube subscribers, a decision he later explained was an attempt to move past controversies like the Christchurch attack.

(01:35:68) [[PewDiePie]] rescinded his $50,000 donation to the [[Anti-Defamation League|ADL]] after facing backlash from his fan base and stating he did not fully understand the organization's activities when he made the initial pledge.
