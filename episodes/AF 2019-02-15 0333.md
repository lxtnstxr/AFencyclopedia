## Summary

Donald Trump signed a government funding bill that averted a shutdown and concurrently declared a national emergency to seek additional border wall funds. The funding bill allocated $1.375 billion for pedestrian fencing in the Rio Grande Valley and included $3.1 billion in aid for Israel. Jussie Smollett, an actor, reported being attacked in Chicago by assailants who used racial slurs and a rope, but police found no supporting surveillance footage and arrested two Nigerian men who had worked with him.
