## Summary

A Virginia bill proposed allowing abortion up until the due date, which Governor Ralph Northam commented could involve post-delivery discussions on resuscitating infants. The United States Senate passed a resolution rebuking President Trump's proposed troop withdrawals from Syria and Afghanistan, with Marco Rubio supporting it and Rand Paul criticizing its timeline. Elizabeth Warren, Bernie Sanders, and Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez proposed progressive economic measures including a wealth tax, a high estate tax, and a 70% marginal income tax. Nicholas J. Fuentes characterized the Democratic platform and debated economic policy, while figures like George Soros and Jeff Bezos were cited as examples within critiques of the economic system.
