## Summary

The New York Times planned to publish Tucker Carlson's home address, leading to Carlson naming the involved journalists, Murray Carpenter and Tristan Spinski. Netflix canceled its Turkish drama "If Only" after Turkish authorities refused a license because the show included a gay character. Russia fined LGBT activist Yulia Tsvetkova for spreading gay propaganda among minors, and Turkey converted the Hagia Sophia from a museum back into a mosque. Twitter banned all QAnon-related content and accounts, while Reddit banned the subreddit "r/the_Donald," and PayPal listed "white advocacy" as an infraction for banning accounts.
