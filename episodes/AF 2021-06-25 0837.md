## Summary

Anna Morgan Lloyd, a grandmother from Indiana, pleaded guilty to a single misdemeanor for her brief entry into the U.S. Capitol on January 6th and was sentenced to probation and a fine by Judge Royce Lamberth. A RAND Corporation and University of Southern California study concluded COVID-19 shelter-in-place policies did not reduce excess mortality. Nicholas J. Fuentes released a "White Boy Summer" playlist on Spotify and argued that critical race theory has become a state ideology in the United States. Facebook reversed its policy to allow discussion of the COVID-19 lab leak theory, which the Wall Street Journal had reported on after initial suppression.
