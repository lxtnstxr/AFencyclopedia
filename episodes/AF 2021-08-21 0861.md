## Summary

The Federal Bureau of Investigation found no evidence of a conspiracy by the White House or rally organizers regarding the January 6th breach of the United States Capitol. The Taliban assumed control of Kabul after the United States completed its military withdrawal from Afghanistan. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued major media outlets produced critical coverage of the withdrawal to manufacture consent for a renewed occupation.
