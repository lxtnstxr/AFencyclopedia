## Summary

JP Morgan Chase debanked Kanye West's Yeezy LLC in November 2022. A Connecticut court ordered Alex Jones to pay $965 million in damages to Sandy Hook families in a defamation case. Nicholas J. Fuentes was banned from YouTube and DLive for his activism and content related to the January 6th Capitol event. Fuentes argued that financial and media institutions coordinated to cancel figures who publicly discussed Jewish people and power structures.
