## Summary

Joe Biden won the South Carolina Democratic primary by a 30% margin and ten of fourteen states on Super Tuesday, becoming the frontrunner. Michael Bloomberg withdrew from the Democratic race and endorsed Biden, consolidating the moderate lane against Bernie Sanders. Nicholas J. Fuentes organized the inaugural AFPAC conference in Washington DC, where keynote speaker Michelle Malkin faced criticism from figures like Robert Spencer and Amanda Carpenter. Fuentes' show was banned from YouTube, leading him to launch a proprietary streaming service with a subscription paywall.
