## Summary

The House Judiciary Committee held a hearing on white nationalism featuring testimony from Candace Owens, the Anti-Defamation League, and civil rights activists. Donald Trump designated Iran's Islamic Revolutionary Guard Corps as a Foreign Terrorist Organization, a move Benjamin Netanyahu publicly thanked him for and claimed was at his personal request. Nicholas J. Fuentes was banned from Twitch for comments on interracial relationships, while alleging Stephen Bonnell orchestrated the ban.
