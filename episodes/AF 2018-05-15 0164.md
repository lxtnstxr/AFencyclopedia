## Summary

Vladimir Putin announced Russia's first annual military spending reduction since 1998, dropping it to the world's third-largest spender. Donald Trump's administration pursued a "maximum pressure" strategy against Iran and North Korea, withdrawing from the Iran nuclear deal and sanctioning companies like Boeing. North Korea canceled a meeting with South Korea and threatened to cancel a summit with the United States over joint military drills. Primary elections were held in Oregon, Idaho, Pennsylvania, and Nebraska, with turnout trends showing significant increases for both major parties.
