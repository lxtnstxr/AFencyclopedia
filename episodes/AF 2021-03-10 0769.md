## Summary

An Ipsos poll showed American belief that George Floyd was murdered fell from over 60% to 36%, while trust in Black Lives Matter declined and trust in local police increased. Texas Governor Greg Abbott labeled the free speech platform Gab anti-Semitic and proposed legislation targeting it, appearing in a video with state legislators Phil King and Goldman. Andrew Torba built Gab as an uncensorable alternative to Twitter, which handled traffic following Donald Trump's ban from that platform.
