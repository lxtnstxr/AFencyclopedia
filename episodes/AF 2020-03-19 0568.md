## Summary

Senate Republicans unveiled a coronavirus economic relief proposal featuring direct cash payments to individuals and couples below specific income thresholds. Italy surpassed China in official confirmed coronavirus deaths, reporting 41,000 cases to China's 80,000. Senator Richard Burr sold substantial stock holdings in late February after receiving confidential briefings on the pandemic. Tulsi Gabbard ended her Democratic presidential primary campaign and endorsed Joe Biden, whose past foreign policy votes included support for the Iraq War.
