## Summary

BG Cumbie, a 20-year-old Protestant from Phoenix, Arizona, attends the University of Mississippi and previously appeared on Fox News' Jesse Watters show. Nicholas J. Fuentes cited Milton Friedman's *Free to Choose* and Ian Fletcher's *Free Trade Doesn't Work*, identifying his primary economic concerns as free trade and mass immigration.
