A study by the Aspen Institute and the COVID-19 Eviction Defense Project found over 6.5 million U.S. households were behind on rental payments. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued the expiring policy would politically damage Joe Biden and the Democratic Party while creating chaos beneficial to the America First movement.
