## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes delivered a campus speech at Iowa State University on March 6, 2019, an event organized by the Iowa State College Republicans. The Iowa State College Republicans disavowed Fuentes the following day, claiming they were misled about his association with Turning Point USA. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued the Republican Party's potential extinction was linked to its disavowal of his views, and he later contrasted his peaceful event with the violent, costly appearance by Richard Spencer at the University of Florida. Fuentes also stated that Andrew Yang's presidential campaign positively introduced Universal Basic Income into mainstream Democratic debate, while he described the United States' relationship with Israel as parasitic, citing annual foreign aid and military engagements.
