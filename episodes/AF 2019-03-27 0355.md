## Summary

Facebook and Instagram announced a policy to ban white nationalist and white separatist content following the Christchurch mosque shootings. U.S. Customs and Border Protection Commissioner Kevin McAleenan stated immigration was at an unprecedented scale, with the El Paso sector holding over 13,400 migrants. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued that platform censorship violates Section 230 and compared the actions of Amazon, Facebook, and Silicon Valley to totalitarianism.
