## Summary

President Donald Trump appointed Neil Gorsuch to the Court after the Republican-controlled Senate obstructed Barack Obama's nomination of Merrick Garland. The Supreme Court upheld the constitutionality of Donald Trump's travel ban and overturned a California pro-abortion law. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued that a conservative Supreme Court could transform the country on issues like abortion and same-sex marriage for decades.
