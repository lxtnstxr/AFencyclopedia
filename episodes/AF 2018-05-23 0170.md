## Summary

A federal judge ruled Donald Trump could not block people on Twitter. The Boy Scouts of America changed its name to Scouts BSA, began allowing girls and LGBTQ individuals to participate, and announced condom availability for its 2019 World Scout Jamboree, after which the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints ended its affiliation. The NFL unanimously approved a policy requiring players to stand during the national anthem, following a UBS Securities poll linking anthem protests to declining viewership. Tomi Lahren was harassed and had water thrown on her in a Minneapolis restaurant, an incident Donald Trump and publications like The Root commented on.
