## Summary

The episode focused on the escalating legal and political confrontation between Texas and the Biden administration over border enforcement, centering on Texas's seizure of Shelby Park in Eagle Pass and its defiance of a Supreme Court order and a federal deadline. It also discussed the potential economic repercussions of the dispute, including new restrictions on liquefied natural gas exports, and noted statements of support for Texas from Republican figures including Nikki Haley and Ron DeSantis. Additional topics included the economic impact of sanctions on Russia and commentary on various domestic political figures and media.
