## Summary

Joe Arpaio's primary election results in Arizona remained unknown the morning after the vote, while Chris Kobach lost the Republican Senate primary in Kansas. Cori Bush, a Ferguson activist, won her Democratic primary in Missouri, and the Seattle City Council moved to abolish the Seattle Police Department. Joe Biden's mental acuity was publicly questioned by a black reporter during an interview where Biden asked if the reporter had been tested for cocaine.
