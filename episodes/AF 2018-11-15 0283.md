## Summary

Michael Avenatti denied committing domestic violence following his release on bail for assault. The Wall Street Journal reported the U.S. Justice Department was preparing to prosecute WikiLeaks founder Julian Assange. Broward County, Florida election officials completed a U.S. Senate race recount two minutes after the state deadline, invalidating results where Rick Scott gained nearly 800 votes on Bill Nelson. Donald Trump signed the Prison Reform and Redemption Act, which a Center for Immigration Studies analysis argued would make deporting criminal foreigners more difficult.
