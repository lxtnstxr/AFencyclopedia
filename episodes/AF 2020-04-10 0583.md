## Summary

Anthony Fauci and officials in Europe and the United States proposed an immunity passport system based on coronavirus antibody tests. Apple and Google announced a collaboration to build contact-tracing software directly into smartphone operating systems. Statistical models projected a need for 140,000 hospital beds in New York, but actual hospitalizations peaked at 18,569. Nicholas J. Fuentes criticized the pandemic response as a pretext for expanded corporate and government control, specifically citing Bill Gates and the World Health Organization.
