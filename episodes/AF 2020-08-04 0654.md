## Summary

Donald Trump reduced U.S. troop levels in Afghanistan to approximately 8,000, with a planned withdrawal from Germany of 12,000 troops. Viacom terminated its relationship with Nick Cannon following anti-semitic comments he made, after which Diddy offered him a platform. Nick Cannon wrote a book report on Bari Weiss's "How to Fight Anti-Semitism" and sought a dialogue with her following the controversy.
