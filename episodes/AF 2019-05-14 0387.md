## Summary

Joe Bernstein's BuzzFeed article resulted in YouTube issuing community guideline strikes against the YouTuber "Soph." Stephen Anderson was banned from entering Ireland under a 1999 law, having previously been barred from South Africa, Jamaica, Botswana, and the United Kingdom. Nicholas J. Fuentes identified Stefan Molyneux as a foundational influence and praised Tulsi Gabbard for her foreign policy criticisms despite his general reluctance to support a female presidential candidate.
