## Summary

Over 600 American doctors warned Donald Trump that economic shutdowns could cause a mass casualty event by disrupting routine medical care. Mike Pompeo traveled to Israel to demand it halt a Chinese-built desalination plant, a request to which Israel later agreed. Nicholas J. Fuentes detailed a history of Israeli technology transfers to China and argued the U.S.-Israel relationship lacks reciprocity. China became Israel's third-largest trading partner, with bilateral trade growing 402% in a decade to approximately $14 billion in 2018.
