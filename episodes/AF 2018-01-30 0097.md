## Summary

Donald Trump delivered his first State of the Union Address on January 30, 2018, promoting economic achievements and a four-pillar immigration framework. The proposal offered a pathway to citizenship for 1.8 million people in exchange for border security funding, an end to chain migration, and an end to the diversity visa lottery. Democratic Party members, including Chuck Schumer, rejected the framework and refused to applaud during that portion of the speech.
