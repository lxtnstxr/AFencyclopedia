## Summary

A major fire severely damaged the Notre Dame Cathedral in Paris in 2019, collapsing its roof and spire. Nicholas J. Fuentes criticized the characterization of Notre Dame as part of a Judeo-Christian heritage and contrasted reactions to its fire with support for removing Confederate monuments. Fuentes also began streaming on DLive and identified figures like PewDiePie and Elon Musk as prototypes for cultural influence.
