## Summary

Iran experienced widespread civil unrest in 2017, described as its worst since the 2009 Green Movement protests against President Mahmoud Ahmadinejad's re-election. Saudi Arabia, under Crown Prince Mohammed bin Salman, imposed a blockade on Qatar and accused Iran of acts of war over Houthi missile attacks. The administration of Donald Trump recognized Jerusalem as Israel's capital, ordered missile strikes on Syria, and refused to certify Iran's compliance with the nuclear deal.
