## Summary

The United States, Canada, and several other nations recognized Juan Guaidó as Venezuela's sovereign in January 2019. The United States imposed heavy oil sanctions on Venezuela and reduced its annual refugee admission ceiling to 30,000 under the administration of Donald Trump. Russia conducted joint military drills with Venezuela in 2018, sending nuclear-capable bombers. Following the Christchurch shooting, Facebook and Instagram announced bans on "white nationalist" and "white separatist" content.
