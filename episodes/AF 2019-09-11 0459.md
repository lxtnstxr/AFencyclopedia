## Summary

Donald Trump announced a Food and Drug Administration policy requiring flavored e-cigarette companies to remove their products from the market. Republicans Dan Bishop and Greg Murphy won special U.S. House elections in North Carolina's 9th and 3rd congressional districts. The Supreme Court allowed a new asylum rule to take effect, preventing most migrants who travel through Mexico from seeking asylum in the United States.
