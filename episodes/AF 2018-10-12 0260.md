## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes hosts the daily call-in program *America First*. Fuentes identifies as a paleoconservative advocating for a return to an older American constitutional order centered on Anglo-Protestant culture. He argued that fascism failed to gain traction in America due to the country's intrinsic culture of individualism and limited government. Fuentes attributed a decline in American Catholic religiosity and liberal views to the reforms of Vatican II.
