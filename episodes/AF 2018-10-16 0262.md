## Summary

Donald Trump posted tweets attacking Elizabeth Warren and Stormy Daniels and announced an investigation into San Juan's mayor. Stephen Miller received authorization from Trump to use executive powers for an immigration crackdown ahead of the midterms. A federal judge dismissed Stormy Daniels' defamation lawsuit against Trump and ordered her to pay his legal fees. Trump later threatened to cut off all U.S. aid to Honduras over a migrant caravan.
