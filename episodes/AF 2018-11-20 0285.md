## Summary

Gavin McInnes founded the fraternal organization Proud Boys in 2016, which the FBI later classified as an extremist group. Chipotle fired and then reinstated a manager in St. Paul, Minnesota, after a social media accusation by Masood Ali, who had a documented history of dine-and-dash thefts. The CIA assessed that Saudi Crown Prince Mohammed bin Salman ordered the killing of Washington Post contributor Jamal Khashoggi inside the Saudi consulate in Turkey.
