## Summary

Courtney Clenney was charged with second-degree murder by Miami-Dade State Attorney Kathy Fernandez Rundle after allegedly stabbing Christian Obumseli. Nicholas J. Fuentes participated in a collaborative stream with Destiny and Lauren Southern, whom he described as influential figures in online political commentary. Fuentes reflected on attending the 2017 "Unite the Right" rally in Charlottesville and later declining a job offer from Jared Taylor of American Renaissance. He asserted that his work following the rally transformed the dissident right-wing scene into the "Groyper" movement.
