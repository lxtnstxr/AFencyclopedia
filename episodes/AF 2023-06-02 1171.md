## Summary

Former President Donald Trump, speaking at a town hall in Iowa, criticized the Biden administration's foreign and domestic policies, declaring the present to be the most dangerous time in U.S. history due to nuclear threats from a "new axis of evil" consisting of China, Russia, and Iran. He claimed his administration had prevented such dangers, secured the border, achieved energy dominance, and deterred invasions of Ukraine and Taiwan, while also taking credit for the overturning of Roe v. Wade. Trump dismissed low-polling primary opponents, focused his criticism on Florida Governor Ron DeSantis, and denounced the ongoing investigations into his handling of classified documents as politically motivated election interference.
