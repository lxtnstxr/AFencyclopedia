## Summary

Facebook and Instagram banned right-wing figures including Milo Yiannopoulos, Laura Loomer, Paul Joseph Watson, InfoWars, and Alex Jones. Former White House Chief of Staff John Kelly joined the board of Caliburn International, a company operating shelters for unaccompanied migrant children. Former Defense Secretary James Mattis reportedly declined orders from Donald Trump regarding military families in South Korea. Nicholas J. Fuentes cited media promotion of insects as a future food source and criticized women's suffrage on its 100-year anniversary.
