## Summary

The AstraZeneca COVID-19 vaccine, which lacked FDA approval in the United States, was halted in several European countries and Canada due to reports of severe blood clots. Matt Gaetz faced a Department of Justice investigation for allegedly having a sexual relationship with a 17-year-old girl and paying for her travel, which he claimed was part of an extortion scheme. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued that federal institutions like the FBI and DOJ were weaponized against conservatives, citing past targets including Donald Trump. Fuentes also reported that the European Medicines Agency concluded the AstraZeneca vaccine was safe but could not rule out a link to a rare blood clotting disorder.
