## Summary

Donald Trump held a rally in Texas endorsing Ted Cruz and later declared himself a nationalist in response to a question from Jim Acosta. Barack Obama and Joe Biden held campaign rallies that reportedly drew smaller crowds than Trump's events. Mike Pompeo stated migrant caravans would not successfully enter the United States illegally, and Trump tweeted a threat to reduce foreign aid to source countries. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued the post-Cold War Republican Party required transformation and that a future populist coalition would draw support from outside coastal urban centers.
