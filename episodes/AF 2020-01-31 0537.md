## Summary

The World Health Organization and the United States declared the coronavirus a global and domestic public health emergency, leading to a U.S. travel ban on China and flight suspensions by major American carriers. Nicholas J. Fuentes announced the America First Political Action Conference, with speakers including Michelle Malkin and Scott Greer, to be held in Washington, D.C. concurrently with the Conservative Political Action Conference.
