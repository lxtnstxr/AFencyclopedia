## Summary

North Korea conducted a missile test overseen by Kim Jong-un. The redacted Mueller Report found no criminal conspiracy between Donald Trump's campaign and Russia but did not exonerate him on obstruction of justice. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued that United States foreign policy toward Iran and Israel negatively affected negotiations with North Korea. Fuentes also criticized Charlie Kirk's defense of Zionism and Israel's annexation of the Golan Heights.
