## Summary

A teacher in France was beheaded after instructing students to draw the Prophet Muhammad, prompting the French government to announce the deportation of 151 suspected terrorists. Nicholas J. Fuentes cited a New York Times article by Roger Cohen, who argued Donald Trump's presidency represented a white American stand against demographic replacement. Fuentes urged viewers in swing states to vote for Trump and reported the White House had requested resignation letters from all political appointees for a potential second term.
