## Summary

The Islamic Revolutionary Guard Corps shot down a United States RQ-4A Global Hawk drone over the Strait of Hormuz. Donald Trump withdrew the United States from the Iran nuclear deal in May 2018 and later described the drone incident as a "very big mistake." Nicholas J. Fuentes argued that defense contractors and the Israel lobby view escalating conflict with Iran as beneficial. Roy Moore announced a bid for the United States Senate in Alabama, having previously lost a 2017 special election to Doug Jones.
