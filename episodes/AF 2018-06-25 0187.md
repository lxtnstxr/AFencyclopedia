## Summary

Donald Trump offered protections for DACA recipients in exchange for border wall funding and ending chain migration, later signing an executive order to detain migrant families together. Nicholas J. Fuentes cited Fox News and FAIR reports on crimes by illegal immigrants and referenced studies by Richard Lynn and Tatu Vanhanen on national cognitive ability. Fuentes advocated for a 25-year halt to all immigration, arguing multiracial societies lead to ethnic conflict and that ending welfare would cause voluntary departure.
