## Summary

The episode discussed the 22-year prison sentence for Proud Boys leader Enrique Tarrio in connection with the January 6 Capitol riot, which was the longest sentence issued in the case. It also covered a public dispute involving the Anti-Defamation League, whose CEO Jonathan Greenblatt criticized Elon Musk for engaging with figures described as anti-Semitic, while conservative commentators like Stephen Miller and Glenn Greenwald accused the ADL of partisan activism. The commentary further included claims from Nicholas J. Fuentes that the prosecutions targeted pro-Trump groups and referenced a brief nationwide grounding of United Airlines flights due to an equipment failure.
