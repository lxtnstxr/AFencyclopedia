## Summary

A potential immigration deal framework in the United States Congress proposed allocating between $1.3 billion and $2 billion for border security, far less than the 2018 military spending bill of $716 billion. Candace Owens of Turning Point USA remarked in London that if Adolf Hitler only wanted to make Germany great, that would have been acceptable, omitting the Holocaust. Virginia Governor Ralph Northam's advisers instructed him to read works by Alex Haley and Ta-Nehisi Coates following his blackface scandal, while Lieutenant Governor Justin Fairfax faced a second rape allegation from Meredith Watson.
