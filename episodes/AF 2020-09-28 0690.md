## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes announced coverage of the first 2020 presidential debate between Donald Trump and Joe Biden scheduled for September 29. Fuentes cited a Project Veritas report alleging a ballot harvesting operation in Minnesota linked to Ilhan Omar's brother, Liban Mohamed, and city council member Jamal Osman. He referenced a separate National File report from Texas alleging a scheme involving Joe Biden's Texas political director, Dallas Jones. Fuentes argued the 2020 election would involve legal battles over mail-in ballots and suggested Republican-controlled state legislatures might appoint their own electors.
