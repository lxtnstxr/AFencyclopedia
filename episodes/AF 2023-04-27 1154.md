## Summary

The Walt Disney Company sued Florida Governor Ron DeSantis and the state government, alleging retaliation for Disney's opposition to the Parental Rights in Education Act. Fulton County District Attorney Fani Willis announced a timeline for potential charging decisions regarding Donald Trump's efforts to overturn Georgia's 2020 election results. Nicholas J. Fuentes characterized the conflict between DeSantis and Disney as a question of corporate versus government control over societal norms.
