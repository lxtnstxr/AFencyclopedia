## Summary

Donald Trump proposed an immigration plan replacing the Diversity Visa Lottery and family-based chain migration with a merit-based "Build America Visa" system. Jared Kushner helped craft the proposal with groups like the Center for Immigration Studies and the American Enterprise Institute. Chuck Schumer and Nancy Pelosi opposed the plan, labeling it a fringe extremist agenda. The state of Alabama passed a law imposing a 100-year prison sentence on doctors performing abortions except to save the mother's life.
