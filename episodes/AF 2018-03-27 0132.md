## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes launched the foreign affairs podcast "America First World Report" with an episode on the civil war in Yemen. The United States Commerce Department announced a citizenship question for the 2020 census, prompting a lawsuit from the state of California. Donald Trump explored alternative funding for a border wall with Mexico, including suggesting the Department of Defense reallocate funds. The Republican Party used the budget reconciliation rule to pass tax cuts in 2017 after an unsuccessful attempt to repeal the Affordable Care Act, which was opposed by Senator John McCain.
