## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes hosted guests including Vince from The Red Elephants and caller Sharia LaBeouf, who discussed Twitter suspensions of accounts like Spicki and Gail. Fuentes cited the 1965 Hart-Celler Act as the origin of modern mass immigration to the United States, naming the Anti-Defamation League, George W. Bush, and corporate interests as proponents. Fuentes also stated a global Catholic theocracy would be preferable to the current state of the United States, citing Joshua Charles's book *Liberty Secrets*.
