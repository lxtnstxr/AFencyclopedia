## Summary

Minneapolis awarded a $27 million settlement to the family of George Floyd. Nicholas J. Fuentes was banned from several social media platforms but maintained a verified Twitter account in March 2021, which he used to promote his America First Political Action Conference. A Twitter spokesperson stated Fuentes' account was not in violation of platform rules as of March 12, 2021.
