## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes described his show *America First* as an esoteric philosophy and a guiding light for alienated young men, while explicitly stating it cares about white people. He expressed admiration for commentators Steve Sailer and Darren Beattie, whom he called one of the great minds of his time.
