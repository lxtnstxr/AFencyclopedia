## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes argued that Oswald Mosley was a greater orator than several modern Western leaders and that contemporary nationalist movements lack inspirational rhetoric. U.S.-backed Syrian forces took control of Raqqa, the former capital of the Islamic State in Syria, which Nicholas J. Fuentes claimed was a major victory for the Donald Trump presidency.
