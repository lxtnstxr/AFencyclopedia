## Summary

Hope Hicks resigned from the Trump administration after months of public speculation. Donald Trump and Jeff Sessions engaged in public sparring regarding their working relationship on Twitter. Nicholas J. Fuentes challenged Ben Shapiro to a debate, accusing him of posting videos about him.

Ahead of Italian parliamentary elections, Matteo Renzi's Democratic Party polled at 27%, while the Five Star Movement polled at 28% and the right-wing coalition above 30%. The right-wing coalition included Silvio Berlusconi's Forza Italia, Matteo Salvini's Northern League, and the Brothers of Italy party. Italy experienced the arrival of over 600,000 migrants between 2013 and 2018, impacting a country with a high debt-to-GDP ratio and 11% youth unemployment.

Ben Shapiro argued Israel is a "creedal nation" based on the Jewish religion, while Orthodox Jewish law defines a person as Jewish only with a Jewish mother. The Israeli Parliament discussed implementing DNA tests to verify Jewish ancestry for individuals claiming the right of return. Pat Buchanan authored an article arguing that nationalism is ascendant globally, citing the strength of ethnic nationalism in China and Russia.
