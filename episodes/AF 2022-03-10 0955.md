## Summary

Russia discovered U.S.-linked biological laboratories studying pathogens like Ebola near the Ukrainian border during its invasion. The American Conservative Union accepted a six-figure donation from the New Venture Fund, a group connected to George Soros, in 2020. Nicholas J.
