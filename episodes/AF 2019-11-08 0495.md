## Summary

Ben Shapiro dedicated a 45-minute speech at Stanford University to addressing the movement led by Nicholas J. Fuentes. Supporters of Nicholas J. Fuentes attended a Turning Point USA event featuring Dan Crenshaw at Arizona State University, where they chanted and were segregated during questioning. Nicholas J. Fuentes outlined a campaign against Turning Point USA, citing the dissolution of its Fresno State chapter and predicting a difficult year for the organization.
