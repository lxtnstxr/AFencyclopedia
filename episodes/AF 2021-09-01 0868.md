## Summary

An FBI report found hate crimes in the United States reached a 12-year high in 2020, with over 10,000 incidents and a surge in crimes targeting Asian and black Americans. Joe Biden defended the U.S. troop withdrawal from Afghanistan, a policy Donald Trump had committed to in February 2020. Fox News commentator Chris Wallace stated a terror attack on U.S. soil originating from Afghanistan would end the Biden administration. Nicholas J. Fuentes expressed admiration for the Taliban and listed them alongside Russia, China, and Iran as sources of hope.
