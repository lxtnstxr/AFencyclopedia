## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes launched social media campaigns using the hashtags #PapaJohnDidNothingWrong and #BringBackPapa to support John Schnatter after his resignation from Papa John's. Disney fired James Gunn as director of Guardians of the Galaxy 3 following Mike Cernovich's exposure of Gunn's old tweets, though Ben Shapiro and Reason.com defended the posts as jokes. President Donald Trump terminated U.S. participation in the Joint Comprehensive Plan of Action and imposed sanctions on Iran after exchanging threats with President Hassan Rouhani.

Fuentes]] announced a social media campaign using the hashtag #PapaJohnDidNothingWrong to support [[John Schnatter|Papa John]] after he was forced to resign as chairman of [[Papa John's]] over an unsubstantiated rumor about his use of a racial slur during a private conference call.

(00:35:76) [[John Schnatter|Papa John]] resigned as chairman of [[Papa John's]], apologized, and was removed from the company's pizza boxes and advertisements following the controversy over his alleged use of a racial slur.
