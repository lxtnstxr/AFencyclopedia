## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes was banned from PayPal, Venmo, and Shopify, disrupting his commercial operations. Fuentes debated Jacob Wall on foreign policy at a Demand Free Speech event in Miami, which faced multiple venue cancellations. Ashley St. Clair was fired from Turning Point USA after being photographed with Nicholas J. Fuentes, prompting the organization to denounce white nationalism. Several commentators from The Washington Examiner criticized St. Clair and Cathy Zhu for their associations with Fuentes.
