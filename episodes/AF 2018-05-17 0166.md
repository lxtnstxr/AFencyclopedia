## Summary

Inspector General Michael Horowitz completed a draft report on the FBI and DOJ's handling of the Hillary Clinton email probe. A New York Times article revealed the FBI investigated Trump campaign aides Michael T. Flynn, Paul Manafort, Carter Page, and George Papadopoulos using surveillance methods. North Korea canceled a high-level summit with South Korea and expressed doubts about a planned meeting with Donald Trump, who later assured a deal would not follow the "Libyan model" of denuclearization. Rudy Giuliani stated the Mueller probe confirmed it could not indict a sitting president.
