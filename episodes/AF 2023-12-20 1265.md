## Summary

The Colorado Supreme Court ruled that Donald Trump was disqualified from the state's 2024 Republican primary ballot, citing the 14th Amendment's insurrection clause related to January 6th, a decision his campaign appealed to the U.S. Supreme Court. The episode also covered an incident at Turning Point USA's AmericaFest in Phoenix, where activist Rob Smith was confronted by a group chanting slurs, leading to widespread condemnation from figures like Charlie Kirk and a subsequent CNN interview. Other topics included the dismissal of similar ballot challenges in Minnesota, New Hampshire, and Michigan, and commentary on the political influence of figures like Nicholas J. Fuentes.
