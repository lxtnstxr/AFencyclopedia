## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes detailed federal agent connections to several perpetrators of mass shootings, including Omar Mateen, Stephen Paddock, and David Hogg. The $1.3 trillion omnibus spending bill signed by Donald Trump provided $1.6 billion for border security but prohibited its use for a concrete wall. Trump described the funding as a down payment and claimed immediate wall construction despite the bill's restrictions. The "March for Our Lives" rallies featured activists like David Hogg and Emma González and advocated for a ban on semi-automatic rifles.
