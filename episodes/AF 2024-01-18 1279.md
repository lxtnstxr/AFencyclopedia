## Summary

The commentary analyzed a series of military strikes between Iran and Pakistan, linking them to broader regional tensions with Israel. It presented claims that Israel had historically infiltrated Baloch militant groups like Jaish al-Adl to conduct covert operations against Iran, aiming to provoke a conflict that would draw in the United States. The discussion also covered a pressure campaign led by donor Bill Ackman that resulted in the resignations of university presidents Claudine Gay and Elizabeth Magill, framing it as an effort to suppress criticism of Israel on campuses under the guise of opposing DEI policies.
