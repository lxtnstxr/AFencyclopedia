## Summary

Ron DeSantis announced his 2024 presidential campaign via a Twitter Space with Elon Musk and David Sacks, which experienced significant technical failures. The DeSantis campaign's announcement format was widely characterized as a glitchy and subdued event that highlighted his lack of charisma. Donald Trump's campaign announcements and rallies were highly produced spectacles demonstrating his focus on presentation and logistical detail. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued Trump possessed a superior strategic instinct for media and campaigning that DeSantis could not match.
