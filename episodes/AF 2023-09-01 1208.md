## Summary

Significant prison sentences were handed down to Proud Boys members Joe Biggs and Zachary Rell for their roles in the January 6 Capitol riot, while prosecutors sought a 33-year sentence for the group's former chairman, Enrique Tarrio. Concurrently, an online campaign using the hashtag "#BanTheADL" gained traction, criticizing the Anti-Defamation League's influence over social media content moderation and its role in advertiser boycotts, particularly following Elon Musk's acquisition of Twitter. The commentary also discussed the ADL's historical origins and cited examples of individuals and platforms, including Kanye West and the Fresh and Fit podcast, facing financial and professional repercussions for statements or associations deemed controversial.
