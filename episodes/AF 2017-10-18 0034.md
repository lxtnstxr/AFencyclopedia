## Summary

Paul Nehlen challenged Paul Ryan in Wisconsin's 1st Congressional District 2018 Republican primary, defining his ideology as economic nationalism. Donald Trump signed an executive order withdrawing the United States from the Trans-Pacific Partnership, an agreement Nehlen opposed and cited as his initial reason for running. Paul Ryan supported fast-track trade promotion authority for the Trans-Pacific Partnership and stated he would sue President Trump over a proposed Muslim ban.
