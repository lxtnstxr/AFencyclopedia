## Summary

The Economist retracted and apologized for labeling Ben Shapiro an "alt-right leader" after he publicly criticized the characterization. Bloomberg argued that high immigration is crucial for the United States's demographic growth and geopolitical competitiveness against China and Russia. Donald Trump blamed Mexico, Honduras, Guatemala, and El Salvador for not stopping illegal immigration and threatened to close the southern border. Breitbart News projected 1.5 million illegal immigrant entries in 2019, and the Trump administration sought congressional authority to deport unaccompanied migrant children.
