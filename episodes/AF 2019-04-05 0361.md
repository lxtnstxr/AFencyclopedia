## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes was banned from Twitch following a coordinated raid and reporting campaign after a political debate. The United States House of Representatives passed a resolution to end U.S. support for Saudi Arabia's military campaign in Yemen. Joe Biden faced new accusations of inappropriate touching and made a joke about receiving consent to touch a child's shoulders during a union speech. Donald Trump issued and later modified threats to close parts of the U.S.-Mexico border over immigration and drug flow concerns.
