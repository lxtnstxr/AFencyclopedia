## Summary

The House Judiciary Committee held a Juneteenth hearing on H.R. 40, a bill by Representative Sheila Jackson Lee to establish a commission to study slavery reparations. Senator Josh Hawley introduced the "Ending Support for Internet Censorship Act" to condition Section 230 legal immunity for large tech platforms on biennial FTC audits certifying political neutrality. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued that Donald Trump's 2019 re-election rally in Orlando lacked the substantive policy focus of his 2016 campaign.
