## Summary

Ron DeSantis announced his 2024 Republican presidential candidacy on a Twitter Spaces audio discussion with Elon Musk and moderator David Sacks. DeSantis secured financial support from major donors including Ken Griffin of Citadel and dined with donor Mariam Adelson during a foreign trip. Elon Musk purchased Twitter for over $40 billion and provided a platform for DeSantis's announcement, while David Sacks, a Claremont Publius Fellow, moderated the event. Peter Thiel and Paul Singer funded the Claremont Institute, a think tank connected to DeSantis's network of supporters.
