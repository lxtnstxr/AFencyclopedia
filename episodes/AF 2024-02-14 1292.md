## Summary

The United States House of Representatives voted to impeach Homeland Security Secretary Alejandro Mayorkas on two articles concerning border policy management. The episode also discussed a special counsel's report on President Joe Biden's handling of classified documents, which cited memory issues, and analyzed Biden's subsequent criticism of Israel's actions in Gaza. Commentator Nicholas J. Fuentes made extensive claims about Jewish influence in American politics and global affairs, alleging a coordinated effort by Israel's Likud party to support right-wing populist movements worldwide.
