## Summary

Joe Biden declared victory in the 2020 presidential election after major media outlets projected his wins in Pennsylvania and Georgia. Nicholas J. Fuentes announced "Stop the Steal" rallies at the state capitol buildings in Madison, Wisconsin, and Lansing, Michigan, and planned to attend a "MAGA March" in Washington, D.C. Fuentes cited alleged irregularities in Wisconsin, including a tripling of voters claiming indefinite confinement and a directive allowing clerks to cure absentee ballots. Michigan House Speaker Lee Chatfield announced the Republican-led legislature would hold hearings on fraud claims following a software glitch in Antrim County that initially misallocated votes.
