## Summary

Governor Ron DeSantis of Florida pushed a bill to ban sanctuary cities, which the Florida State Senate passed on a 22 to 18 vote. President Trump announced the United States was withdrawing its signature from the U.N. Arms Trade Treaty, which former President Barack Obama had signed in 2013. Health officials ordered quarantines at UCLA and California State University in Los Angeles due to a measles outbreak, which President Trump addressed by telling Americans to "get their shots."
