## Summary

Iran launched missiles at American bases in Iraq, after which a Ukrainian airliner crashed in Iran. U.S. and Canadian officials stated Iran shot down the airliner, a claim Iranian officials denied. A U.S. appeals court ruled in favor of transferring $3.6 billion in military funds for a border wall, a project which had built approximately 90 miles of replacement barriers by January 2020.
