## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes announced his return to broadcasting and previewed discussion of the Sri Lanka Easter Sunday bombings. The Sri Lankan defense minister stated the Easter bombings had a connection to the Christchurch mosque shooting in New Zealand, with ISIS claiming responsibility. Following the Christchurch shooting, New Zealand implemented a blanket ban on assault rifles and similar firearms. Winnipeg police arrested three owners of the B'nai Brith Cafe and charged them with public mischief for staging a hate crime.
