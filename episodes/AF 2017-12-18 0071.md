## Summary

Twitter banned Jared Taylor, American Renaissance, and several alt-right accounts in a mass suspension on December 18th. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued the United States invasion of Iraq destabilized the region, empowered Iran, and led to the rise of ISIS. Fuentes also stated that authoritarian leaders like Saddam Hussein and Muammar Gaddafi provided preferable order to the anarchy that followed their removals.
