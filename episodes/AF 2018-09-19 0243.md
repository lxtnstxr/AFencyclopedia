## Summary

Christine Blasey Ford accused Supreme Court nominee Brett Kavanaugh of groping her at a 1982 party, leading the Senate Judiciary Committee to schedule a public hearing. Patrick Smith, a witness named by Ford, denied through his attorney Eric Bruce having seen any misconduct by Kavanaugh. Claire McCaskill, a Democratic Senator from Missouri facing a tough 2018 re-election, announced she would vote against Kavanaugh's confirmation. Cody Wilson, inventor of a 3D-printed firearm, was accused of sexually assaulting a minor and fled to Taiwan while under investigation.

(00:17:12) [[Brett Kavanaugh]] faced new allegations from [[Christine Blasey Ford]], a psychology professor in [[California]], who accused him of groping her at a high school party in 1982, which she described to [[The Washington Post]].
