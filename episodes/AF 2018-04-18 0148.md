## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes and J.F. discussed a United States missile strike on Syria, which J.F. asserted was aligned with the interests of Israel. J.F. argued the strike would be remembered as an unjust act of aggression that impeded Syria's development. Nicholas J. Fuentes stated that neoconservatives in the Donald Trump administration, like John Bolton and Mike Pompeo, wanted the strike for Israel's national security interests.
