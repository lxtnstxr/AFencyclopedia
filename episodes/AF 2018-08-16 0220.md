## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes cited a Pennsylvania grand jury report finding 301 Catholic priests had abused approximately 1,000 children over seventy years. Fuentes argued that over 350 newspapers, including the Boston Globe, published coordinated editorials demanding press freedom in defiance of Donald Trump. He stated that six major conglomerates control 95% of U.S. media and that not a single outlet endorsed Donald Trump in the 2016 election. Fuentes reported the deplatforming of figures including Alex Jones from major tech platforms and Gavin McInnes from Twitter.
