## Summary

Anthony Fauci stated hospitals in some U.S. states were on the verge of having to choose who receives life-saving medical treatments. Major news outlets like NBC and the Washington Post published headlines questioning the ethics of rationing care based on COVID-19 vaccination status. The U.S. Food and Drug Administration was investigating potential links between mRNA vaccines and heart disease while Israeli researchers studied the vaccines' long-term effects. During the initial pandemic, Donald Trump implemented a wartime act to mobilize factories for ventilator and protective equipment production.
