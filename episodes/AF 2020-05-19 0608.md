## Summary

Donald Trump threatened to permanently withdraw United States funding and membership from the World Health Organization unless it demonstrated independence from China within 30 days. Newly released videos from 2017 showed Ahmaud Arbery being questioned by police in a Glynn County park and later being handcuffed for allegedly shoplifting from Walmart. Oklahoma Governor Kevin Stitt signed a bill banning the enforcement of federal red flag laws in the state.
