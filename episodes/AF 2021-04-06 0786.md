## Summary

The Arkansas state legislature overrode a gubernatorial veto to pass the Save Adolescents from Experimentation Act, banning gender-affirming medical treatments for minors. Anthony Fauci stated the United States government would not mandate COVID-19 vaccine passports, while White House advisor Andy Slavitt confirmed the Biden administration was only providing private sector guidance. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued that mandates from large corporations like Walmart or Pfizer were functionally equivalent to government mandates and criticized limited government conservatism. Delta Airlines, United Airlines, and PayPal publicly opposed Georgia's new voter ID law.
