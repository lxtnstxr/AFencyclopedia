## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes will headline a September 28th event in Miami, Florida, organized by demandfreespeech.org, where he will debate Jacob Wohl on Donald Trump's foreign policy. The Texas Republican Party expressed concern over demographic changes following Donald Trump's narrow 2016 victory and Democratic gains in the 2018 elections. U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement arrested 39 individuals across the United States in late August as part of "Operation No Safe Haven V." Mexico's Foreign Secretary Marcelo Ebrard announced a 56 percent reduction in illegal crossings into the United States between May and July 2019 following an agreement with the Trump administration.
