Nicholas J. Fuentes organized the first America First Political Action Conference in Washington, D.C. in 2020 and held a rally there outside CPAC in 2023 after the MGM Grand hotel canceled his contract. Fuentes was trespassed from the 2023 CPAC event, which had an estimated attendance of only 2,000 to 2,200 people.
