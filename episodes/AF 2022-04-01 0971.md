## Summary

Vladimir Putin demanded European countries pay for Russian natural gas in rubles to counter Western sanctions, threatening supply cuts. Germany, reliant on Russia for 40% of its gas, prepared for rationing as the European Union refused the payment demand. Following the invasion of Ukraine, the United States and NATO enacted severe sanctions, including seizing Russian currency reserves and banning Russian banks from the international system. Nicholas J. Fuentes was swatted live on his show and faced platform bans following the January 6th Capitol riot.
