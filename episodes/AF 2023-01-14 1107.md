## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes leads the "Groyper" movement, which he characterized as fighting Jewish power and advocating for Christian governance in the United States. Fuentes claimed his movement influenced the 2022 defeat of Joe Kent and blamed Republican leaders for the party's narrow House majority. The House Freedom Caucus, led by Matt Gaetz, secured a rules package restoring the motion to vacate the chair in exchange for electing Kevin McCarthy as Speaker. Fuentes revealed he worked with Kanye West on political ideas and criticized Marjorie Taylor Greene for making an early deal with McCarthy.
