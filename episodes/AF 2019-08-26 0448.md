## Summary

YouTube banned James Alsup's channel and several others, including those of Nicholas J. Fuentes and V-Dare, following an Anti-Defamation League article listing them. Nicholas J. Fuentes attended the Charlottesville "Unite the Right" rally and predicted his own channel's removal. A Pew Research study found 109 U.S. counties shifted from majority white to majority non-white between 2000 and 2018, a demographic trend analyzed by Ryan Girdusky and cited by Fuentes. University of Pennsylvania Law School professor Amy Wax advocated for immigration restriction at the National Conservatism Conference, prompting condemnation from Dean Theodore Ruger.
