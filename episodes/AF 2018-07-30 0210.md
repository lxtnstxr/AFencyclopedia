## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes debated libertarian Adam Kokish on a program moderated by Alan. The Koch Brothers network announced it would not fund the Republican Senate nominee in North Dakota and criticized Trump's trade policies. Disney fired director James Gunn for offensive tweets, though several lead actors later signed an open letter urging his rehiring. CBS appointed an independent investigator following sexual misconduct allegations against its Chairman and CEO, Les Moonves. Donald Trump stated he would meet with Iranian leaders without preconditions, an offer Iran rejected, and threatened to shut down the U.S. government over border wall funding.
