## Summary

Donald Trump terminated the Deferred Action for Childhood Arrivals program in September 2017. The White House later demanded $18 billion for a border wall and other security measures as part of a potential DACA deal. Stephen Miller was responsible for torpedoing an earlier DACA agreement by introducing a list of demands. The Trump administration deported 90,000 Haitians and 200,000 Salvadorians in a single month.
