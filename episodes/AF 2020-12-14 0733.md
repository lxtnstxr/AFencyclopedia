## Summary

Joe Biden received 306 electoral votes to Donald Trump's 232 in the formal December 14 Electoral College vote. Texas filed a lawsuit challenging election results in Michigan, Wisconsin, Pennsylvania, and Georgia, which the Supreme Court later dismissed due to a lack of standing. Nicholas J. Fuentes organized an America First rally in Washington, D.C., where a clip of his speech declaring "we are going to destroy the GOP" went viral on Twitter. Republican electors in Pennsylvania, Georgia, Nevada, and Arizona cast conditional electoral votes for Donald Trump and Mike Pence on December 14.
