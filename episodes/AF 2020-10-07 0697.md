## Summary

Kamala Harris and Mike Pence debated as the 2020 vice presidential nominees in Miami, moderated by Susan Page of USA Today. Harris accused the Trump administration of covering up the severity of COVID-19 in January 2020, while Pence credited Trump's China travel suspension with enabling Operation Warp Speed. Pence defended a Rose Garden event for Amy Coney Barrett's nomination, which Harris characterized as a "super spreader." Harris stated Joe Biden would repeal Trump's tax cuts and not raise taxes on those earning under $400,000, while Pence claimed those cuts saved the average family $2,000.
