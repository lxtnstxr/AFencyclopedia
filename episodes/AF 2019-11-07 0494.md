## Summary

The Young America's Foundation released a 2018 memo alleging unethical practices by Turning Point USA and that some of its members attended the Charlottesville "Unite the Right" rally. Turning Point USA, founded by Charlie Kirk, operates approximately 400 chapters and has a working relationship with the Trump presidential campaign.
