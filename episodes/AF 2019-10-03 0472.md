## Summary

The film "Joker," directed by Todd Phillips, premiered on October 4, 2019. Nicholas J. Fuentes compared the film's cultural impact for alienated individuals to that of "Black Panther" for black audiences. The United States Department of Defense and the New York City Police Department issued warnings and deployed officers over fears of violence at screenings. Concurrently, Speaker Nancy Pelosi announced a formal impeachment inquiry into Donald Trump concerning a phone call with Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelenskyy.
