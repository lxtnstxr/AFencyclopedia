## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes attended an Andrew Yang campaign rally in Chicago and assessed Yang's chances of winning the nomination as low. Attorney General William Barr submitted a summary concluding the Robert Mueller investigation recommended no further charges against Donald Trump, who subsequently recognized Israel's sovereignty over the Golan Heights. The U.S. Department of Defense reallocated one billion dollars from military personnel accounts to construct 57 miles of border fencing. All 16 felony charges against Jussie Smollett for allegedly staging a hate crime were dropped by Chicago police despite video evidence and the alleged attackers being known to him.
