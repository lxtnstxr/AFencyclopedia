## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes stated he received information suggesting the anonymous author of a New York Times op-ed was a person within the State Department. Facebook, YouTube, and several other platforms removed Alex Jones and InfoWars, with Twitter and Periscope issuing bans later the same day. Twitter CEO Jack Dorsey and Facebook COO Sheryl Sandberg testified before the Senate about shadow banning and election interference on September 5th. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued that power was consolidated in individuals like Jeff Bezos, Larry Page, Mark Zuckerberg, and Jack Dorsey, who could crush dissent without accountability.
