## Summary

Donald Trump rescinded the DACA program in August 2017 and offered to restore it in exchange for border wall funding, an end to chain migration, and an end to the diversity visa lottery. The Trump dossier, compiled by Christopher Steele and funded by the Hillary Clinton campaign and the DNC, was used as the basis for FISA warrants to surveil the Trump campaign. Nicholas J. Fuentes stated he was a fan of Richard Nixon's foreign policy and announced an upcoming debate with Jacob Wolf.
