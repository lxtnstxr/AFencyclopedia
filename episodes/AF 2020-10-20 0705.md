## Summary

The United States Department of Justice filed an antitrust lawsuit against Google, alleging it maintained unlawful monopolies in search and advertising. Activist group Shut Down DC planned disruptive post-election activities in Washington, D.C., including potential blockades, to counter a predicted coup attempt by Donald Trump. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued that only the federal government could challenge major tech companies and that the Department of Justice's lawsuit was leverage to stop discriminatory behavior.
