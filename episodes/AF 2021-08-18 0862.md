## Summary

The Taliban's takeover of Afghanistan prompted calls from figures like George W. Bush and Donald Trump for the United States to accept Afghan refugees. President Joe Biden authorized $500 million for refugee needs and had campaigned to raise the annual U.S. refugee admission cap. The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention was preparing to recommend COVID-19 booster shots for all Americans eight months after vaccination, citing the Delta variant. Nicholas J. Fuentes attended Boston University before working with the Right Side Broadcasting Network and stated that China quickly recognized the Taliban government.
