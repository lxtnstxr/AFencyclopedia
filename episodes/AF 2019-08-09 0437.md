## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes expressed opposition to interracial relationships and was challenged to a debate on the topic by CJ Pearson. Fuentes cited a Vox article arguing that ICE raids create a low-skilled labor shortage, and he contended that mass immigration suppresses wages for corporate benefit. Matteo Salvini's League party in Italy passed legislation to fine NGO migrant-rescue ships and saw illegal crossings drop during his tenure as Interior Minister. Fuentes contrasted the national media coverage of the El Paso and Dayton shootings with the lack of coverage for a California stabbing spree by a Mexican perpetrator.
