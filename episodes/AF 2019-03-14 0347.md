## Summary

The United States State Department referred to the Golan Heights as an area "under Israeli control" instead of "occupied territory" in a 2019 human rights report. The U.S. Senate passed a resolution, with more than a dozen Republican defections, to revoke President Donald Trump's emergency powers to allocate funds for a border wall. The Trump administration recognized Jerusalem as Israel's capital and moved the U.S. embassy there, while also withdrawing from the Iran nuclear deal and cutting aid to Palestine.
