## Summary

Scientific American endorsed Joe Biden for president, citing the Trump administration's impact on science and its handling of the coronavirus pandemic. Nicholas J. Fuentes planned to disrupt the digital convention "CancelCon," predicted a race war following the presidential election, and characterized the $12 million Louisville settlement for Breonna Taylor's family as a result of activist pressure.
