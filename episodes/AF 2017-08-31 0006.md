## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes alleged Benny Polatsek operated the "Reagan Battalion" Twitter account and linked Polatsek's firm, Colossal PR, to conservative commentators Ben Shapiro, Michael J. Knowles, Andrew Klavan, and Steven Crowder. Fuentes claimed Marco Rubio followed Polatsek on Twitter and that Treasury Secretary Steve Mnuchin canceled plans to feature Harriet Tubman on the $20 bill. Los Angeles replaced Columbus Day with Indigenous Peoples' Day, and Fuentes argued Barack Obama's presidency created conditions for Donald Trump's election.
