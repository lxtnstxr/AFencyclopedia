## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes declared victory in the "Groyper Wars" following Charlie Kirk's final campus tour event at the University of Houston. The majority of the audience at that event consisted of Groypers, who challenged Kirk on his criticism of Fuentes and his organization's funding. Charlie Kirk's attempt to play a critical video clip of a teenage Fuentes was met with technical difficulties and booing, after which the crowd chased him off campus. Ben Shapiro declined to debate Nicholas J. Fuentes, citing a Fuentes livestream gag about the video game Grand Theft Auto.
