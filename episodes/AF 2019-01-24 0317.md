## Summary

Donald Trump proposed a compromise offering border wall funding and temporary protections for certain immigrants in exchange for no pathway to citizenship. The United States Senate voted 50-47 against Trump's bill, with Democrat Joe Manchin voting in favor and Republicans Mike Lee and Tom Cotton voting against it. CNN reported Trump was preparing to declare a national emergency to allocate wall funding, and he later announced he would not deliver the State of the Union address until after the government shutdown ended.
