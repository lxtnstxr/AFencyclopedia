## Summary

House Democrats proposed a new $3 trillion stimulus bill including $1,200 cash payments for individuals. The remains of Confederate General Nathan Bedford Forrest were scheduled for exhumation from a Memphis park following the removal of his monument by the nonprofit Memphis Greenspace. Nicholas J. Fuentes criticized several conservative figures, including Dan Crenshaw, whom he accused of shifting political allegiances and demonstrating ignorance on immigration law.
