## Summary

The United States Senate approved a relief package focused on paid family leave and free testing and later passed a $1 trillion economic stabilization package. Joe Biden won the Democratic presidential primaries in Arizona, Florida, and Illinois, and FiveThirtyEight analysis concluded his delegate lead over Bernie Sanders was insurmountable. Global confirmed coronavirus cases reached 218,383, with China leading at 80,894 cases, and the United States surpassed South Korea in total cases. Donald Trump invoked the Defense Production Act to order the production of medical equipment and used the term "China virus" during a press conference.
