## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes announced a new "Groyper War" on TikTok and participated in a seven-hour Zoom call with approximately 100 political influencers from the platform. Fuentes argued that TikTok, with 800 million active users, represents a critical platform for his America First movement, as U.S.-based platforms like Facebook and YouTube have restricted similar content. Donald Trump tweeted he would sign an executive order to temporarily suspend immigration into the United States, citing the coronavirus pandemic.
