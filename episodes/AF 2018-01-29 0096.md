## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes constructed a new computer and broadcast the process via Periscope. Republican lawmakers led by Devin Nunes compiled a memo from an Office of the Inspector General report concerning FBI surveillance and the Trump-Russia dossier. Nicholas J. Fuentes claimed the dossier, compiled by Christopher Steele for Fusion GPS, was used to obtain FISA warrants targeting Donald Trump's campaign. FBI Deputy Director Andrew McCabe resigned after being named in the memo, according to Nicholas J. Fuentes.
