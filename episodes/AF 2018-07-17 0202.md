## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes reported that Donald Trump corrected his remarks on Russian election meddling at the urging of Mike Pompeo and Mike Pence. The Mercer family provided $15 million to Milo Yiannopoulos after CPAC rescinded his invitation, but his comeback failed following "Free Speech Week." Cassie Dillon wrote a critical piece about Milo Yiannopoulos in The Washington Post after previously defending him in Breitbart. Nicholas J. Fuentes cited the November 2015 Paris attacks and the July 2016 Nice truck attack as consequences of migration into France.
