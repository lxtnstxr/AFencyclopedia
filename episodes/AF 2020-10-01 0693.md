## Summary

The FBI warned of a threat of widespread extremist violence from fringe groups following the upcoming presidential election. The Trump administration set the fiscal year 2021 refugee admissions cap at 15,000, the lowest level since the program's inception. Donald Trump declined to commit to a peaceful transfer of power if he lost the election to Joe Biden.
