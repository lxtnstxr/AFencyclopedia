## Summary

Bill de Blasio called out Hasidic Jewish communities in New York City for not properly social distancing during the coronavirus pandemic. The Anti-Defamation League, the American Jewish Committee, and Ben Shapiro criticized de Blasio's remarks as generalizations and anti-Semitic. A New York Times article stated Hasidic communities faced unique challenges controlling coronavirus spread due to highly communal lifestyles and skepticism about social distancing. Anthony Fauci of the National Institute of Allergy and Infectious Diseases stated data showed the drug Remdesivir had a clear-cut positive effect in diminishing time to recovery from coronavirus.
