## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes announced Nikki Haley's departure as United Nations Ambassador. Nikki Haley was a neoconservative UN Ambassador who advocated for military intervention in Syria, Iran, and North Korea and was a favorite of the American Israel Public Affairs Committee and the American Enterprise Institute. Kevin McCarthy planned to introduce a bill in the U.S. House of Representatives to allocate $23.4 billion for a border wall. Hillary Clinton stated that one cannot be civil with a political party that wants to destroy what you stand for.
