## Summary

U.S. Ambassador to NATO Kay Bailey Hutchison stated the United States would seek to destroy a banned Russian cruise missile system if Russia did not halt its development. During his Supreme Court confirmation, multiple allegations emerged against Brett Kavanaugh, including a 1985 bar fight reported by the New York Times and gang rape accusations from Julie Swetnick. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued that NATO expansion prompted Russia's development of short-range missiles and endorsed a view of classical civilization as a uniquely white European inheritance.
