## Summary

Mark and Patricia McCloskey were charged with felony unlawful use of a weapon by St. Louis Circuit Attorney Kim Gardner for brandishing firearms at protesters. Michelle Malkin was attacked by Antifa and Black Lives Matter counter-demonstrators while speaking at a Back the Blue rally in Denver, Colorado. (01:27:52) [[Donald Trump|President Trump]] threatened to send federal law enforcement officers to major U.S. cities including [[Chicago]], [[New York City]], [[Philadelphia]], [[Detroit]], [[Baltimore]], and [[Oakland]] to control ongoing protests, following the deployment of a DHS unit to [[Portland]], [[Oregon]].
