## Summary

An episode examined Iran's direct missile and drone strikes on Israel, launched from Iranian territory and by proxies, which Iran claimed was retaliation for an Israeli attack on its Syrian embassy. It was argued that Israel had deliberately provoked Iran into this response, with the United States and other allies intercepting most of the attacks and subsequently urging Israel to de-escalate to avoid a wider regional conflict, which some claimed was a long-term Israeli objective. Amidst these developments, the U.S. Congress faced renewed pressure to pass military aid to Israel, potentially breaking a legislative logjam.
