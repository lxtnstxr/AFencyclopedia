## Summary

The World Health Organization declared the coronavirus outbreak a global health emergency while advising against unnecessary travel or trade restrictions and stating China had done everything right. Elizabeth Warren proposed civil and criminal penalties for election disinformation and a plan to hold technology companies like Facebook and Twitter responsible. China reported 9,800 confirmed coronavirus infections and over 200 deaths, with cases confirmed in 18 other countries including the United States, which recorded its first domestic human-to-human transmission.
