## Summary

China brokered the normalization of diplomatic relations between Saudi Arabia and Iran. Saudi Arabia approved a memorandum to become a dialogue partner in the China-led Shanghai Cooperation Organization and refused a Biden administration request to increase oil production. China and Brazil secured a deal to conduct bilateral trade in their own currencies, eliminating the U.S. dollar. The United States seized approximately $300 billion of Russia's foreign currency reserves after its invasion of Ukraine.
