## Summary

Donald Trump declared a national state of emergency under the Stafford Act, flanked by Mike Pence and corporate executives from Walmart, Target, and Walgreens. His administration partnered with Google to develop a coronavirus screening website and with major retailers to establish drive-through testing sites. Treasury Secretary Steve Mnuchin indicated further fiscal stimulus measures were under consideration. The S&P 500, NASDAQ, and Dow Jones rebounded approximately 9-10% during Trump's Rose Garden press conference.
