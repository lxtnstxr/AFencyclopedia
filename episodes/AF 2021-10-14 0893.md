## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes launched the streaming platform Cozy.TV, featuring streamers Jaden McNeil and Vince James and announcing three more would join the following week. Netflix co-CEO Ted Sarandos defended the Dave Chappelle special "The Closer" amid employee protests, and the company suspended and later reinstated three employees, including Tara Field, for crashing an executive meeting. A federal judge in Texas issued an injunction blocking United Airlines from placing unvaccinated employees on unpaid leave, which CEO Scott Kirby and President Bret Hart defended in a company memo. Nicholas J. Fuentes cited the Biden administration's extension of a CDC eviction moratorium after a Supreme Court ruling and its announced federal vaccine mandate as examples of governing without regard for the law.
