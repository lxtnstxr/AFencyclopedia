## Summary

Kamala Harris, Cory Booker, Jay Inslee, Kirsten Gillibrand, and Bill de Blasio launched direct attacks on Joe Biden during the second Democratic primary debate. Tulsi Gabbard delivered a widely-applauded attack on Kamala Harris, criticizing her prosecutorial record in California. Joe Biden defended his moderate platform on healthcare and immigration, arguing it made him the most electable candidate against Donald Trump. Andrew Yang performed strongly by consistently linking his debate answers back to his universal basic income proposal.

(01:08:21) [[Joe Biden]] argued that his moderate platform on issues like healthcare and immigration made him the most electable candidate against [[Donald Trump]], contrasting himself with more progressive rivals.
