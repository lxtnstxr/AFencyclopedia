## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes announced Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez and Bill Clinton as headline speakers for the Democratic National Convention's second night. Tom Perez opened the official nomination roll call from Milwaukee, Wisconsin, on the 100th anniversary of the 19th Amendment's ratification. The convention featured nominations for Bernie Sanders by Bob King and Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez and for Joe Biden by Jacqueline Asby and Lisa Blunt Rochester. Tom Perez announced Joe Biden as the Democratic Party's official presidential nominee after a state-by-state roll call vote.
