## Summary

Donald Trump withdrew the United States from the Iran nuclear deal and reapplied sanctions, later allowing oil export waivers for Iran to expire. John T. Ernest killed Lori Gilbert-Kaye during a shooting at the Chabad of Poway synagogue in San Diego, after which Oscar Stewart rushed the attacker. Nicholas J. Fuentes stated he would support a hypothetical Donald Trump dictatorship that carried out nationalist policies, even if it suspended the United States Constitution. A Charlottesville circuit judge ruled statues of Robert E. Lee and Stonewall Jackson are war monuments protected under Virginia state law.
