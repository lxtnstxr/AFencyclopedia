## Summary

Donald Trump announced a plan to withdraw United States troops from northeastern Syria, relocating many to western Iraq while maintaining a residual force near oil fields and the borders with Israel and Jordan. The film *Joker*, starring Joaquin Phoenix, earned nearly $900 million worldwide despite pre-release warnings from the United States Department of Defense about potential mass violence. A Texas jury ruled against a father attempting to prevent his young son from undergoing a gender transition, ordering the father to take classes on transgenderism.
