## Summary

The daughter of Russian political theorist Alexander Dugin was killed in a car bombing in Russia, an act the New York Times reported was authorized by parts of the Ukrainian government. OPEC Plus, led by Saudi Arabia and Russia, announced a production cut of two million barrels per day, a move the Biden administration criticized and countered with a release from the U.S. Strategic Petroleum Reserve. Ukraine distributed potassium iodide to its residents, and the U.S. government placed a $290 million order for the same radiation-blocking drug.
