## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes reported state-issued shelter-in-place orders in California, Illinois, and New York, followed by similar announcements for Florida, Ohio, and Texas. He observed widespread shortages of essential goods at major retailers in the Chicagoland area and cited escalating case counts in Italy, Spain, and the United States. Donald Trump announced suspended student loan payments and proposed direct cash payments, while Congress negotiated a $1 trillion stimulus package. Fuentes described the progression of lockdowns in Italy and warned of potential martial law in the United States, pointing to military vehicles in Baltimore, Chicago, and New York.
