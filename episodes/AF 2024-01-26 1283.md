## Summary

The episode centered on the escalating legal and political conflict between Texas and the Biden administration over border security, after Texas defied a U.S. Supreme Court ruling and continued to install barriers along the Rio Grande. It also covered the halt of a Senate immigration bill following private opposition from Donald Trump, and analyzed a terrorist attack in Iran alongside claims about U.S. and Israeli involvement. The discussion further included commentary on long-term political strategies and demographic changes, as well as critiques of Republican leadership and donor influence on immigration policy.
