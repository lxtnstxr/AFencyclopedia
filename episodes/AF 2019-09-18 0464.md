## Summary

Donald Trump appointed Robert O'Brien as National Security Advisor, a choice influenced by Jared Kushner and Mike Pompeo and seen in foreign policy circles as a neoconservative. Following a missile attack on Saudi Arabian oil facilities, the United States blamed Iran and increased sanctions, while Japan and France stated they saw no evidence of Iranian responsibility. Justin Trudeau faced a scandal after Time magazine published a photograph of him wearing blackface from a 2001 costume party.
