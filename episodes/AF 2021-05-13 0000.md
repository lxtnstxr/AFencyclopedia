## Summary

The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention announced that vaccinated individuals no longer need to wear masks indoors or outdoors in most situations. An independent audit of 2020 presidential election ballots in Maricopa County, Arizona, uncovered forensic evidence of deleted voter data and found significant physical ballot discrepancies. The U.S. Department of Justice intervened in the Arizona audit, alleging the process itself constituted voter fraud and intimidation. Nicholas J. Fuentes attended protest rallies in multiple state capitals to demand independent audits and sold out his America First merchandise line.
