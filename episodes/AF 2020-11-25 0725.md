## Summary

Donald Trump attended a Pennsylvania election hearing by phone and later called a state Senate hearing in Gettysburg, claiming voter fraud. A Pennsylvania judge ordered a halt to the certification of votes, and Trump urged the state legislature to appoint Republican electors. The Wisconsin GOP filed an emergency petition to stop certification, alleging illegal votes exceeded Joe Biden's margin. Nicholas J. Fuentes announced protest rallies in Arizona, Wisconsin, and Pennsylvania, and Donald Trump pardoned Michael Flynn.
