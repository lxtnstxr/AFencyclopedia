## Summary

The Leadership Institute developed "Operation Normandy," a project focused on generating sensational speaking events on college campuses. Stephen Paddock carried out a mass shooting from the Mandalay Bay hotel in Las Vegas, killing 59 people. The Clark County Sheriff stated Paddock must have had assistance, conflicting with the FBI's lone wolf narrative, and investigators found at least one automatic weapon among his arsenal. Elon Musk founded SpaceX with the goal of establishing a human colony on Mars.
