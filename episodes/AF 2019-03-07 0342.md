## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes delivered a speech on immigration at Iowa State University after campus groups withdrew support and counter-protesters filled his reserved room. The United States House of Representatives passed a revised anti-Semitism resolution condemning bigotry against several groups, a response prompted by comments from Ilhan Omar. Fuentes argued that America must maintain a white demographic majority for national cohesion, citing policies in Singapore and Israel.
