## Summary

Patrick Casey leads Identity Europa, which conducted demonstrations at the Mexican Consulate in New York City involving banner drops and members wearing construction uniforms. The Mexican government, a New York City council member, and a congressman condemned Identity Europa's actions, while some bystanders and responding police officers expressed support. Nicholas J. Fuentes praised the activism model of Identity Europa and Generation Identity for being controlled and member-vetted.
