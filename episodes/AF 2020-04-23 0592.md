## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes, Jaden McNeil, and Jake Lloyd received permanent bans from TikTok after a mass reporting campaign involving users like Gabby and Nick Videos. Jared Holt of Right Wing Watch contacted TikTok about these accounts prior to their removal. Donald Trump signed an executive order suspending new green cards for 60 days but exempting family members and certain essential workers. The U.S. House of Representatives passed a $484 billion coronavirus relief package, with Democrats securing provisions for hospital funding and testing.
