## Summary

Jeff Flake demanded a one-week FBI investigation into Brett Kavanaugh as a condition for his confirmation vote, a request joined by senators Lisa Murkowski, Susan Collins, and Joe Manchin. Mitch McConnell and Chuck Grassley requested President Donald Trump order the FBI investigation, which he possesses the sole authority to do. The Senate Judiciary Committee, chaired by Grassley, holds hearings and votes to recommend Supreme Court nominees to the full United States Senate, which exercises the Constitution's "advise and consent" role. Nicholas J. Fuentes debated Adam Kokesh on free markets, criticizing the U.S.-China trade deficit and advocating for infiltrating modern institutions to secure political power.
