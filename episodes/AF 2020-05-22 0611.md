## Summary

Joe Biden's remark that black people who do not vote for him are not black was defended by his campaign advisor Symone Sanders as being in jest. Over 30 college conservative organizations signed a letter demanding Donald Trump expand an immigration executive order to include work visas, a policy also urged by Republican lawmakers including Ted Cruz and Matt Gaetz. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued that low-skilled immigration increases black unemployment and that the America First worldview is rooted in Christianity, while also stating he does not support the BDS movement against Israel.
