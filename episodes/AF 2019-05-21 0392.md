## Summary

Donald Trump selected Ken Cuccinelli for a newly created White House immigration czar position, a role originally established under the Barack Obama administration. Stephen Miller leaked a list of demands from Chris Kobach, which led to Kobach being passed over for the appointment. A Harvard paper by John Lott found women's suffrage coincided with immediate increases in state government expenditures and revenue. Electoral analysis indicated that if only women had voted in the 2016 presidential election, Hillary Clinton would have won 458 to 80 electoral votes.
