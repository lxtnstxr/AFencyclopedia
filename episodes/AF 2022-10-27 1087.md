## Summary

Elon Musk was legally obligated to complete a $44 billion acquisition of Twitter by an October 28th court deadline. Twitter permanently banned President Donald Trump in January 2021, an action Nicholas J. Fuentes framed as the culmination of a censorship regime. The Anti-Defamation League organized the "Stop Hate for Profit" advertising boycott against Facebook in June 2020 and later demanded Twitter adopt its "Anti-Hate by Design" principles. Following the January 6th Capitol incursion, Apple and Google banned Parler from their app stores and Amazon terminated its web hosting services.
