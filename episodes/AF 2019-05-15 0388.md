## Summary

Alabama passed a law banning nearly all abortions and reclassifying the procedure as a Class A felony, which Governor Kay Ivey signed. The U.S. birth rate in 2018 was the lowest in 32 years, with fertility rates at historic lows according to the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention. Donald Trump withdrew the United States from the Joint Comprehensive Plan of Action, an Iran nuclear deal negotiated by the Obama administration. The United States gives Israel approximately $3.8 billion in foreign aid annually, according to Nicholas J. Fuentes.
