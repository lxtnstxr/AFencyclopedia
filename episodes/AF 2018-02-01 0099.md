## Summary

Donald Trump possessed a House Intelligence Committee memo alleging the FBI and Department of Justice used a Clinton campaign-funded dossier to appoint the Special Counsel for the Russia investigation. Several unexplained events occurred from October onward, including the Las Vegas shooting, power outages at Atlanta and New Orleans airports, and plane crashes killing Richard Cousins and Bruce Steinberg. Political figures including Nancy Pelosi, Chuck Schumer, and Adam Schiff opposed the memo's release, while Rod Rosenstein and FBI Director Chris Wray pleaded with Trump at the White House.
