## Summary

Jeffrey Epstein died by suicide in federal custody at the Metropolitan Correctional Center in New York City on August 10, 2019. The Unite the Right rally occurred in Charlottesville, Virginia, on August 12, 2017, to protest the removal of a Robert E. Lee statue. Nicholas J. Fuentes attended the rally, where he met individuals including James Allsup and made his first appearance on *The Weekly Sweat* podcast. Following Epstein's death, U.S. Attorney General William Barr directed the FBI and Inspector General to investigate the circumstances.
