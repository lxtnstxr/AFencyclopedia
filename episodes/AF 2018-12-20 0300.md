## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes hosted the 300th episode of his program and unveiled new "America First" merchandise, including a sweatshirt and mug. He humorously announced an "inauguration" into the alt-right, presenting new rules for the movement that included a ban on homophobia and a request to stop using a racial slur against his girlfriend. Nicholas J. Fuentes reported on a legislative dispute over border wall funding, noting the United States House of Representatives passed a bill with over $5 billion for a wall while the Senate faced procedural hurdles. He also stated Donald Trump ordered the withdrawal of U.S. troops from Syria and Afghanistan, a decision linked to Defense Secretary James Mattis's resignation.
