## Summary

Donald Trump announced a travel ban on Europe, excluding the United Kingdom, and proposed fiscal stimulus measures. The United States stock market fell 10%, erasing approximately $11 trillion in value and all gains made since the 2016 election. Multiple state governors, including Andrew Cuomo of New York and Jay Inslee of Washington, banned large public gatherings to combat the coronavirus outbreak. Major sports leagues, including the NBA and NHL, suspended their seasons indefinitely, and Disneyland closed for the first time since the September 11 attacks.
