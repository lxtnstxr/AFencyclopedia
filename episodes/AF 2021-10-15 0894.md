## Summary

President Joe Biden announced a federal vaccine mandate for large private businesses, federal workers, and federal contractors. The Occupational Safety and Health Administration drafted and submitted an emergency rule to the Office of Management and Budget to enforce the mandate. Mayor Lori Lightfoot mandated vaccination status reporting for Chicago city workers, prompting the Chicago Police Union, led by John Catanzara, to instruct officers to defy the order and pursue legal action. Two dozen Republican state attorneys general threatened to sue the Biden administration over the mandate.
