## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes launched afcandidates.com for America First movement candidates seeking U.S. House or Senate seats. Robert Hoogland was jailed for contempt of court in British Columbia for opposing his child's medical transition. Florida's pandemic-era unemployment rate was 4.8%, lower than Texas, New York, and California. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued Ron DeSantis's pandemic response was superior to Donald Trump's, which could affect a potential 2024 primary contest.
