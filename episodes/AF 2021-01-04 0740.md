## Summary

A joint session of the United States Congress will convene in Washington, D.C. on January 6 to count Electoral College votes from the 2020 presidential election, with a protest scheduled at the White House ellipse. Nicholas J. Fuentes announced his attendance at that protest and urged his audience to boycott the January 5 Georgia runoff election for Senate seats held by Republicans Kelly Loeffler and David Perdue. Fuentes argued the Republican Party establishment had multiple legal and constitutional opportunities to keep Donald Trump in office but systematically refused to act.
