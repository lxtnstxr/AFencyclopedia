## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes hosted a live 2018 midterm election broadcast with guests Bryden Proctor and Vince. Mike Braun won the Indiana Senate race against Joe Donnelly, while Beto O'Rourke lost the Texas Senate race to Ted Cruz. Ron DeSantis led Andrew Gillum in the Florida governor's race, and Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez won New York's 14th congressional district. FiveThirtyEight analysis showed voting outcomes differed drastically based on racial demographics.
