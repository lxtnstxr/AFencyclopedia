## Summary

The episode discussed Israel's planned major military offensive in Rafah, a city in the Gaza Strip where over a million displaced Palestinians were sheltering, which drew widespread international concern over potential mass civilian casualties and a refugee crisis. It detailed Egypt's initial threats to withdraw from the Camp David Accords over the operation, though later reports indicated Egypt had backed away from that threat. The commentary also covered U.S. President Joe Biden's criticism of Israel's conduct, internal political pressure he faced following a press conference, and the assertion that Israel's broader military campaign aimed to permanently displace Gaza's population.
