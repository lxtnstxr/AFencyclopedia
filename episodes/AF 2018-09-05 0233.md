## Summary

The New York Times published an anonymous op-ed by a senior official describing a faction inside the Donald Trump administration working to constrain the president. Donald Trump responded by calling the author "gutless" on Twitter and later questioned the individual's existence. Nicholas J. Fuentes identified figures like Nick Ayers, Mick Mulvaney, and Johnny DeStefano as key personnel who allegedly staffed the administration with NeverTrump opponents.
