## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes reported widespread closures and a historic drop in the Dow Jones Industrial Average due to the coronavirus pandemic. He stated the Democratic primaries in Arizona, Florida, and Illinois would proceed while Ohio postponed its contest. Fuentes presented global case numbers, citing 4,525 confirmed cases in the United States, and reported new federal guidelines recommending against gatherings over ten people. He also noted mandatory shelter-at-home orders in the San Francisco Bay Area and predicted similar lockdowns for other major U.S. cities.
