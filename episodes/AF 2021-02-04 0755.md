## Summary

Kyle Rittenhouse was charged with murder after shooting two individuals during unrest in Kenosha, Wisconsin, and was released on a $2 million bail. Bank of America provided customer transaction data from Washington, D.C., on January 5th and 6th to the Federal Bureau of Investigation to aid in identifying Capitol riot suspects.
