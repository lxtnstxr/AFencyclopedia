## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes streamed on YouTube and DLive as the novel coronavirus spread from China to the United States. Jaden McNeil founded the America First Students organization at Kansas State University following the disbanding of its Turning Point USA chapter. The Trump administration's immigration enforcement relied on policies like the Migrant Protection Protocols with Mexico and diverted Pentagon funds for border wall construction. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued that cultural practices and global travel from regions like China and India increased pandemic risks to the United States.
