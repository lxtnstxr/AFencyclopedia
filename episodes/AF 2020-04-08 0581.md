## Summary

Bernie Sanders dropped out of the 2020 Democratic presidential primary, leaving Joe Biden as the presumptive nominee. Joe Biden secured the nomination by building an insurmountable delegate lead, winning key states like South Carolina, Florida, and Ohio. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued that the Democratic Party's dysfunctions paved the way for Donald Trump's re-election and advocated for persistent political engagement. Fuentes also proposed creating a Christian commune called "Nicktown" and warned that automation and technological change could cause mass societal destabilization.
