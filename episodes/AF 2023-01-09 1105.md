## Summary

The United States Congress passed a $1.7 trillion omnibus spending bill with funding for Ukraine, the FBI, and Capitol Police, and it reformed the Electoral Count Act. Donald Trump hosted a Log Cabin Republicans gala at Mar-a-Lago, where he affirmed support for the gay community and was joined by attendees including Kari Lake and Richard Grenell. Kanye West appeared on InfoWars with Alex Jones and made statements about Adolf Hitler and the Nazis.

(03:24.360) The omnibus spending bill passed the [[United States House of Representatives|House]] with the support of nine [[Republican Party (United States)|Republican]] members and the [[United States Senate|Senate]] with the support of eighteen [[Republican Party (United States)|Republican]] senators.
