## Summary

The Conservative Political Action Conference was organized by the American Conservative Union and held in Maryland, featuring speakers including Mike Pence, Donald Trump, Nigel Farage, and Marine Le Pen. Major sponsors included the Heritage Foundation, the American Enterprise Institute, and the Heartland Institute, organizations significantly funded by the Koch brothers. Attendee Nicholas J. Fuentes interacted with multiple figures, including taking a picture with Nigel Farage and Marine Le Pen, while having confrontations with Will Chamberlain and Cabot Phillips.
