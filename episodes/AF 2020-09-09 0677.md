## Summary

The United States announced a troop reduction in Iraq to approximately 3,000 and planned a withdrawal of roughly 4,000 troops from Afghanistan. Donald Trump was nominated for a Nobel Peace Prize for brokering a deal between Israel and the United Arab Emirates. William Barr announced Operation Legend in Chicago resulted in over 500 arrests and a 50% drop in the city's murder rate over five weeks. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued that leaving any U.S. troops in Iraq, Afghanistan, or Syria invited future redeployments and that older, white working-class voters might support Donald Trump if he fully ended those wars.
