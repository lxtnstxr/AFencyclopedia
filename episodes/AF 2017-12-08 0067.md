## Summary

Adult film actress August Ames died at age 23 following cyberbullying over her refusal to work with a performer from gay pornography. Nicholas J. Fuentes predicted Roy Moore would win the Alabama Senate election, demonstrating the declining power of the mainstream media and Republican establishment. Fuentes stated multiple members of Robert Mueller's investigation exhibited anti-Donald Trump bias and credited Trump's policies with the territorial defeat of ISIS. Fuentes also claimed the DNA company 23andMe sometimes added trace African DNA to customer reports as a prank.
