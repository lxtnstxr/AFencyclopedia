## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes hosts the online program *America First* and participated in an interview on Eli Schafer's show *Slightly Offensive*. BuzzFeed accused Fox News host Judge Jeanine Pirro of echoing a white supremacist conspiracy theory, connecting her comments to the El Paso mass shooting manifesto. Donald Trump's executive assistant Madeleine Westerhout resigned from the White House, and Trump later publicly defended her after her dismissal. Kevin McAleenan, who protested Trump's nomination at the 2016 RNC convention, was appointed Secretary of Homeland Security.
