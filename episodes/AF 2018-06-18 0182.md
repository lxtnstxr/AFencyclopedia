## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes announced upcoming appearances by Laura Loomer, Jared Taylor, and Faith Goldy on his program. The 2017 Las Vegas shooting resulted in 58 deaths, and Laura Loomer later reported that documents showed Stephen Paddock's bank account had been seized beforehand on suspicion of terrorism financing. Donald Trump and Kim Jong-un held a summit in Singapore in June 2018, after which the United States and South Korea canceled joint military exercises. The Justice Department's Inspector General released a report on the FBI's Hillary Clinton email investigation, which revealed texts from agent Peter Strzok about stopping Donald Trump's election.
