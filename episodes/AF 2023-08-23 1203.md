## Summary

The 2024 Republican presidential primary debate featured seven candidates who critiqued the Biden administration's economic and energy policies while debating federal spending and abortion. Key exchanges included Vivek Ramaswamy labeling climate change a "hoax" and facing criticism over his experience, while several candidates advocated for a federal 15-week abortion ban. The absence of former President Donald Trump was a notable subtext throughout the event.
