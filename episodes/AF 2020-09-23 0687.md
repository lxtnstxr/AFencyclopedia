## Summary

A Louisville grand jury did not bring homicide charges against police officers in the Breonna Taylor shooting, leading to citywide rioting and a $12 million settlement. Donald Trump declined to commit to a peaceful transfer of power after the November election, citing mail-in ballot fraud. Nicholas J. Fuentes claimed the Democratic Party planned to use mail-in fraud to swing key states and characterized Black Lives Matter as a revolutionary shock troop movement.
