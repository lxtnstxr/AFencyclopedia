## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes released podcast content on SoundCloud and moved future episodes to the Maker Support platform. Emmanuel Macron deployed French troops to Manbij, Syria, as multiple nations, including Iran, China, and Russia, intervened militarily in the Syrian Civil War on behalf of Bashar al-Assad. The United States supported Kurdish forces in Syria, with Donald Trump authorizing an arms deal, while Turkey under Recep Tayyip Erdoğan captured Afrin and threatened Manbij, creating a potential NATO conflict. David Hogg led a Twitter campaign that caused advertisers to boycott Laura Ingraham's Fox News show, prompting her apology.
