## Summary

Los Angeles County extended its stay-at-home order for three months, and the California State University system closed all campuses for the fall 2020 semester. Illinois Governor JB Pritzker announced the state would not reopen until a vaccine was developed. United Airlines planned to lay off 14,000 employees in October after accepting federal aid with a no-firing stipulation. Charlie Kirk filed a DMCA complaint against Nicholas J. Fuentes and had previously campaigned for his removal from YouTube and Twitter.
