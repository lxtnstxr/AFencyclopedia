## Summary

A Manhattan grand jury indicted Donald Trump on charges related to a hush money payment to Stormy Daniels, making him the first former U.S. president to face criminal charges. Florida Governor Ron DeSantis announced his state would not assist in extraditing Trump to Manhattan. The U.S. Congress proposed the RESTRICT Act and the DATA Act, legislation intended to grant the president authority to ban TikTok and regulate software transactions with companies linked to China.
