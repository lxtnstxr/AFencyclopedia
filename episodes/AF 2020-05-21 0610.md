## Summary

Jaden Hayden was arrested for assaulting elderly patients at a Detroit nursing home and posted videos of the attacks on YouTube. Nicholas J. Fuentes claimed major news organizations did not cover the assault, contrasting it with the national attention given to the killing of Ahmaud Arbery. Donald Trump tweeted support for Michelle Malkin, which an article in The Bulwark by Mona Charen criticized as legitimizing Fuentes's movement. Fuentes argued that figures like Charen and Bill Kristol oppose Trump and support Middle East wars, placing them at odds with the Republican Party majority.
