## Summary

Donald Trump ordered a National Guard deployment to the U.S.-Mexico border and scheduled a meeting with North Korea's Kim Jong-un. Secretary Kirstjen Nielsen stated the military would explore border wall construction and confirmed Mexico disbanded a migrant caravan from Honduras. A shooting at YouTube headquarters in San Bruno, California, was perpetrated by Nassim Aghdam, who cited the platform's demonetization as her motive. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued Trump's foreign policy, including recognizing Jerusalem as Israel's capital, was influenced by donors like Sheldon Adelson.
