## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes hosted Vincent James on the America First show, praising his video arguing the United States is not a nation of immigrants. Vincent James's article for The Red Elephants projected Democrats will have 68 million more voters than Republicans by 2050 based on census data and consistent racial voting patterns. Vincent James traced the origins of cultural Marxism to the Frankfurt School's influence on universities like Yale. Nicholas J. Fuentes criticized mass immigration for eroding national cohesion and expressed concern about anti-white propaganda in school curricula from publishers like McGraw-Hill.
