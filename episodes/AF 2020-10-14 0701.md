## Summary

The New York Post published leaked emails implicating Hunter Biden in corruption schemes involving Ukraine and China during Joe Biden's vice presidency. Facebook and Twitter suppressed the New York Post's article and locked the accounts of the Post, Jack Posobiec, and Kayleigh McEnany for sharing it. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued that social media suppression of the story constituted election interference and that Donald Trump's pro-Israel policies were influenced by donor Sheldon Adelson. The Trump administration built 360 miles of border wall, renegotiated NAFTA, imposed tariffs on China, and directed the DOJ to bring an antitrust case against Google.
