## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes announced a marathon election night stream featuring several guests for the final night before the 2020 presidential election. Nearly 97 million people had voted early in the 2020 election, with registered Democrats leading in early voting in several key swing states. The activist group Shut Down DC, part of the Sunrise Movement network, planned to fill Washington, D.C. with protesters to shut down the White House and disrupt Congress after election day. Nicholas J. Fuentes described his show's operational model as a decentralized network reaching approximately 14,000 nightly viewers.
