## Summary

The episode centered on a debate regarding the so-called "Jewish question" and allegations of Jewish influence, prompted by Tristan Tate's public dismissal of the topic and Nicholas J. Fuentes's subsequent criticism of the Tate brothers. Fuentes argued that elite university admissions policies, particularly following the Supreme Court's decision to end affirmative action, were part of a deliberate effort to reduce white demographic influence, a process he characterized as "white genocide." The discussion also included Fuentes's claims that legacy admissions at schools like Harvard University disproportionately benefit Jewish students and his criticism of various public figures for avoiding the issue of Jewish influence.
