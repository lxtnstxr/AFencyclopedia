## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes reported on suspicious packages sent to prominent figures including Joe Biden, Robert De Niro, Eric Holder, and Barack Obama. Megyn Kelly was fired from NBC following comments she made on her program questioning the racism of blackface Halloween costumes. Secretary James Mattis announced the deployment of 800 additional U.S. troops to the southern border to assist with a migrant caravan. Donald Trump's approval ratings were high and Republican candidates were surging in several key Senate races ahead of the midterm elections.
