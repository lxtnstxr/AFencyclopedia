## Summary

Facebook announced policies to suppress premature election victory declarations and bar new political ads ahead of the 2020 presidential election. The company designated the Kenosha shooting involving Kyle Rittenhouse as a mass murder and removed posts supporting him. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued that major technology platforms threatened the America First movement and advocated for infiltration of conservative campus groups.
