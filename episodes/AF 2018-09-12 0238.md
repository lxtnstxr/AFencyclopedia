## Summary

Alphabet employees contributed $5.8 million to the Democratic Party in 2016, compared to $403,000 for Republicans. Google held 67% of the U.S. search engine market share, and a senior employee described a voter outreach effort as a "silent donation" to the Hillary Clinton campaign. Leaked footage from a 2016 Google meeting featured executives Sergey Brin, who compared Trump supporters to fascists, and Sundar Pichai, who committed to developing AI to combat "misinformation." Nicholas J. Fuentes cited Pew Research data showing low Republican identification among Mexican immigrants and identified AIPAC as a highly influential foreign lobby.
