## Summary

Donald Trump retweeted a video from Michelle Malkin discussing tech censorship, which publications Haaretz and The Daily Beast criticized by linking Malkin to Nicholas J. Fuentes. Nicholas J. Fuentes reported his YouTube channel was banned, moving his show to DLive, and identified figures like Ben Shapiro and Charlie Kirk as gatekeepers within establishment conservatism. Fuentes characterized the conservative movement as comprising neoconservative, neoliberal, and socially conservative factions, with the first two historically blacklisting the third.
