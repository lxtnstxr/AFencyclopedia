## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes participated in post-election protests in Michigan, Washington, D.C., and Atlanta following the 2020 presidential election. In Atlanta, Fuentes demonstrated with Alex Jones, rode on an InfoWars tank, and stormed the main stage of a rally after organizers Scott Presler and Brandon Straka refused to share it with him. The Oath Keepers organization refused to provide security for Fuentes at the Atlanta rally due to his controversial views. Fuentes later advocated for destroying the Republican Party, blaming it for failing to defend Donald Trump against alleged election fraud.
