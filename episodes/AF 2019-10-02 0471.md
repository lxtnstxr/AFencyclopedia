## Summary

North Korea conducted a submarine-launched ballistic missile test in 2019, which analysts considered its most provocative action since dialogue with the United States began. The United States Air Force responded by testing an unarmed Minuteman III ICBM from Vandenberg Air Force Base in California. The Federal Bureau of Investigation closed its case on the 2017 Las Vegas shooting in August 2018 without determining a motive for perpetrator Stephen Paddock. During the 2020 Democratic primary, leading candidates Joe Biden, Bernie Sanders, and Elizabeth Warren were all over the age of 70.
