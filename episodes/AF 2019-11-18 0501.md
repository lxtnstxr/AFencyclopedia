## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes announced the conclusion of phase one of the Groyper Wars at the University of Houston. Fuentes accused Charlie Kirk of supporting mass immigration policies and collaborating with left-wing journalist Jared Holt of Right Wing Watch. A coordinated media campaign against Fuentes was launched by Young America's Foundation, National Review, Daily Wire, and Human Events using a clip of him praising segregation. Fuentes appeared on InfoWars for an interview with Alex Jones and Owen Shroyer.
