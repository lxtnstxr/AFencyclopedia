## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes launched a new midweek episode of his podcast after reaching a Patreon goal. Federal authorities changed the official timeline of the Las Vegas shooting three times, and a New York Times investigation constructed a contradictory account. The Democratic National Committee and Hillary Clinton's campaign funded the creation of the Steele dossier through the law firm Perkins Coie. Stephen Paddock, the Las Vegas shooter, previously worked as an IRS agent and for the United States Department of Defense.
