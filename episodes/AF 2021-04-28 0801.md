## Summary

Joe Biden addressed a joint session of Congress 100 days after inheriting crises he described as the worst pandemic in a century, the worst economic crisis since the Great Depression, and the worst attack on democracy since the Civil War. Biden's administration administered over 220 million COVID-19 vaccine shots in its first 100 days and passed the American Rescue Plan, which provided direct relief, expanded Affordable Care Act enrollment, and aimed to cut child poverty in half. Biden proposed the American Jobs Plan to modernize infrastructure and create jobs, guided by a "Buy American" principle, and called for an Advanced Research Projects Agency for Health to combat diseases.
