## Summary

Texas filed a lawsuit against four swing states over alleged irregularities in the 2020 presidential election, gaining support from 18 states and 106 congressmen led by Mike Johnson. Donald Trump's administration oversaw 13 federal executions, while figures including Kim Kardashian West and Senators Dick Durbin and Cory Booker urged clemency for federal inmate Brandon Bernard. Nicholas J. Fuentes planned to attend Stop the Steal rallies in Washington D.C. and later dismissed any future collaboration with Stefan Molyneux.
