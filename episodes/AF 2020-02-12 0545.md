## Summary

Bernie Sanders won the 2020 New Hampshire Democratic primary with 25.7% of the vote. The World Health Organization renamed the novel coronavirus COVID-19 in February 2020. Nicholas J. Fuentes received a community guideline strike from YouTube and announced development of an alternative streaming platform.
