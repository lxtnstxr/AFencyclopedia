## Summary

The CDC's Vaccine Adverse Event Reporting System recorded over 12,000 deaths following COVID-19 vaccination, while a Harvard study commissioned by the CDC concluded such systems historically report only about 1% of total adverse events. Ben & Jerry's announced it would halt ice cream sales in areas of Israel it termed "Occupied Palestinian Territory," prompting Governor Ron DeSantis of Florida and Governor Greg Abbott of Texas to threaten action under state anti-BDS laws. Revolver News reported that undercover agents constituted approximately 60% of participants in the plot to kidnap Michigan Governor Gretchen Whitmer, and FBI Special Agent Richard Trask, a central figure in that investigation, was charged with assaulting his wife.
