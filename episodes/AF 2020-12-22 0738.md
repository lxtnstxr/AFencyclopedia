## Summary

Donald Trump threatened to veto a $900 billion COVID-19 relief bill unless Congress increased direct payments to $2,000 per person. A Georgia State Senate subcommittee determined the state's 2020 election results were untrustworthy, citing a monitor's absence during ballot counting at State Farm Arena. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued the governmental response to the pandemic would create a permanent expansion of state power surpassing the post-9/11 security apparatus.
