## Summary

The commentary centered on the U.S. Supreme Court's ruling that affirmative action in college admissions was unconstitutional, specifically addressing the cases involving Harvard University and the University of North Carolina. It extensively argued that elite universities serve as pipelines for national leadership and claimed affirmative action policies were designed to diminish non-Jewish white representation in those institutions. The discussion also included allegations of significant Jewish influence in American society and politics, referencing the exposure of commentator Pedro Gonzalez for past anti-Semitic statements and citing figures like academic John Mearsheimer.
