## Summary

Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez and Edward Markey spearheaded the non-binding Green New Deal resolution aiming to transition the United States to 100% zero-emission energy. A 2018 Yale University survey reported 92% of Democrats and 64% of Republicans supported the Green New Deal proposal. Declared 2020 Democratic presidential candidates in early 2019 included Cory Booker, Elizabeth Warren, Amy Klobuchar, Kamala Harris, and Kirsten Gillibrand. Donald Trump imposed a government shutdown in late 2018 to secure border wall funding, which ended with a continuing resolution expiring on February 15, 2019.
