## Summary

Donald Trump announced a plan to use $100 billion from a stimulus bill to pay hospitals for treating uninsured coronavirus patients. The United States halted shipments of respirators and N95 masks to France and Germany and ordered manufacturer 3M to stop sending masks to Canada and Mexico. Unidentified American buyers outbid officials in Germany and France for protective masks at Asian airports, diverting shipments to the United States.
