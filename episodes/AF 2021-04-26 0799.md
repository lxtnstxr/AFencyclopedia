## Summary

George W. Bush announced he was working with the Koch network to assist Joe Biden in lobbying for mass immigration legislation in Congress. A petition to recall Governor Gavin Newsom in California reached over 1.6 million verified signatures, forcing a recall election. Nicholas J. Fuentes advocated for amendments to a social media censorship bill in the Florida State Legislature and planned to cover Joe Biden's address to Congress. California lost a congressional seat for the first time due to population decline, a shift attributed to demographic changes from immigration.
