Fuentes announced the live stream of the first 2020 presidential debate between Donald Trump and Joe Biden, moderated by Chris Wallace and sponsored by the Commission on Presidential Debates. The debate covered topics including the Supreme Court nomination of Amy Coney Barrett, the COVID-19 pandemic, and the Affordable Care Act.
