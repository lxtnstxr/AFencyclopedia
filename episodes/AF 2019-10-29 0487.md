## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes and his supporters waged an ideological campaign against Charlie Kirk's Turning Point USA campus tour, challenging him at events in Colorado, Iowa, New Hampshire, and Ohio State University. Charlie Kirk declared unwavering support for Israel, stated that opposing demographic change or homosexuality is racist and homophobic, and leads a movement featuring ambassadors like Rob Smith and Ricky Rebel. Nicholas J. Fuentes accused Kirk of loyalty to Israel and Zionist donors, promoting homosexuality contrary to Christianity, and fighting for a future where white Americans are a demographic minority.
