## Summary

Rose-Hulman Institute of Technology canceled a scheduled speech by Nicholas J. Fuentes after its Young Americans for Liberty chapter was disaffiliated from the national organization. Christine Blasey Ford alleged that Brett Kavanaugh sexually assaulted her at a party in 1982, an accusation Kavanaugh denied. Republican Senators Jeff Flake, Bob Corker, and Susan Collins called for a delayed confirmation vote to allow testimony from both Ford and Kavanaugh. Dianne Feinstein forwarded Ford's allegation letter to the FBI in July 2018 before its contents became public weeks later.
