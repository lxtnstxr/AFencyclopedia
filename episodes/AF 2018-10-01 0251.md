## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes detailed the renegotiation of NAFTA into the USMCA, which altered dairy market access, automotive manufacturing rules, and intellectual property protections. Rachel Mitchell authored a memorandum concluding a reasonable prosecutor would not pursue Christine Blasey Ford's uncorroborated allegations against Brett Kavanaugh. Fuentes asserted that race is genetically determined, argued for preventing white demographic decline, and suggested racial minorities complaining of racism should relocate. Jared Holt of Right Wing Watch repeatedly identified Nicholas J. Fuentes as a white nationalist.
