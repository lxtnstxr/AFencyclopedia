## Summary

Ben Shapiro conceded that Andrew Neil defeated him in a contentious BBC interview. U.S. border crossings averaged 3,891 daily over a 122-day period in 2019, exceeding the total of the previous 13 years. NATO and the Barack Obama administration's 2011 intervention in Libya led to the overthrow of Muammar Gaddafi and subsequent lawlessness. Italy's policy under Matteo Salvini to engage Libyan forces reduced Mediterranean migrant arrivals by 92% from mid-2017 levels.
