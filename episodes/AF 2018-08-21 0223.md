## Summary

Paul Manafort was convicted on eight counts of tax and banking fraud related to his financial activities from 2014. Michael Cohen pleaded guilty to eight counts, including campaign finance violations that implicated Donald Trump. The White House fired speechwriter Darren Beatty after CNN inquired about his attendance at an H.L. Mencken Club conference. The Mueller investigation secured indictments against Paul Manafort for financial crimes predating the 2016 presidential campaign.
