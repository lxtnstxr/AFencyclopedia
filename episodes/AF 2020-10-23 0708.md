## Summary

Facebook and Twitter suppressed a New York Post article about Hunter Biden's laptop, leading the United States Senate to subpoena Mark Zuckerberg and Jack Dorsey. Madison Cawthorn won North Carolina's 11th district primary and later published an op-ed in The Daily Wire advocating for a Republican Party focused on issues like healthcare and climate change. Nicholas J. Fuentes claimed an axis of figures including Cawthorn, Ben Shapiro, and Nikki Haley was attempting to inherit the Republican Party from Donald Trump. Nearly 50 youth conservative organizations signed an open letter demanding Senate action against Big Tech censorship.
