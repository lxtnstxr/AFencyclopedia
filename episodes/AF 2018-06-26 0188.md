## Summary

The Supreme Court of the United States upheld President Donald Trump's third travel ban in a 5-4 decision. Sarah Huckabee Sanders was asked to leave the Red Hen restaurant in Lexington, Virginia, because she worked for the Trump administration. Representative Maxine Waters urged the public to confront members of Donald Trump's cabinet in public places. Henry McMaster, the Trump-endorsed candidate, won the Republican gubernatorial primary in South Carolina.
