## Summary

The Sinaloa Cartel besieged Culiacán, taking federal police hostage and forcing the Mexican government to release Ovidio Guzmán López. Hillary Clinton accused Tulsi Gabbard of being a Russian asset groomed to run as a third-party candidate, which Gabbard countered by calling Clinton the "queen of warmongers." The United States spent an estimated $6 trillion on post-9/11 wars in the Middle East.
