## Summary

Glenn Youngkin won the 2021 Virginia gubernatorial election against Terry McAuliffe, centering his campaign on opposing critical race theory and school mask mandates. Following Youngkin's victory, Donald Trump issued a press release taking credit for the win, which prompted public criticism from conservative commentators including Ann Coulter and Mike Cernovich. Nicholas J. Fuentes declared his allegiance to Donald Trump as the "rightful king of America" and criticized Trump's former advisors like Jared Kushner, while also criticizing Ron DeSantis's charisma and his actions regarding Israel. Fuentes argued Donald Trump would not lose a 2024 Republican primary and expressed uncertainty about the survival of Trumpism after Trump's death.
