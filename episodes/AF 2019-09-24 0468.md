## Summary

The Democratic Party initiated an impeachment investigation into Donald Trump over allegations he pressured Ukraine to investigate Hunter Biden. Nancy Pelosi formally directed House committees to proceed with the impeachment inquiry, stating Trump's actions constituted a betrayal of his oath. Donald Trump addressed the United Nations General Assembly, criticizing Iran and China while calling for the worldwide decriminalization of homosexuality. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued the Republican-controlled Senate would acquit Trump and that the impeachment effort would rally his political support.
