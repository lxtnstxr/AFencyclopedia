## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes argued that Georgia's "heartbeat bill" constitutes a de facto abortion ban and credited Donald Trump's Supreme Court nominations with creating an environment favorable to overturning *Roe v. Wade*. Netflix threatened to divest from Georgia over its heartbeat bill, while Louisiana's legislature passed a similar bill for Governor John Bel Edwards to sign. The Satanic Temple challenged an Indiana law requiring burial of fetal remains on religious freedom grounds, and a federal judge temporarily blocked Mississippi's heartbeat law after a challenge from the Center for Reproductive Rights.
