## Summary

The World Health Organization stated COVID-19 may become a permanent endemic virus similar to HIV. Former U.S. Representative Katie Hill resigned from Congress in 2019 following a public scandal. Republican Mike Garcia won a special election for California's 25th congressional district, marking the first Republican capture of a Democratic seat in the state in 22 years. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued that demographic trends favored the Democratic Party in California and characterized the GOP's victory in the 25th district as a potential last gasp.
