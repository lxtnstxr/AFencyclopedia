## Summary

Roy Moore won the Alabama Republican Senate primary by almost 10%, defeating Luther Strange despite an endorsement from Donald Trump and establishment backing. Nicholas J. Fuentes announced the development of "America First Media," a new media venture for the movement, and argued for racial separatism as a solution to perpetual conflict. Steve Bannon and Breitbart played a crucial role in organizing for Roy Moore's victory, after which Bannon planned similar primary challenges in Nevada, Arizona, and Tennessee.
