## Summary

Acting Secretary of Homeland Security Kevin McAleenan designated white nationalism as a major national security threat following incidents in El Paso, Pittsburgh, and Christchurch. Greta Thunberg led global climate protests and accused world leaders of inaction at a United Nations summit where Donald Trump made an unexpected appearance. Nicholas J. Fuentes characterized the terrorism designation as the Trump administration's biggest failure and argued the "Make America Great Again" movement centered on white racial consciousness.
