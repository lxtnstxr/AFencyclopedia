## Summary

Kanye West publicly endorsed Donald Trump and described their relationship as sharing "dragon energy." Kim Kardashian defended Kanye West against media criticism, arguing it unfairly labeled his free thinking as a mental health issue. Chance the Rapper stated that black people are not obligated to vote for Democrats, echoing a point made by Dinesh D'Souza. Nicholas J. Fuentes analyzed these events through esoteric frameworks, referencing Carl Jung and contrasting Western and Eastern dragon symbolism.
