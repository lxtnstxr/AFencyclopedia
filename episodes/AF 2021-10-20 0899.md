## Summary

Chicago police warned luxury stores on the Magnificent Mile about a string of armed robberies and burglaries by youths. Southwest Airlines reversed its employee COVID-19 vaccine mandate policy after mass flight cancellations, which Nicholas J. Fuentes attributed to an unofficial employee strike. Fuentes stated that major American news outlets omitted the protest narrative, while Russia Today reported the policy reversal followed a week of protests. The Biden administration's federal vaccine mandate applied to private companies with over 100 employees, federal contractors, and government workers, with enforcement fines filed through OSHA.
