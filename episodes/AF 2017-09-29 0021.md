## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes hosted a debate on North Korea featuring a guest identified as Vendetta. The guest argued a U.S. first strike would risk nuclear retaliation against Seoul and Tokyo and that eliminating North Korea's nuclear threat would require a full-scale invasion. Fuentes countered that the long-term risk of a nuclear-armed North Korea, including proliferation and regime instability, was unacceptable. The guest asserted North Korea has demonstrated rational self-preservation and that major powers like China and Russia were pursuing sanctions and diplomacy over military action.
