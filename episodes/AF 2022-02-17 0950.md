## Summary

The FBI appointed a new cryptocurrency task force, and the Biden administration announced new restrictions on crypto trading. The Trudeau government invoked Canada's Emergencies Act against the Freedom Convoy, arrested its leaders, and froze participants' bank accounts. Nicholas J. Fuentes announced speakers for AFPAC 3, including Joe Arpaio and Wendy Rogers, and gave a deposition to the January 6th Committee. The U.S. Justice Department created a National Cryptocurrency Enforcement Team led by prosecutor Eun Young Choi.
