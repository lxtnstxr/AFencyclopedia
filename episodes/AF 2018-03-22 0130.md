## Summary

John Bolton replaced H.R. McMaster as National Security Advisor on April 9th. Congress passed a $1.3 trillion omnibus spending bill in March, which allocated $1.6 billion for border security but prohibited funding a concrete wall. Donald Trump announced $60 billion in tariffs on China, and Kim Jong-un agreed to a historic meeting with Trump to discuss denuclearization.
