## Summary

The United States government blamed Iran for attacks on the oil tankers *Front Altair* and *Kokuka Courageous* in the Gulf of Oman and imposed new sanctions. Oberlin College was ordered to pay over $44 million in damages to Gibson's Bakery following a shoplifting incident and subsequent protests. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued that neoconservative forces within the United States sought war with Iran, while Donald Trump stated the country did not want a conflict.
