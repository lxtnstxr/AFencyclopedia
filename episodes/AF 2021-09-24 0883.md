## Summary

The United States resettled approximately 500,000 Afghan refugees following the war's end. The Taliban retook control of Afghanistan after the withdrawal of United States forces. The United States House of Representatives passed a bill providing one billion dollars for Israel's Iron Dome missile defense system, with Thomas Massie as the only Republican voting against it.
