## Summary

Brenton Tarrant killed 49 people in a mass shooting at two Christchurch mosques and live-streamed the attack on Facebook. Tarrant's manifesto, posted on 8chan, cited the Stockholm truck attack and aimed to provoke gun control debates in the United States. Following the attack, New Zealand Prime Minister Jacinda Ardern called for changes to the country's gun laws. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued that censorship on demographic change fosters extremist violence and identified Jared Holt and Christopher Mathias as sharing culpability.
