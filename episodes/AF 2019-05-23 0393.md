## Summary

Kyle Kashuv verified and apologized for past racist comments, including using slurs and making Holocaust jokes, leading to his resignation from Turning Point USA. Turning Point USA removed several chapter leaders and field directors, including Riley Grisar and Crystal Clanton, following reports of their racist statements. The United States, under the Trump administration, terminated waivers for Iranian oil purchases and issued a warning to Syria over alleged chemical weapons use. Nicholas J. Fuentes claimed to have shifted his focus to opposing figures like Ben Shapiro and Turning Point USA.
