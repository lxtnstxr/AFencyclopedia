## Summary

Anthony Fauci called for another economic lockdown during the coronavirus pandemic and publicly disagreed with Donald Trump's assessment of the situation. Donald Trump brokered a peace deal normalizing ties between Bahrain and Israel, with Jared Kushner credited for helping broker the agreement. Nicholas J. Fuentes rejected the official narrative of the September 11 attacks and argued that the pandemic response aimed to sabotage Trump's re-election. Paul Krugman claimed hate crimes against Muslims in the United States spiked after September 11, a claim later disputed by Al Jazeera.
