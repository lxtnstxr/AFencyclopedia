## Summary

Major riots erupted in Dublin following a stabbing attack by an Algerian immigrant, after which the Irish government proposed stringent new hate speech legislation. A U.S. House Republican committee report estimated the annual net cost of illegal immigration at $150 billion, citing figures from the Federation for American Immigration Reform and highlighting statements from New York City Mayor Eric Adams and an Arizona hospital CEO. The episode also noted planned online appearances by commentators Nicholas J. Fuentes and Richard Spencer.
