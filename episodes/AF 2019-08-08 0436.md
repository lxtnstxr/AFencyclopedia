## Summary

India revoked Kashmir's autonomous status under Prime Minister Narendra Modi, prompting Pakistan to suspend diplomatic relations and trade. U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement detained approximately 700 undocumented immigrants in Mississippi raids, later releasing about half. Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez experienced a staff shakeup and promoted the Green New Deal, which divided the Democratic Party. Tucker Carlson argued white supremacy was an unimportant issue and a political tool, an analysis Nicholas J. Fuentes compared to Steve Bannon's focus on class conflict.
