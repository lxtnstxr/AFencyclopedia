## Summary

A report from the Armed Conflict Location and Event Data Project and Princeton University found 93% of Black Lives Matter demonstrations in a 2020 period were peaceful. Michael Forest Reinoehl fatally shot Aaron J. Danielson in Portland and was later killed by U.S. Marshals in Washington State. Kyle Rittenhouse killed two protesters in Kenosha, Wisconsin, claiming self-defense.

(00:01:30) A report from the [[Armed Conflict Location and Event Data Project]] and [[Princeton University]]'s Bridging Divides Initiative, published on a Thursday, found that 93% of demonstrations associated with the Black Lives Matter movement between May 24 and August 22 were peaceful, with violence occurring in fewer than 220 distinct locations across the [[United States]].
