## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes announced a weekday schedule for his show beginning at 7 p.m. Central Standard Time. Fuentes characterized Abraham Lincoln as a conservative statesman and labeled Confederate secession as a revolutionary act. He also described Alexander Hamilton and John Adams as right-wing figures who countered the classical liberalism of Thomas Jefferson.
