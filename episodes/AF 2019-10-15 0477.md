## Summary

The fourth Democratic presidential primary debate featured a record twelve candidates, including newcomers Tom Steyer and Tulsi Gabbard.  Joe Biden, Elizabeth Warren, and Bernie Sanders led national polls, with Warren becoming the primary target of attacks during the event.  Donald Trump's 2016 debate style and his focus on Hunter Biden's business dealings in Ukraine were noted in related commentary.
