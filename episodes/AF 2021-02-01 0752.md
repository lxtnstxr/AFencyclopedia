## Summary

Major anti-lockdown protests occurred across several European countries including Denmark, the Netherlands, Austria, Hungary, and Poland. Mitch McConnell criticized Marjorie Taylor Greene for promoting conspiracy theories, calling them a "cancer for the Republican Party." Ted Cruz described Donald Trump's post-election claims of a landslide victory and widespread fraud as "reckless and irresponsible." Nicholas J. Fuentes argued the GameStop short squeeze was "fake" theater embraced by Netflix, Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez, and Jon Stewart, and asserted the 2020 election was fraudulent in Wisconsin, Michigan, and Georgia.
