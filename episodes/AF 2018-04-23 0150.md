## Summary

Tucker Carlson resumed his show and Twitter activity after an absence following his commentary on a Syrian chemical attack narrative. Kanye West was hospitalized in California for a psychological evaluation after not sleeping and later reactivated his Twitter to endorse Candace Owens and share videos of Scott Adams. Donald Trump secured Rand Paul's vote for Mike Pompeo's Secretary of State confirmation with a personal guarantee on Iraq War policy. Nikki Haley stated the 2018 U.S. missile strikes in Syria, coordinated with Russia, aimed to deter chemical weapons without causing casualties or regime change.
