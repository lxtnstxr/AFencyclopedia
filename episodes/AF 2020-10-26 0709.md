## Summary

The United States Senate confirmed Amy Coney Barrett to the Supreme Court by a 52-48 vote, with Susan Collins as the sole Republican opposing. Donald Trump signed an executive order reclassifying federal employees to Schedule F, affecting an estimated 100,000 workers. Nicholas J. Fuentes called for civil disobedience against COVID-19 regulations and expressed a philosophical opposition to women serving on the Supreme Court. Trump's administration recognized Israeli sovereignty over the Golan Heights and designated the Iranian Revolutionary Guard as a terrorist organization.
