## Summary

Douglas Mackey operated the influential pro-Donald Trump Twitter account Ricky Vaughn and was later charged with election interference by the United States Department of Justice under the Biden Administration. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued that Ron DeSantis's electability against Donald Trump eroded as DeSantis adopted more conservative positions, and he criticized the Republican establishment's tactical approach to cultural issues. Fuentes also warned that a Mackey conviction would set a precedent for prosecuting political misinformation and dismissed Marjorie Taylor Greene's legislative efforts as impotent compared to her appearance at his AFPAC event.
