## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes live-streamed the "Cancel Con" event sponsored by Young America's Foundation. Panelists Dennis Prager, Adam Carolla, Dave Rubin, and Ben Shapiro presented various arguments on contemporary political and cultural controversies. Ben Shapiro cited data showing his website, Dailywire.com, was the top publisher on Facebook in July 2020. Dennis Prager claimed he may have helped Sergey Brin's family emigrate from the Soviet Union and later argued Brin was "creating a Soviet Union" in America through Google and YouTube.
