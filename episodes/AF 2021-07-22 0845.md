## Summary

Ben & Jerry's ceased ice cream sales in Israeli settlements in the West Bank and Gaza Strip, prompting accusations of supporting the BDS movement from Israeli officials. Israeli Ambassador Gilad Erdan urged U.S. state governors to act against the company under anti-BDS laws, while Prime Minister Naftali Bennett vowed action against its parent company Unilever. FBI agent Richard Trask, a key figure in the Gretchen Whitmer kidnapping investigation, was arrested and charged with assault. Nicholas J. Fuentes announced a presale for sunglasses and the restoration of credit card processing for his merchandise website.
