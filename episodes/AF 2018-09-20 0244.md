## Summary

Christine Blasey Ford accused Supreme Court nominee Brett Kavanaugh of groping her in 1982, leading the Senate Judiciary Committee under Chuck Grassley to schedule a hearing she initially declined. The United States Senate approved an $854 billion spending bill with $675 billion for the Pentagon but no funding for a border wall, which President Donald Trump signed despite objections. Trump had previously proposed a deal to Democrats offering a DACA extension in exchange for $25 billion for a wall, but a later omnibus bill allocated only $1.6 billion with restrictions on barrier construction. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued that mass immigration was altering the United States' demographic composition and contrasted the $38 billion aid commitment to Israel with the lack of wall funding.
