## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes announced a delay for his "White Boy Summer" documentary and criticized Mike Cernovich and Jack Murphy for advocating mask-wearing and vaccine compliance. Pfizer planned to seek FDA authorization for its COVID-19 vaccine for infants and children aged five to eleven. Over 9,300 mostly Haitian migrants were held under the Del Rio International Bridge in Texas, where Governor Greg Abbott altered border security plans and the FAA imposed a temporary flight restriction.

(00:16:00) [[Nicholas J. Fuentes]] recounted a debate between [[Jack Murphy]] and [[Tim Pool]] on [[Tim Pool|Tim Pool's]] show regarding vaccine mandates for children's sports participation.
