## Summary

Donald Trump and Melania Trump tested positive for the coronavirus, leading to Trump's hospitalization at Walter Reed National Military Medical Center. Newly released audio from the Breonna Taylor investigation featured Louisville police officers stating they announced their presence before entering her apartment. Vladimir Putin and Kim Jong-un were among the world leaders who wished Donald Trump a speedy recovery from COVID-19. Louisville paid a $12 million settlement to Breonna Taylor's mother following her death.
