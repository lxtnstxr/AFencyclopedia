## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes declared the day of his broadcast to be "white man's day" and argued that men's societal role leads to high rates of suicide, homelessness, and workplace fatalities. He reported that midterm election recounts in Florida and Georgia confirmed victories for Republicans Ron DeSantis, Rick Scott, and Brian Kemp. Fuentes criticized a Fox News interview where Chris Wallace questioned Donald Trump about criticism from retired Navy SEAL Bill McRaven. He argued that Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez's populist economic message threatened the Republican Party, which he stated had failed to provide tangible benefits to its core voters.
