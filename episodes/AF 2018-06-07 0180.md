## Summary

Donald Trump and Kim Jong-un held a bilateral summit in Singapore on June 12th, 2018. Mike Pompeo, as CIA Director and Secretary of State, made arrangements for the summit and publicly distanced the administration from Rudy Giuliani's remarks on the negotiations. Rich Madalino released a campaign ad for Governor of Maryland highlighting his opposition to Donald Trump, which culminated in footage of him kissing his husband. Nicholas J. Fuentes gained attention after a debate at Boston University, which was live-streamed by Cassie Dillon and viewed by Ben Shapiro and Milo Yiannopoulos.
