## Summary

A Donald Trump supporter was killed at a rally in Denver, an incident receiving minimal mainstream media coverage. Nicholas J. Fuentes criticized the nomination of Amy Coney Barrett and argued for the strategic importance of her Supreme Court confirmation. YouTube banned Baked Alaska's channel following a campaign by Tim Heidecker and Vic Berger, which Fuentes cited as part of a broader removal of right-wing content creators from major platforms. Fuentes also predicted Donald Trump would likely lose the 2020 election and advocated for the United States to retain territorial holdings like Hawaii and Puerto Rico without granting voting rights.
