## Summary

New York City declared a state of emergency after receiving over 17,000 migrants since April, a situation Mayor Eric Adams stated could cost $1 billion. Joe Biden issued a federal pardon for individuals convicted of simple marijuana possession and urged state governors to issue similar pardons. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued that marijuana legalization increased consumption and related harms and advocated for the death penalty for drug dealers, a policy Donald Trump also supported.
