## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes announced Styxhexenhammer666 would appear on his show to discuss a gubernatorial bid in Vermont. Emmanuel Macron met with Donald Trump to discuss the Iran nuclear deal, trade, and Syria, following Macron's 2017 electoral victory over Marine Le Pen. A United Kingdom high court ruled against the parents of Alfie Evans, preventing travel to Italy for medical treatment under the country's child protection laws.
