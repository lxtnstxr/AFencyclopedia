## Summary

Corey Stewart ran for the United States Senate in Virginia as a Republican and previously led Donald Trump's 2016 campaign in the state. The Daily Wire and The Weekly Standard published articles criticizing Stewart for his 2017 praise of Paul Nehlen. The Trump administration's 2017 Tax Cuts and Jobs Act reduced taxes for most Americans and cut the corporate tax rate from 35% to 21%. Donald Trump withdrew the United States from the Joint Comprehensive Plan of Action and re-imposed sanctions on Iran.
