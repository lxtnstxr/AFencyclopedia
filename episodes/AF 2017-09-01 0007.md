## Summary

President Donald Trump considered canceling the Deferred Action for Childhood Arrivals program, while Speaker Paul Ryan urged a legislative solution. An 88-year-old German woman was imprisoned for Holocaust denial, a crime in Germany, prompting criticism of commentators like Ben Shapiro for their silence. Nicholas J. Fuentes advocated terminating DACA and expressed admiration for French nationalist Marine Le Pen, while also rejecting democracy and fascism.
