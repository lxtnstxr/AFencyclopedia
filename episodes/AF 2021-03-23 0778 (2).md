## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes announced a regular audio stream on his Telegram channel after a test with 350 participants. Ahmad Al-Aliwi Alissa, a 21-year-old from Arvada, Colorado, was identified as the suspect in a shooting at a Boulder King Soopers that killed ten people. The FBI had been aware of Alissa from an unrelated investigation one day prior to the shooting. Joe Biden called on the Senate to pass House bills to close background check loopholes and ban assault weapons following shootings in Atlanta and Boulder.
