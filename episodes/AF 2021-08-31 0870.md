## Summary

A BBC report found U.S. hate crimes reached a 12-year high in 2020, with over 10,000 incidents and black Americans as the most targeted group. President Joe Biden defended the U.S. troop withdrawal from Afghanistan and vowed to continue bombing ISIS. Several Fox News hosts, including Chris Wallace and Greg Gutfeld, warned that a terror attack on U.S. soil from Afghanistan would end the Biden administration. Nicholas J. Fuentes praised the Taliban's victory and listed them among sources of hope alongside Russia, China, and Iran.
