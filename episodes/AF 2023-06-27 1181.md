## Summary

The episode discussed the aborted rebellion by the Wagner Group in Russia, analyzing its advance toward Moscow and the subsequent deal brokered by Belarusian President Alexander Lukashenko that ended hostilities. It also covered the federal criminal case against former President Donald Trump regarding his handling of classified documents, characterizing the charges as minor paperwork issues and alleging prosecutorial bias. Notable figures discussed included Yevgeny Prigozhin, Vladimir Putin, and Trump, with the commentary expressing skepticism toward the official narratives of both events.
