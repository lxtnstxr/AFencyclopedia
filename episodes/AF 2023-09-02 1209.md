## Summary

Republican presidential candidate Vivek Ramaswamy called for ending U.S. foreign aid to Israel, prompting sharp criticism from rivals Mike Pence and Nikki Haley, who accused him of misunderstanding the alliance and the threat from Iran. Separately, political commentator Nicholas J. Fuentes outlined his goals to counter what he termed "white genocide" and establish a Catholic elite, while detailing the financial and platform consequences he faced following the January 6th Capitol riot. Fuentes also praised influencer Keith Woods as a rising star within his movement.
