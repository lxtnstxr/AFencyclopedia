## Summary

Donald Trump announced a 30-day travel suspension from Europe, excluding the United Kingdom, and requested $50 billion from Congress for small business loans. New York Governor Andrew Cuomo deployed the National Guard to a containment zone in New Rochelle and shifted state university systems to online classes. Italy's Prime Minister Giuseppe Conte ordered the closure of most non-essential stores nationwide, while the National Basketball Association suspended its season following Rudy Gobert's positive test.
