## Summary

Oakland, California approved a privately funded program providing $500 monthly to low-income families of color. The Department of Homeland Security considered placing suspected domestic violent extremists and Trump supporters on the FBI's no-fly list following a January 7th call from Representative Bennie Thompson. Nicholas J. Fuentes announced a new weekly radio show titled "Good Morning, Groyper" on Telegram and an upcoming AMA on the Politics Discord server.
