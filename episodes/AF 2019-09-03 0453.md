## Summary

Emilio Carrasco Hernandez, a previously deported illegal immigrant, was arrested for rape in Montgomery County, Maryland. Nicholas J. Fuentes identified Turning Point USA figures as a primary threat and argued economic conservatives like Ben Shapiro are greater enemies than the left. Following an attack on an ICE facility in Tacoma, the Seattle Antifa chapter issued a letter calling the perpetrator a martyr. Montgomery County Executive Mark Elrich signed an executive order designating the county a sanctuary jurisdiction.

(00:01:33) [[Emilio Carrasco Hernandez]], an illegal immigrant previously deported in 2017, was arrested for raping a 15-year-old girl in [[Montgomery County, Maryland]], marking the seventh such rape by an illegal immigrant in that county since July 25th.

(00:02:56) An individual used a Molotov cocktail to attack an [[Immigration and Customs Enforcement|ICE]] facility in [[Oakland Park, Florida]], following similar attacks on ICE facilities in [[San Antonio, Texas]] and [[Tacoma, Washington]] earlier in the summer.
