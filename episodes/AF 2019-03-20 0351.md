## Summary

A Senegalese migrant, Ousseynou N'Doye, set a school bus on fire in Milan to protest the anti-immigration policies of Italian Deputy Prime Minister Matteo Salvini. Matteo Salvini's policies as Interior Minister, which included closing Italian ports to NGO rescue ships, contributed to a 92% decrease in illegal migrant crossings into the European Union from 2015 to 2018. The 2015 European migrant crisis was substantially caused by the destabilization of the Middle East through U.S. and NATO interventions, including the deposition of Muammar Gaddafi in Libya. Donald Trump's administration abandoned its zero-tolerance border policy, which had led to a family separation crisis, and oversaw a high number of border crossings in February 2019.
