## Summary

The United States Department of Justice opened an investigation into Hillary Clinton and the Clinton Foundation over alleged pay-to-play schemes. Hillary Clinton maintained a private email server to transmit classified documents and deleted 33,000 emails after a congressional subpoena. U.S. Senators Lindsey Graham and Chuck Grassley referred Christopher Steele for criminal investigation regarding the Trump-Russia dossier compiled for Fusion GPS. Donald Trump demanded $18 billion in border wall funding in exchange for protecting DACA recipients.
