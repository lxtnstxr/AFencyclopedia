## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes confirmed he spoke with Anya Parampil about her article for The Gray Zone, a publication run by Max Blumenthal. Parampil's article accused Fuentes of urging violence, undermining the America First movement, and having his assets frozen due to his presence at the U.S. Capitol on January 6. Fuentes stated the U.S. Department of Justice cited his January 6 activities as the reason for investigating his assets, contradicting the article's claim of a separate money laundering probe. The article characterized Fuentes as a "Christian reactionary" whose America First Foundation hosts a convention rivaling CPAC.
