## Summary

Donald Trump vowed to seal the United States border against a migrant caravan that grew to between 5,000 and 10,000 people as it moved through Mexico. Trump also announced his intention to withdraw the United States from the 1987 Intermediate-Range Nuclear Forces Treaty, alleging Russia violated it with a new SSC-8 missile. At the Politicon event, Tucker Carlson debated Cenk Uygur on immigration, with Carlson focusing on legality and Uygur defending the caravan. Nicholas J. Fuentes characterized the event as assembling the left and controlled opposition from the right.
