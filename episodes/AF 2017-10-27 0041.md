## Summary

The Canary Mission website maintains a database of individuals in the United States who have criticized Israel, listing figures like Nicholas J. Fuentes, James Alsup, and Richard Spencer. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued that organizations like the Anti-Defamation League and Canary Mission create registries of critics of Israel. Fuentes also stated that Israel has been the largest recipient of U.S. foreign aid since the late 1970s, receiving approximately $3.8 billion annually. He further contended that Israel has sold advanced U.S. military technology to China and Iran and built civilian settlements in the West Bank, which are illegal under international law.
