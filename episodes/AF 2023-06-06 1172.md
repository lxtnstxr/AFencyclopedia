## Summary

The episode detailed a reversal by Representative Marjorie Taylor Greene on releasing January 6th Capitol security footage, shifting from a long-standing demand for full public access to supporting limited distribution to select media outlets. It centered on a political deal between Greene and Speaker Kevin McCarthy, where her support for his speakership and a debt ceiling bill was exchanged for committee assignments, despite an earlier concession from McCarthy to release the tapes. Key claims included that McCarthy's position was backed by corporate and foreign lobbyists, and that the handling of the footage was used to manage internal Republican dissent.
