## Summary

The United States Congress passed a $1.9 trillion COVID-19 stimulus bill without Republican support, bringing total pandemic-related spending to $6 trillion. Government officials initiated economic lockdowns on March 11, 2020, presenting them as a temporary measure to slow COVID-19's spread. The New York Post reported that Cassidy Kural, a 39-year-old surgical technician from Utah, died days after receiving a second Moderna vaccine dose. China released the genetic sequence of the novel coronavirus in January 2020.
