## Summary

New York became the 13th state to allow undocumented immigrants to obtain driver's licenses following a dismissed legal challenge to Governor Andrew Cuomo's "Green Light Law." A proposed 2020 federal spending bill allocated $1.4 billion for border barrier construction, $738 billion for military spending, and included a permanent repeal of three major health insurance taxes. The bill also provided $12.5 million each to the CDC and NIH for gun violence research and contained no new funding for international family planning help.
