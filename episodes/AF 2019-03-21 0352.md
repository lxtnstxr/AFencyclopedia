## Summary

Donald Trump recognized United States recognition of Israel's sovereignty over the Golan Heights, annexed from Syria. The Trump administration secured $38 billion in military aid for Israel, revoked all foreign aid to Palestine, and withdrew from the Iran nuclear deal. Israel undertook its largest expansion of settlements after a Trump request, and the United States maintained a military presence in Syria citing Israeli interests.
