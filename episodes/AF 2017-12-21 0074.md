## Summary

Donald Trump commuted the prison sentence of Sholom Rubashkin and moved the United States embassy in Israel from Tel Aviv to Jerusalem. The United Nations General Assembly passed a resolution condemning the United States for recognizing Jerusalem as the capital of Israel. Trump withdrew the United States from the Paris Climate Accords, the Trans-Pacific Partnership, and repealed the DACA program. The Dow Jones Industrial Average added 5,000 points in the year following Trump's election, and the United States experienced GDP growth over 3% for three consecutive quarters in 2017.
