## Summary

Donald Trump responded to Megyn Kelly's 2015 debate question about his comments toward women by stating "Only Rosie O'Donnell." Nicholas J. Fuentes argued Bernie Sanders's social behavior and Michael Bloomberg's lack of charisma were major electability flaws for the 2020 Democratic primary. Fuentes also asserted the historical and cultural superiority of Italians, citing the Roman Empire and the voyages of Christopher Columbus.
