## Summary

Dave Rubin hosts The Rubin Report on YouTube and released a video describing an "intellectual dark web" including figures like Ben Shapiro and Jordan Peterson. A CBS/YouGov poll showed Donald Trump's State of the Union address had a 97% approval rating among Republican Party viewers. The House Intelligence Committee voted to release a memo detailing possible FBI abuses of the FISA warrant system, which FBI Director Christopher Wray urged Donald Trump not to release.
