## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes reported 7,711 coronavirus infections and 170 deaths in China, with five confirmed cases across four U.S. states. The United States House of Representatives prepared to vote on repealing the 2002 Authorization for Use of Military Force and a separate measure from Representative Ro Khanna to block funds for military action against Iran. White House National Security Advisor Robert O'Brien cited the 2002 AUMF as legal authority for the strike that killed Iranian General Qasem Soleimani, while Secretary of Defense Mark Esper cited Article Two of the Constitution.
