## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes participated in a four-hour political debate on the "TrainwrecksTV" Twitch podcast against Destiny and Hasan Piker. During the debate, Destiny repeatedly called Nicholas J. Fuentes a "Nazi" and asked him if he believed in the Holocaust. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued that the fundamental issue of the Mueller Report was a media conspiracy and highlighted Hasan Piker's employment at The Young Turks through his uncle, Cenk Uygur.
