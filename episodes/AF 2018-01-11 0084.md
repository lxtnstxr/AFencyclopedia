## Summary

Donald Trump described Haiti, El Salvador, and African countries as "shithole countries" and suggested preferring immigrants from places like Norway. Senators Dick Durbin and Lindsey Graham proposed a bipartisan DACA bill offering a pathway to citizenship and allocating $2.7 billion for border security. Following the passage of tax cuts, Fiat Chrysler announced a $1 billion investment in Michigan, and Walmart raised its minimum wage to $11 per hour. U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement conducted workplace raids at 7-Eleven stores, rounding up 23 undocumented immigrants.
