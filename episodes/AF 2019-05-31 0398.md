## Summary

Russia censored gay and drug-related scenes from the Elton John biopic *Rocketman*, drawing condemnation from Elton John, the film's producers, and Amnesty International. Donald Trump announced escalating tariffs on Mexico, starting at 5%, to compel action on illegal immigration, a plan opposed by the National Association of Manufacturers and criticized by Mexican President Andrés Manuel López Obrador. Steve Bannon and Chris Kobach's "We Build the Wall" initiative constructed a half-mile of private border fencing in New Mexico at a cost of approximately $7 million.
