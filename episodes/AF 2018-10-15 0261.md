## Summary

Elizabeth Warren released a genetic test result indicating a potential Native American ancestor, which the Cherokee Nation declared useless for determining tribal citizenship. A judge dismissed Stormy Daniels's defamation lawsuit against Donald Trump, ruling it had no legal merit. Jamal Khashoggi, a Washington Post journalist, disappeared after entering the Saudi consulate in Turkey, prompting calls for a strong response from officials like John Bolton and Mike Pence. Saudi Arabia's foreign ministry warned it would retaliate against any punitive measures, citing its economic influence.
