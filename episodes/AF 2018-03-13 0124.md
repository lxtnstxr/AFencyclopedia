## Summary

Donald Trump campaigned for Rick Saccone at a Pennsylvania rally, emphasizing combating the opioid epidemic. Conor Lamb, a Democratic candidate, adopted a Trump-like platform on trade and opioids while avoiding immigration. The special election occurred in Pennsylvania's historically Republican 18th congressional district, which was 96 percent white. The winner of the election would lose the seat due to a Supreme Court-mandated redistricting in Pennsylvania.
