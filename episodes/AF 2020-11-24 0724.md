## Summary

The Trump campaign announced public hearings on election fraud in Pennsylvania, Michigan, and Arizona. Senator Cory Booker introduced a bill proposing USDA-funded land grants of up to 160 acres to black individuals, which an analysis by Red Elephants stated could redistribute 32 million acres over a decade. Senator Bob Menendez led a group urging YouTube to remove election misinformation videos, while YouTube temporarily suspended One America News Network for violating its COVID-19 misinformation policy.
