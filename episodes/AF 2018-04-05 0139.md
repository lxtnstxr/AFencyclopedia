## Summary

Benjamin Netanyahu accused George Soros and the New Israel Fund of endangering Israel's security and Jewish character. The U.S. Trade Department announced plans to impose tariffs on an additional $100 billion worth of Chinese goods. Nicholas J. Fuentes criticized commentators like Ben Shapiro and Ted Cruz for opposing Donald Trump's tariffs and announced a scheduled debate on gun control for the following Tuesday.
