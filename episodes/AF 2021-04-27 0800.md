## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes was prevented from boarding flights on American Airlines, United Airlines, and Southwest Airlines at Chicago airports. Fuentes filed a redress inquiry with the Department of Homeland Security after a TSA representative declined to confirm or deny his status on a federal no-fly list. Figures such as Michelle Malkin, Alex Jones, and Tucker Carlson publicly addressed aspects of travel restrictions or deplatforming affecting political commentators. Fuentes argued these actions represent a political persecution of dissidents by federal agencies and major technology companies.
