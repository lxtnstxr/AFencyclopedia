## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes hosts the political live stream *America First* on Wednesday evenings. Donald Trump instructed the U.S. Navy to shoot down Iranian gunboats following a harassment incident involving 11 Islamic Revolutionary Guard Corps Navy vessels. CDC Director Robert Redfield warned a second coronavirus wave coinciding with flu season could severely strain the healthcare system. New antibody studies in California suggested the coronavirus fatality rate might be as low as 0.1%.
