## Summary

The episode discussed the aftermath of a deadly incident involving civilians around a humanitarian aid convoy in Gaza, with conflicting reports attributing the deaths to Israeli gunfire or a crowd stampede. It also analyzed U.S. warnings and analyst predictions of a potential future Israeli military offensive against Hezbollah in Lebanon, contingent on the conclusion of its operation in Rafah. Furthermore, it covered Tucker Carlson's interviews with various international right-wing political figures, framing them as part of a broader geopolitical contest involving alliances with Israel and the U.S., countering left-wing governments, and securing critical resources like Argentina's lithium.
