## Summary

Steven Crowder launched the "Stop Big Con" initiative against conservative media and rejected a four-year, $50 million contract offer from The Daily Wire. The Daily Wire's Jeremy Boreing confirmed the contract details, while Ben Shapiro and Candace Owens criticized Crowder's rejection of the deal. Nicholas J. Fuentes claimed The Daily Wire has financial ties to the Wilks Brothers and makes content moderation agreements with platforms like YouTube. Fuentes also expressed solidarity with figures like Elijah Schaffer and supported Crowder's decision to refuse the contract.
