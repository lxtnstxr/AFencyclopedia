## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes hosted InfoWars writer Jake Lloyd, a former U.S. Army soldier from Texas. Lloyd cited Ann Coulter and Pat Buchanan as mainstream-acceptable figures whose ideas aligned with nationalist ideals. Both participants argued that immigration, not abortion, was the paramount political crisis facing the United States. They speculated that President Donald Trump's imminent Supreme Court nominee, likely Brett Kavanaugh or Amy Coney Barrett, would shape immigration policy for decades.
