## Summary

Mike Pence abruptly canceled a planned campaign event in New Hampshire without providing an explanation. Vladimir Putin conferred with his defense minister after 14 Russian sailors died in a submersible fire. Donald Trump gave an exclusive interview to Tucker Carlson in Japan, where he commented on Big Tech companies and urban decay.
