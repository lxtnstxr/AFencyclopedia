## Summary

Representative Paul Gosar of Arizona delivered a keynote address at the America First Political Action Conference, drawing criticism from Representative Liz Cheney and others within the Republican Party. MSNBC described the conference's host, Nicholas J. Fuentes, as a white nationalist, a characterization Fuentes denied while noting the event's diverse speakers. Former Ambassador Rick Grenell publicly debated figures like Lauren Witzke over LGBTQ inclusion in the Republican Party, which Nicholas J. Fuentes argued was an attempt to redirect the party's traditional social values.
