## Summary

Texas filed a lawsuit with the Supreme Court against Georgia, Michigan, Pennsylvania, and Wisconsin over their 2020 election procedures, joined by several other Republican-led states. The United States House of Representatives passed the National Defense Authorization Act without the Section 230 repeal requested by Donald Trump and with provisions obstructing troop withdrawals from Afghanistan. Nicholas J. Fuentes criticized Tim Pool for excluding him as a guest while hosting figures like Will Chamberlain and Ian Kaczynski, whom Fuentes derogatorily labeled. Fuentes argued the Republican Party betrayed its voters by supporting the NDAA and other legislation, advocating for consequences in primary elections.
