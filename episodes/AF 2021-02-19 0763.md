## Summary

The Russian Foreign Ministry condemned the United States government under Joe Biden for a persecution campaign against individuals disputing the latest presidential election results and criticized the FBI's handling of cases related to the January 6th Capitol events. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued the immigration plan served the interests of the Democratic Party and big business, while contrasting his America First conference with CPAC, which he stated Google funds and which banned Marjorie Taylor Greene.
