## Summary

Israel conducted major airstrikes in Syria after reporting an Iranian drone incursion, leading to a Syrian downing of an Israeli jet. Donald Trump proposed a $4.4 trillion 2018 budget with historic military spending, border wall funding, and cuts to entitlements. Barack and Michelle Obama unveiled official portraits painted by commissioned artists, which faced widespread criticism for their artistic style.
