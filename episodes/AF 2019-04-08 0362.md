## Summary

Kirstjen Nielsen resigned as Secretary of Homeland Security in April 2019, with Kevin McAleenan appointed as her acting replacement. Donald Trump pushed to reinstate a family separation policy at the border, a plan advocated by his aide Stephen Miller. Facebook and Instagram banned Faith Goldy under a policy targeting white nationalism following the Christchurch shooting. Cory Booker introduced a Senate bill to study reparations for descendants of slaves, a companion to legislation from Sheila Jackson Lee.
