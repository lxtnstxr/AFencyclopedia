## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes titled his show "LA Riots 2" following a police shooting in Los Angeles, where 75 to 100 protesters later assembled after a Black Lives Matter demonstration call. Donald Trump visited Kenosha, Wisconsin, to survey riot damage, a move Fuentes argued signaled a return to his 2016 campaign instincts and a move away from Jared Kushner's influence. Trump and his campaign accused commentators Matt Drudge and Joe Lockhart of spreading health rumors, which Trump denied on Twitter after Politico reported the allegations. A forthcoming book by New York Times reporter Michael Schmidt alleged Mike Pence was put on standby to assume presidential powers during an unplanned November hospital visit by Donald Trump.
