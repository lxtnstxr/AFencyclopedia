## Summary

The Democratic Party gained full control of the Virginia state government following the November elections. Governor Ralph Northam advocated for a semi-automatic weapons ban, declared a state of emergency ahead of a pro-gun rally, and threatened to deploy the National Guard to enforce proposed gun laws. Donald Trump signed a phase-one trade agreement with China, which included commitments on intellectual property and agricultural purchases. The West Virginia state Senate passed a bill affirming the right of Virginia counties to secede and join its state.
