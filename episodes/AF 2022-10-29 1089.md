## Summary

Ben Shapiro publicly advocated for COVID-19 vaccination in 2020 but later concluded the vaccine was useless for low-risk individuals. Nicholas J. Fuentes maintained that COVID-19 vaccine efficacy claims were unproven and linked the vaccine to sudden deaths, while also predicting advertiser pressure on Twitter after Elon Musk's acquisition. Elon Musk completed his acquisition of Twitter, fired its leadership, and proposed shifting the platform to a subscription-based model. Groups including the Anti-Defamation League previously pressured corporate advertisers to boycott social media platforms over content moderation policies.
