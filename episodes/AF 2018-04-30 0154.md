## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes spoke at an American Renaissance conference in Tennessee and terminated his show's relationship with Maker Support over withheld funds. Israel conducted a massive airstrike in Syria's Hama and Aleppo provinces, and Benjamin Netanyahu presented a dossier alleging Iran pursued a clandestine nuclear weapons program. The 1996 Clean Break Memo, authored by Richard Perle, Douglas Feith, and David Wurmser for Netanyahu, outlined a strategy for Israel to roll back Syria and Iran.
