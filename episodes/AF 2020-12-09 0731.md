## Summary

YouTube announced a policy to remove channels alleging fraud in the 2020 presidential election won by Joe Biden. Texas filed a lawsuit against Wisconsin, Michigan, Pennsylvania, and Georgia, alleging unconstitutional election processes, which the Supreme Court agreed to hear. Nicholas J. Fuentes planned rallies in Washington, D.C. and cited the Trump Accountability Project as an initiative cataloging Trump associates. Michigan State Representative Cynthia Johnson was stripped of committee assignments after posting a threatening video directed at Trump supporters.
