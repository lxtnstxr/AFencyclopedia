## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes criticized the Republican congressional leadership of Paul Ryan and Mitch McConnell for failing to deliver promised votes for border wall funding. Fuentes cited a Vice.com article by Will Nardi that presented data on women's searches for violent pornography and referenced a 2012 study from the University of North Texas and the University of Notre Dame on female sexual fantasies. He argued that societal messages against traditional gender roles cause hardwired desires to manifest perversely, advocating instead for sexuality within procreative marriage.
