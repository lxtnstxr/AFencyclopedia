## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes held a meetup in Laguna Beach, Orange County, organized by Vince James of Red Elephants. Fox News and Breitbart published articles labeling Fuentes a white nationalist and Holocaust denier, with Breitbart editor Joel Pollak pressuring Congressman Paul Gosar to disavow him. Fuentes argued that conservative media, including Breitbart under Pollak and the Daily Wire under Ben Shapiro, is dominated by Zionist perspectives. He also contended that figures like Steve Bannon promote economic nationalism to avoid addressing racial demographic concerns.
