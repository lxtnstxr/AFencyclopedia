## Summary

U.S. Customs and Border Protection reported a 79% annual increase in illegal border crossings for March 2021, with over 170,000 apprehensions. President Joe Biden announced executive actions to regulate "ghost guns" and pistol-stabilizing braces, nominating David Chipman to lead the ATF. Nicholas J. Fuentes returned to Twitter after a suspension and argued that Biden's gun measures would not have prevented recent mass shootings in Atlanta and Boulder. Fuentes also characterized the border situation as a historic crisis and stated that feminism is incompatible with a Christian life.
