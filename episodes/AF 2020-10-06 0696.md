## Summary

Donald Trump called off negotiations with the Democratic Party over a second coronavirus stimulus package in October 2020, then later challenged House Speaker Nancy Pelosi to send him standalone legislation for stimulus checks. Facebook announced a total ban on all QAnon-related content across its platform and Instagram. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued that government-mandated lockdowns caused the economic crash and that platforms like Facebook banned QAnon because its adherents engaged in independent research outside media gatekeeping.
