## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes recommended Samuel P. Huntington's book *Who Are We?* and advised viewers to prepare for instability by acquiring supplies and assets like gold or Bitcoin. Saudi Arabia intercepted a Houthi missile from Yemen, declared it an act of war by Iran, and subsequently blockaded Yemen's ports. The Prime Minister of Lebanon resigned citing Iranian influence and was reportedly held under house arrest in Saudi Arabia, which had urged its citizens to leave Lebanon. The New York Times published allegations of sexual misconduct by Louis C.K., and The Washington Post published allegations against Alabama Senate candidate Roy Moore.
