## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes traveled to New York to meet with local America First supporters and later appeared on Compound Media with host Anthony Cumia. Fuentes endorsed Arizona Senate candidate Blake Masters, who faced criticism for comments on gun crime, and contrasted Masters's approach with that of Washington candidate Joe Kent. Fuentes argued that racial outcome disparities in the United States stem from biological differences and that the movement inspired by Donald Trump must move further right on race and immigration.
