## Summary

Dickinson, Texas required Hurricane Harvey relief recipients to pledge not to boycott Israel. Texas enacted a law prohibiting state contracts with companies that boycott Israel, which the Council on American Islamic Relations legally challenged. A national anti-boycott law targeting Israel was considered for a federal spending bill in December. U.S. aid to Israel is provided as an interest-free loan at the start of the fiscal year, which is later forgiven.
