## Summary

Kanye West met with Donald Trump and Jim Brown at the White House, delivering a monologue in the Oval Office. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued the Democratic Party relies on receiving over 85% of the black vote to be competitive in states like Pennsylvania and Michigan. Fuentes contrasted Kanye West's MAGA support with Taylor Swift's endorsement of a Democrat opposing Marsha Blackburn in Tennessee. He also noted Donald Trump's first foreign visit was to Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, where he signed a $110 billion arms contract.
