## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes announced an upcoming appearance by Sticks Hexenhammer to discuss his gubernatorial bid. Mike Pompeo was confirmed as U.S. Secretary of State with votes from all Senate Republicans and Democrats Joe Manchin and Heidi Heitkamp. North Korean leader Kim Jong-un and South Korean President Moon Jae-in held their first summit in 11 years, setting the tone for a planned meeting with U.S. President Donald Trump. Media personalities Diamond and Silk received payments from congressional candidate Paul Nehlen, who later positioned himself as a leader of a white revolution.
