## Summary

Donald Trump endorsed Tommy Tuberville over Jeff Sessions in the Alabama Senate Republican primary runoff, which Tuberville won. Nicholas J. Fuentes criticized Trump for rescinding a rule on international student visas and for proposals on DACA citizenship. Fuentes cited statements from Nick Cannon and an Instagram sticker as examples of what he called the dehumanization of white people.
