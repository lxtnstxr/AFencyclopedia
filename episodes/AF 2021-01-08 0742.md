## Summary

Donald Trump was permanently banned from Twitter, Facebook, Instagram, Reddit, Twitch, and Snapchat in January following the Capitol siege. Twitter also purged thousands of accounts associated with QAnon and the siege, including those of Michael Flynn, Sidney Powell, and Lin Wood. Apple and Google removed the alternative platform Parler from their app stores, demanding stricter content moderation. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued that the Republican Party had failed to act against tech censorship despite previously controlling Congress, the White House, and the Supreme Court.
