## Summary

A jury found former Minneapolis police officer Derek Chauvin guilty on all charges related to the death of George Floyd on April 20, 2021. George Floyd died on May 25, 2020, after officers placed him on the ground for over nine minutes during an arrest. Defense attorney Eric Nelson argued the death resulted from drug use and pre-existing conditions, while prosecutors contended it was caused by asphyxiation. Following the verdict, public figures including Kamala Harris and Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez emphasized ongoing racial justice work.
