## Summary

Ron DeSantis made his second foreign trip as Florida governor, beginning in Israel before planned stops in South Korea, Japan, and the United Kingdom. During the trip, DeSantis signed legislation in Israel prohibiting certain political activities and outlawing anti-Semitism in Florida, citing Anti-Defamation League data on anti-Semitic incidents in the state. Elon Musk's ownership of Twitter saw the reinstatement of many previously banned accounts, followed by permanent bans of figures including Andrew Anglin, Kevin MacDonald, and Nicholas J. Fuentes, who was banned a day after reinstatement. Miriam Adelson, an early backer of Ron DeSantis's gubernatorial campaign and a major donor to Donald Trump's campaigns, received the Presidential Medal of Freedom from Trump in 2018.
