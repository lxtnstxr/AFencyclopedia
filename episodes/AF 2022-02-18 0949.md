## Summary

The FBI appointed a new task force and created a Virtual Asset Exploitation Unit targeting cryptocurrency-related crime. The Government of Canada invoked the Emergencies Act against the Freedom Convoy, arresting leaders Tamara Lich and Chris Barber while freezing participant bank accounts. Nicholas J. Fuentes testified before the January 6th Committee, invoking the Fifth Amendment, and announced speakers including Wendy Rogers, Joe Arpaio, and Kari Lake for the AFPAC 3 conference.
