## Summary

A terrorist attack at a military parade in Iran killed 29 people. Christine Blasey Ford alleged Brett Kavanaugh assaulted her at a 1982 party, but named witnesses Leland Keyser and Mark Judge did not corroborate her account. The New York Times reported it found no corroboration for a separate allegation by Deborah Ramirez against Kavanaugh in The New Yorker. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued the allegations were intended to stall Kavanaugh's confirmation to preserve Roe v. Wade.
