## Summary

The Commission on Presidential Debates announced a virtual format for the second presidential debate between Donald Trump and Joe Biden. Donald Trump refused to participate in the virtual debate and instead planned a rally, while Joe Biden scheduled a town hall on ABC. The FBI uncovered a plot by anarchists and far-right militias to kidnap Michigan Governor Gretchen Whitmer, leading to federal charges against multiple individuals. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued the timing of the plot's announcement was politically convenient and appeared on InfoWars for an interview with Alex Jones.
