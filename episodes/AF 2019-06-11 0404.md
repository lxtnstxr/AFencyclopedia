## Summary

Pinterest suppressed content related to Ben Shapiro and pro-life topics according to a Project Veritas report. Will Chamberlain purchased Human Events and blacklisted Nicholas J. Fuentes, whom he has publicly debated. Over 180 corporate executives signed a letter stating that abortion access is essential for business. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued that Republican leaders are impediments to Donald Trump's immigration and trade reform.
