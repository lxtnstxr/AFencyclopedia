## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes directed viewers to a Timcast IRL stream featuring a debate between Charlie Kirk and Vaush and encouraged a coordinated spam campaign in its live chat. Fuentes cited Lindsey Graham's COVID-19 case, an outbreak on a fully vaccinated U.K. ship, and FDA warnings about the Johnson & Johnson vaccine to argue against COVID-19 vaccine efficacy and safety. He referenced figures including Dr. Brett Weinstein and Dr. Robert Malone to question vaccine mandates while noting a 1904 Supreme Court case established precedent for school vaccine requirements. Fuentes also discussed critical race theory, citing Christopher F. Rufo's tactical use of the term and the National Education Association's advocacy, before arguing against race-based policies in favor of class-based programs.
