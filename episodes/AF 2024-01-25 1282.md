## Summary

The Supreme Court ruled that Texas must allow U.S. Border Patrol agents access to a park along the Rio Grande, but Texas state officials refused to comply, leading to a standoff supported by several other states. Concurrently, Donald Trump's victory in the New Hampshire Republican primary over Nikki Haley was analyzed, noting the withdrawal of major donor Reid Hoffman and Trump's warning to her remaining supporters. The discussion also covered international right-wing populist figures, including Tucker Carlson's interviews with Javier Milei and Viktor Orbán, and policy contrasts between the Biden and Trump administrations on immigration.
