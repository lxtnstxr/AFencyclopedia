## Summary

Robert F. Kennedy Jr. launched a 2024 Democratic presidential primary campaign in Boston, Massachusetts, challenging Joe Biden. The United States Congress considered legislation to grant the Biden administration power to ban TikTok over national security concerns related to its parent company ByteDance. British regulators fined TikTok 12.7 million pounds for failing to remove underage users and improperly using their data. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued the United States provides Israel $3.8 billion in annual direct foreign aid.
