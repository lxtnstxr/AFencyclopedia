## Summary

Dan Crenshaw held university events at Texas A&M and the University of Texas at Austin in early November, where he faced questions on U.S. laws regarding Israel and affirmative action. Turning Point USA scheduled events featuring Charlie Kirk and Donald Trump Jr. at the University of California, Los Angeles, while internal strategies included smearing opponents and distributing a blacklist. Nicholas J. Fuentes advocated for disruptive protest tactics at such events and made doctrinal claims about the superiority of the Catholic Church over the Eastern Orthodox Church.
