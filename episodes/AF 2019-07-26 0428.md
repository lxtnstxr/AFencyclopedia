## Summary

The United States Supreme Court authorized the use of $2.5 billion in Pentagon funds for border barrier construction in Arizona, New Mexico, and California. Donald Trump and Guatemala's president agreed to a "third safe country" asylum rule requiring migrants to apply in Guatemala first. Lawrence James Davis, a suspect with a violent history, killed Tyler Wingate following a minor car collision in Detroit.

(00:01:02) The [[United States]] Supreme Court greenlit the use of $2.5 billion in [[United States Department of Defense|Defense Department]] funds for the construction of replacement border barriers in [[Arizona]], [[New Mexico]], and [[California]].

(00:01:32) [[Donald Trump]] and the President of [[Guatemala]] reached a "third safe country" agreement requiring migrants passing through [[Guatemala]] to seek asylum there before applying in the [[United States]].
