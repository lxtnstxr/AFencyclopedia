## Summary

Justin Trudeau wore blackface on multiple occasions, including instances where he dressed as Aladdin and mocked a Jamaican singer. Antifa members firebombed an ICE facility in Spokane, and left-wing protesters planned demonstrations at the private residences of ICE officials. A series of mob attacks in downtown Minneapolis involved Somali assailants targeting white victims, leading to 16 arrests and a police request for 400 additional officers. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued that demographic shifts, rather than solely political policy, explained rising crime in Minneapolis and cited Canada's projected demographic changes.
