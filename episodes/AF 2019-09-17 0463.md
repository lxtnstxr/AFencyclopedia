## Summary

Donald Trump considered a retaliatory strike on Iran with Saudi Arabian execution after an attack on Saudi oil facilities. Benjamin Netanyahu's Likud party failed to secure a majority in Israel's election, leaving coalition negotiations uncertain. An NBC poll showed Joe Biden leading the Democratic presidential primary with 31% support, followed by Elizabeth Warren at 25%. United States officials, including Mike Pompeo, blamed Iran for the Saudi Arabia attack, though Houthi rebels in Yemen claimed responsibility.
