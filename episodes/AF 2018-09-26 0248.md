## Summary

Multiple new allegations of misconduct arose against Brett Kavanaugh during his Supreme Court confirmation process, with attorney Michael Avenatti representing one accuser, Julie Swetnick. Christine Blasey Ford testified before the Senate Judiciary Committee about her allegation against Kavanaugh, having previously taken a polygraph test administered by a former FBI agent. The U.S. Congress passed an $853 billion spending bill to fund several government departments through December, which President Donald Trump agreed to sign despite its lack of border wall funding.
