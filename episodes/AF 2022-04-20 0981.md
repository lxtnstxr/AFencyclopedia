## Summary

Ron DeSantis passed a bill through the Florida State Legislature to repeal special zoning laws for Disney World. Nicholas J. Fuentes announced a debate on the Cozy.tv platform and later argued that the United States maintains its relationship with Taiwan to check Chinese power in the Southeast Pacific.
