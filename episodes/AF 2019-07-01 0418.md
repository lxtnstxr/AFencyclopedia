## Summary

Donald Trump became the first sitting U.S. president to enter North Korea during a surprise summit with Kim Jong-un at the Korean Demilitarized Zone. A CNN poll showed Joe Biden leading the 2019 Democratic primary field with 22% support, followed by Kamala Harris and Elizabeth Warren. Antifa attacked journalist Andy Ngo during a Portland rally, with police reporting projectiles containing quick-dry cement.
