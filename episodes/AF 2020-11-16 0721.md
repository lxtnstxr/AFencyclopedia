## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes organized and spoke at the Million MAGA March in Washington, D.C., which drew an estimated 500,000 Trump supporters to Freedom Plaza. Fuentes announced plans for rallies at the Georgia state capitol in Atlanta to protest the state's election audit process, which he claimed was insufficient and corrupt. During the events, Fuentes and attendees reported confrontations with Antifa, criticized the police response at Harry's Bar, and led chants of "Christ is King" and "America First."
