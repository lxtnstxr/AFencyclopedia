## Summary

Donald Trump commemorated nearly 300 miles of border wall construction in Arizona, with his administration having cut legal immigration in half and allocated funds for over 700 total miles. Joe Biden's campaign platform included revoking Trump's border emergency declaration, ending the "Remain in Mexico" policy, and creating a pathway to citizenship for millions. The 2020 Democratic National Convention averaged 21.6 million viewers per night, a 17.6% decline from 2016, and an analysis found women and people of color accounted for more than half of its speaking time.
