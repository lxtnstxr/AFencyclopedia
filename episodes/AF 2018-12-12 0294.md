## Summary

A shooting at a Christmas market in Strasbourg, France killed two people and injured 13, with the gunman Chérif Chekatt later identified as the perpetrator. Columbia University student Julian Von Abele was doxed and received death threats after a viral video showed him making racially charged statements to fellow students. Nancy Pelosi secured a deal with the Democratic caucus to become Speaker of the United States House of Representatives for a second time. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued that Israel serves as a national refuge for Jewish people, which he contrasted with the lack of a similar homeland for white populations in Western nations.
