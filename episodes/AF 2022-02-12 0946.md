## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes announced the AFPAC 3 conference for February 25th in Orlando, Florida, with confirmed speakers including Stu Peters, Michelle Malkin, and Gavin McInnis. The United States Department of Homeland Security urged Canadian officials to end trucker protests that closed ports of entry, including the Ambassador Bridge between Windsor, Ontario and Detroit, Michigan. The United States deployed additional troops to Poland and Germany while stating Russia had amassed significant forces near Ukraine, a nation whose strategic alignment Russia viewed as a critical national security issue. Nicholas J. Fuentes characterized the United States government as an interventionist regime whose eroding moral authority could lead to geopolitical realignments favoring China and Russia.
