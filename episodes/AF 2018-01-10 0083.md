## Summary

Donald Trump rescinded the DACA program, setting a March 5th expiration date for its protections. The White House designated a House Judiciary Committee bill, proposed by Bob Goodlatte, as a starting point for immigration negotiations, which included $30 billion for border security and an end to chain migration. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued Trump used DACA recipients as leverage to secure a border wall and other concessions. Fuentes also announced a scheduled debate with commentator Jacob Wohl on Israel policy.
