## Summary

New Mexico Governor Michelle Lujan Grisham signed legislation replacing Columbus Day with Indigenous Peoples' Day. Brunei introduced a strict new Sharia penal code, prompting Ellen DeGeneres and George Clooney to boycott hotels owned by its Sultan. Caitlin Caruso and D.J. Hill accused Joe Biden of inappropriate touching, leading to a video statement from Biden. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued the accusations were fitting given Democratic standards and posited the 2020 Democratic primary would reveal significant internal dysfunction.
