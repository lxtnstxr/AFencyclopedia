## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes hosts the online show *America First* and initiated a campaign termed the "Groyper War" against Turning Point USA and Charlie Kirk in late October 2019. Turning Point USA canceled the Q&A portion of a UCLA book talk featuring Donald Trump Jr. and Kimberly Guilfoyle, which Fuentes claimed was a protest against Kirk. The *Guardian* described the event as revealing a factional rift on the Trump-supporting right and identified Fuentes's supporters as believing the Trump administration was subverted by a cabal of internationalists. Figures like Paul Gosar and Jim Banks tweeted statements Fuentes interpreted as supportive, while Michelle Malkin defended him against critics like Dan Crenshaw and Ben Shapiro.
