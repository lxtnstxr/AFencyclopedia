## Summary

Australia's national government implemented a mandatory QR code system linked to a vaccination registry for entry into public places, and its military was later deployed to enforce it. The FBI and DOJ found scant evidence of a White House or Stop the Steal conspiracy to breach the U.S. Capitol on January 6th, concluding only isolated groups within the Proud Boys and Oath Keepers had planned to enter. InfoWars host Owen Shroyer was charged with misdemeanors for his conduct at the Capitol, and Nicholas J. Fuentes characterized those charges as political persecution. Donald Trump advocated for vaccination at a rally and used the phrase "everything woke turns to shit" in Alabama.
