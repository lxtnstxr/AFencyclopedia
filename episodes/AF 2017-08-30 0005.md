## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes cited Pat Buchanan, Samuel Francis, and Thomas Hobbes among his political influences while asserting that women are generally unsuited for politics or high-level business. Fuentes reported that Google threatened The Liberty Conservative with demonetization over an article by James Alsup, which Fuentes characterized as an analysis differentiating the alt-right from Nazis. He criticized Paul Ryan for condemning neo-Nazis after the Charlottesville rally and contrasted this with Nancy Pelosi's condemnation of Antifa violence in Berkeley. Fuentes also claimed the Charlottesville police ignored a Department of Homeland Security warning and exacerbated violence by corralling protesters.
