Fuentes]] recounted entering a United States Post Office without a mask, refusing repeated demands from a postal worker to wear one, and stating "it's okay" before being threatened with a police call, which he dismissed as a bluff before completing his transaction and leaving.

(01:42:48) [[Philadelphia]] imposed a citywide curfew from 9 p.m. to 6 a.m. following two nights of riots and looting that resulted in 81 arrests, 297 reports of looting, 23 injured officers, and widespread vandalism, sparked by the police shooting of [[Walter Wallace Jr.]].

(01:50:20) [[Walter Wallace Jr.]], whose shooting by police sparked riots in [[Philadelphia]], was an aspiring rapper with a criminal record including a guilty plea for assaulting a police officer in 2013 and a 2017 guilty plea for robbery, assault, and putting a gun to a woman's head after kicking down her door; he was awaiting trial for allegedly threatening to shoot up another woman's house at the time of his death.
