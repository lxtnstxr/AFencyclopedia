## Summary

The Democratic National Convention's third night featured speeches from Barack Obama, Hillary Clinton, and Nancy Pelosi, and included performances by Jennifer Hudson and Billie Eilish. Speakers promoted Joe Biden's record and plans on climate action, gun violence legislation, and the Violence Against Women Act, while criticizing Donald Trump's policies on immigration and the environment. Hillary Clinton endorsed Joe Biden and Kamala Harris, warning that Trump's reelection would worsen conditions, and Nancy Pelosi accused Trump and Mitch McConnell of obstructing Democratic legislation.
