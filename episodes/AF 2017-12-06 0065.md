## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes and Millennial Matt debated controversies involving female commentators Lauren Southern and Tara McCarthy within their movement. Millennial Matt argued attacks on these women were detrimental, while Nicholas J. Fuentes contended the core issue was their violation of a culture prioritizing humor and traditionalism over feminism. Both agreed women were important to the movement but needed resilience against criticism, with Fuentes rejecting McCarthy's demand to cease critiques of feminism.
