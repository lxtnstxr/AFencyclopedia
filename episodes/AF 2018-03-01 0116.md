## Summary

Donald Trump planned tariffs of 25% on steel and 10% on aluminum based on a United States Commerce Department national security assessment. Vladimir Putin announced new Russian weapons systems including a hypersonic missile he stated could penetrate any defense shield. Angela Merkel acknowledged the existence of "no-go zones" in Germany and other European countries with majority-Muslim populations. The European Union, led by Jean-Claude Juncker, threatened a retaliatory trade war against Trump's proposed tariffs.
