## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes argued that Albert Einstein's theory of relativity and Werner Heisenberg's uncertainty principle shifted Western consciousness away from philosophical realism. Fuentes claimed immigration from Central America, South America, and Africa dilutes the United States' gene pool and cited Charles Murray's *Coming Apart* on social segregation by IQ. He contended the 2017 Alabama Senate election was illegitimate and viewed the Georgia and Montana special elections as more informative for the 2018 midterms. Fuentes proposed an "Anglo alliance" between former British Empire countries and identified Ben Shapiro as America's top conservative commentator due to his media machinery.
