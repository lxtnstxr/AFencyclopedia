## Summary

A mass shooting at a hospital in Tulsa, Oklahoma, resulted in four deaths. Joe Biden delivered a prime-time address calling for a ban on assault-style weapons and for the United States House of Representatives to pass gun control legislation. Nicholas J. Fuentes alleged federal agencies like the FBI had prior knowledge of some shootings and claimed most gun violence stems from black individuals and gang activity. Fuentes also identified right-wing personality Bronze Age Pervert as Costin Alamariu and linked him to mentor David Sidorsky of the Jewish Institute for National Security Affairs.
