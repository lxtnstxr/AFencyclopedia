## Summary

The DarkSide cybercrime group's ransomware attack shut down the Colonial Pipeline, causing gasoline shortages across the southeastern United States. Nicholas J. Fuentes described the Israeli-Palestinian conflict as rooted in competing territorial claims following the British Mandate and Israel's 1948 declaration of independence. Fuentes argued the United States has no strategic interest in the conflict and that support for Israel is driven by political donations from lobbies like AIPAC. He also characterized the World Economic Forum, led by Klaus Schwab, as a gathering of global elites planning a "Great Reset" agenda.
