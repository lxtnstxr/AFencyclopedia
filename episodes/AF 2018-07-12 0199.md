## Summary

Donald Trump secured a NATO agreement for increased defense spending after a summit in Brussels and later criticized Theresa May's Brexit strategy during a visit to the United Kingdom. The Trump administration proposed $200 billion in tariffs on Chinese goods, targeting a manufacturing sector that employed 67% of China's workforce. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued demographic changes in cities like Los Angeles resembled population replacement and criticized groups like the Proud Boys while dismissing QAnon as a fringe belief.
