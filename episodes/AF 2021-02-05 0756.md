## Summary

A Time Magazine article described a coordinated 2020 election effort by institutions like the U.S. Chamber of Commerce and the AFL-CIO, which it characterized as "fortifying democracy." The Virginia House of Delegates passed a reparations bill requiring five pre-1865 public universities to offer scholarships to descendants of slaves who labored there. Nicholas J. Fuentes claimed Mark Zuckerberg directed $350 million into get-out-the-vote efforts in Democratic areas during the 2020 election.
