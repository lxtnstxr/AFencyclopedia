## Summary

The statue of Robert E. Lee that was a focal point of the 2017 Unite the Right rally in Charlottesville, Virginia, was melted down in a private ceremony, with its bronze intended for a new, inclusive monument. The episode also covered a proposed U.S. aid package to Israel, contrasting political figures' stances on unconditional support, and analyzed the long-term impact of the Charlottesville rally on white nationalist activism and media discourse. Figures discussed included Nicholas J. Fuentes, who detailed his involvement in the rally and his subsequent activism, as well as various political commentators and officials.
