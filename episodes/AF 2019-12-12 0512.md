## Summary

The Conservative Party won a historic majority in the United Kingdom general election, capturing Blythe Valley from the Labour Party. Donald Trump's administration signed an executive order reinterpreting Judaism as a nationality, and Mark Levin declared Trump "the first Jewish president." Time magazine named Greta Thunberg its Person of the Year and Lizzo its Entertainer of the Year for 2019. The United States and China moved close to signing a trade deal, which Jennifer Hillman of the Council on Foreign Relations criticized as insubstantial.
