## Summary

Donald Trump's 2024 campaign plan involves reimplementing a Schedule F executive order to reclassify and potentially fire tens of thousands of federal civil servants, replacing them with political loyalists to transform agencies like the Pentagon and the State Department. Nicholas J. Fuentes hosts the America First show and cited sources confirming this administrative purge plan, while also criticizing Reince Priebus's role in Trump's first-term staffing. Fuentes connected global populist movements led by figures like Andrés Manuel López Obrador and Jair Bolsonaro to a reaction against Western globalization. The Los Angeles Times and U.S. State Department reported a significant influx of U.S. citizens into Mexico City, increasing rents and displacing local residents.
