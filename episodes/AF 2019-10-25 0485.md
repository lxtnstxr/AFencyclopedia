## Summary

Donald Trump faced a growing bloc of newly naturalized, liberal-leaning immigrant voters in states like Texas, Arizona, and Georgia. U.S. District Judge Keith Ellison presided over a naturalization ceremony for 2,155 immigrants and encouraged them to register to vote. The Syrian Democratic Forces alleged Turkey was continuing a genocide in northern Syria despite a permanent ceasefire. Figures including Nicholas J. Fuentes, Faith Goldy, and Laura Loomer were banned from the previous year's CPAC, and Ashley St. Clair was fired from Turning Point USA for associating with such figures.
