## Summary

Chelsea Manning attempted suicide and later ran for the United States Senate on a platform of eliminating borders and instituting a universal basic income. Ben Shapiro responded to Manning's suicide attempt by calling her a "precious creature of God" and separately advocated for ruining the lives of people deemed "real racists." Donald Trump defended attendees of the Charlottesville rally, stating there were "very fine people on both sides." Figures within the alt-right, such as Chris Cantwell and Richard Spencer, have argued for using abortion for eugenic purposes.
