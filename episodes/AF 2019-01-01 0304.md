## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes returned to hosting in early 2019 and declared a focus on opposing figures like Ben Shapiro. Donald Trump and Jair Bolsonaro exchanged messages on Twitter and were scheduled to meet at the White House later that year. A government shutdown that began in December 2018 entered its twelfth day with a Senate bill containing $5.6 billion for border wall funding. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued Donald Trump's reelection path depended on securing wall funding, while criticizing Trump's communications and his unfulfilled promises regarding troop withdrawals from Syria and Afghanistan.
