## Summary

Donald Trump threatened to close parts of the U.S.-Mexico border, citing record apprehensions of Central American families and Congressional inaction on asylum laws. The Department of Homeland Security raised the H-2B visa cap by 30,000, a program expanded under Trump that allowed more foreign workers for non-agricultural jobs. Amy Lappos accused Joe Biden, the Democratic front-runner for the 2020 election, of inappropriate touching at a 2009 fundraiser.
