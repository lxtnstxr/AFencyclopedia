## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes advocated for state regulation of communication platforms to mandate all legal speech and proposed government enforcement of aesthetic and cultural standards. Lucian Wintrich stated that The Gateway Pundit faced significant reduction on Facebook and expressed fear that independent publications would fail due to actions by social justice activists within tech companies. Fuentes identified the Immigration and Nationality Act of 1965 as pivotal for changing America's demographic composition and argued that ethnic diversity lowers social trust. Wintrich cited Minnesota as a case where Somali refugee importation led to child rape gangs and increased welfare dependency.
