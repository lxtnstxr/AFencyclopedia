## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes announced coverage of the Republican Party's Speaker of the House nomination following the 2022 midterm elections. Marjorie Taylor Greene endorsed Kevin McCarthy for Speaker, while Matt Gaetz and members of the House Freedom Caucus opposed him. Fuentes declared support for Dave Chappelle, Kanye West, Candace Owens, and Kyrie Irving amid criticism of Chappelle's *Saturday Night Live* monologue from Jonathan Greenblatt and others. He argued the Republican establishment, including Kevin McCarthy and Mitch McConnell, had consistently betrayed the MAGA movement.
