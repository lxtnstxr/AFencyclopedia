## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes argued the El Paso shooting narrative was fabricated and cited Operation Northwoods and MKUltra as evidence of government-covert events. Jim Watkins, owner of 8chan, stated the shooter's alleged manifesto was uploaded by a different user and that the shooter had posted on Instagram instead. The United States Treasury Department labeled China a currency manipulator after China devalued its currency, following Donald Trump's announcement of new tariffs. Mexico threatened legal action against the United States after the El Paso shooting, with Foreign Minister Marcelo Ebrard calling it a terrorist act against Mexican citizens.
