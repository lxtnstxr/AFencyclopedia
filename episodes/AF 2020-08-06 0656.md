## Summary

New York Attorney General Letitia James filed a lawsuit seeking to dissolve the National Rifle Association and redistribute its assets. The Obama administration's HUD implemented a 2015 housing rule requiring extensive disparity surveys, which the Trump administration repealed in July 2020. A massive explosion in Beirut, Lebanon, was later assessed as one of the largest non-nuclear blasts in history. Joe Biden's 2020 campaign platform included proposals to ban assault weapons and repeal the Protection of Lawful Commerce in Arms Act.
