## Summary

The Supreme Court blocked Donald Trump's 2017 rescission of the Deferred Action for Childhood Arrivals program, a policy established by Barack Obama that protected approximately 700,000 individuals. The domain registrar Network Solutions moved to deplatform the website VDARE, an action previously taken against The Daily Stormer following the Charlottesville rally. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued that Donald Trump's executive order on tech censorship would expand deplatforming, following PayPal's termination of his account and Google's demonetization of The Federalist and Zero Hedge.
