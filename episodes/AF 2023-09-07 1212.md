## Summary

The episode discussed legal efforts in several states to disqualify former President Donald Trump from the 2024 presidential ballot using the Fourteenth Amendment's Disqualification Clause, citing the January 6th Capitol riot. It also covered the U.S. Department of Justice's lawsuit against Texas, which successfully obtained a court order to remove a state-installed buoy barrier from the Rio Grande on the grounds it violated federal law and posed safety risks. Additional topics included criticism of the Anti-Defamation League and claims of selective law enforcement regarding January 6th prosecutions versus Black Lives Matter protests.
