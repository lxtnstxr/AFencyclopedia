## Summary

Robert Bowers killed 11 people at the Tree of Life synagogue in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. Jair Bolsonaro won the Brazilian presidency by a 10-point margin, aided by his dominance of Facebook and WhatsApp. Donald Trump proposed sending 800 active-duty troops to the U.S.-Mexico border, and the Pentagon later announced plans for up to 14,000 personnel. Trump had imposed tariffs on $250 billion of Chinese goods and considered tariffs on an additional $257 billion.
