## Summary

Amazon removed books by Kevin MacDonald and Jared Taylor from its platform. Andrew Yang launched a 2020 Democratic presidential campaign centered on a universal basic income and "human-centered capitalism." Nicholas J. Fuentes argued demographic changes would make Texas and Florida reliably Democratic and that border security had worsened under the Trump administration.
