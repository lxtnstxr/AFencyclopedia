## Summary

Donald Trump delivered a State of the Union address that moderated his border wall rhetoric and explicitly called for increased legal immigration, which he affirmed was a policy change. Virginia Governor Ralph Northam faced scandals involving a yearbook photo showing blackface or a Klan robe and his support for late-term abortion legislation. Commentators Ann Coulter and Scott Greer criticized the address, while Ben Shapiro and Charlie Kirk praised it as unifying.
