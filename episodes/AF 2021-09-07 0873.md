## Summary

The Supreme Court of the United States declined to intervene against a Texas state law that banned abortion after six weeks of pregnancy. The Satanic Temple filed a lawsuit challenging the law's constitutionality under the First Amendment and the Religious Freedom Restoration Act. More than a thousand rabbis associated with Rabbis for Repro publicly advocated for abortion rights in opposition to the Texas statute. Rutgers University implemented a policy banning an unvaccinated student from accessing online classes, despite his remote location.
