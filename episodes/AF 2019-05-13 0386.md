## Summary

Viktor Orbán, the Prime Minister of Hungary, held a meeting with Donald Trump at the White House. Donald Trump raised tariffs to 25% on $200 billion worth of Chinese imports after China reneged on a trade deal, prompting retaliatory tariffs from China. Nicholas J. Fuentes described Hungary under Viktor Orbán as a prototype for nationalist ideals and contrasted Orbán's systemic policies with Trump's rhetoric.
