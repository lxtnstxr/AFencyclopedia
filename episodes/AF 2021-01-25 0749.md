## Summary

The New York Times reported a doubling of teen and child suicides in Clark County, Nevada, attributed to COVID-19 lockdowns. An improvised explosive device bombed the First Works Baptist Church in El Monte, California, which was previously targeted for its opposition to homosexuality and Black Lives Matter. Nicholas J. Fuentes hosts the nightly political commentary program *America First* and announced it would air exclusively on americafirst.live. Brandon Straka was arrested and charged in connection with the January 6th Capitol siege.
