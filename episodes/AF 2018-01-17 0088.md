## Summary

Donald Trump rescheduled his announced "Fake News Awards" in January 2018 after a linked website failed to function. South Korea and North Korea agreed to march under a unified flag at the 2018 Winter Olympics, a move credited to Trump's diplomatic pressure. The Trump administration renegotiated the U.S. security alliance with Saudi Arabia, securing a $300 billion arms deal. The United States faced a government shutdown threat in January 2018 as funding was set to expire.
