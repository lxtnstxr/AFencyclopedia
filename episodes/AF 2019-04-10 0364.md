## Summary

Benjamin Netanyahu formed a coalition government after his Likud party secured a majority in the Israeli election. Donald Trump recognized Israeli sovereignty over the Golan Heights and designated Iran's Islamic Revolutionary Guard Corps as a terrorist organization. The U.S. Department of Defense awarded $976 million in contracts to build sections of a border barrier, including a $789 million contract to SLSCO Ltd.
