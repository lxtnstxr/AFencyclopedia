## Summary

A U.S. intelligence report alleged Russia, China, and Iran interfered in the 2020 presidential election, with Russia aiding Donald Trump and China and Iran aiding Joe Biden. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued that domestic media conglomerates and tech companies exerted more election influence than foreign interference. Fuentes also characterized the U.S. political system as an occupied regime run by a transnational class foreign to American interests.
