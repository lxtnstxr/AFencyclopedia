## Summary

Michael Cranston shot and killed Barry Washington in Oregon, with District Attorney John Hummel comparing the incident to the lynching of Emmett Till. The Cook County State's Attorney's Office dropped all charges against five suspects in a Chicago gang shootout, citing insufficient evidence after police had sought murder charges. Nicholas J. Fuentes announced rallies in Illinois and New York, a new streaming platform, and criticized diversity initiatives by Twitch and the Nasdaq. During a 2016 presidential debate, Hillary Clinton cited Donald Trump's comments about Judge Gonzalo Curiel and the Khan family as evidence of his unfitness for office.
