## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes announced a decisive victory for his movement at a Turning Point USA event featuring Charlie Kirk and Graham Allen at the University of Florida. Fuentes reported that his supporters constituted more than half the audience and that Charlie Kirk conceded error on his past support for EB-5 visas during the event's Q&A. The final stop of Charlie Kirk's tour was scheduled for North Carolina State University with Lara Trump in attendance.
