## Summary

Julian Assange's arrest followed his publication of Democratic National Committee emails through WikiLeaks, an act widely considered to have influenced the 2016 U.S. presidential election. The New York Times reported a severe crisis within the U.S. immigration system, where a backlog of over 800,000 cases existed. Nicholas J. Fuentes advocated for Chris Kobach, a Harvard, Oxford, and Yale graduate who helped create the NSEERS system, to be appointed to lead the Department of Homeland Security.
