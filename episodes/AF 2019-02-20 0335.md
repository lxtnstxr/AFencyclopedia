## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes described the Chicago Police Department's confirmation that Jussie Smollett orchestrated a hoax attack on himself. Hoda Muthana and Shamima Begum traveled to join ISIS in 2014 and later sought to return to the United States and United Kingdom, respectively. Ilhan Omar faced bipartisan criticism for her tweets stating the American Israel Public Affairs Committee funneled money into Congress to ensure support for Israel.
