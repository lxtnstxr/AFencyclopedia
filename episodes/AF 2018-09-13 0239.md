## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes announced a speech at the Rose-Hulman Institute of Technology, whose president later condemned him as a white supremacist. The European Parliament voted to trigger Article 7 proceedings against Hungary for alleged breaches of EU values, a measure opposed by some conservative MEPs. Viktor Orbán's Fidesz party was accused by international monitors of using bogus opposition parties in the 2018 national elections. Over-pressurization by the Columbia Gas Company caused a series of explosions and fires in Lawrence, Massachusetts.
