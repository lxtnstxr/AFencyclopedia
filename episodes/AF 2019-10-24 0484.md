## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes reported that Charlie Kirk conducted a Turning Point USA campus tour at the University of New Hampshire, Colorado State University, and the University of Iowa. Fuentes stated that Kirk supported Zionism as ethnic nationalism for Jews but condemned it for Europeans, and that Kirk declined a debate with Fuentes after a question at the University of New Hampshire. Judge Kim Cooks of a Texas family court ruled that both Jeff Younger and Anne Georgulas must consent to any medical transition procedures for their child, James Younger. Fuentes argued that a "deep state" of intelligence agencies and bureaucrats subverted the policies of elected officials like Barack Obama and Donald Trump.
