## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes reported on the uncalled 2020 presidential election, focusing on diminishing leads for Donald Trump in Pennsylvania and Georgia and a narrowing lead for Joe Biden in Arizona. Fuentes cited pending lawsuits in Pennsylvania and potential recounts in Georgia as elements of the Trump campaign's legal strategy. He also referenced a Pennsylvania court order granting Republican Party observers access to tabulation rooms and a Department of Justice approval for armed federal agents to investigate potential voter fraud.
