## Summary

Facebook and Instagram removed accounts belonging to Laura Loomer, Alex Jones, Milo Yiannopoulos, and Paul Joseph Watson. The Federal Trade Commission initiated probes into Facebook and Amazon, while the United States Department of Justice prepared potential antitrust cases against Google and Apple. Dewayne Craddock, a city employee, killed 12 people in a shooting at the Virginia Beach municipal center. During a state visit to the United Kingdom, Donald Trump met with the British Royal Family while London Mayor Sadiq Khan led large protests against him.
