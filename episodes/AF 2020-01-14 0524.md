## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes noted the January 14, 2020 Democratic debate featured seven candidates, including Joe Biden, Bernie Sanders, Elizabeth Warren, and Amy Klobuchar. Bernie Sanders argued the wars in Vietnam and Iraq were based on lies and highlighted his War Powers resolution on Yemen, which Congress passed but was vetoed. Joe Biden defended his 2002 Iraq War vote as a mistake but emphasized his role in executing the troop withdrawal ordered by Barack Obama. Elizabeth Warren called for an end to the defense industry's "revolving door" with the Pentagon and supported the USMCA trade agreement as a modest improvement.
