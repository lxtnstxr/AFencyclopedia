## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes conducted a casual Friday call-in segment on his January 4, 2019 episode. Fuentes identified as a Catholic integralist and expressed admiration for Jordan Peterson, who had stated a country needed to be mostly white. He rejected a pagan caller's revolutionary views, citing Donald Trump's election as evidence for change within the existing system.
