## Summary

Nicholas J. Fuentes hosts *America First* and issued a public challenge on Twitter to debate David Hogg. Following the Parkland shooting, an investigation found the FBI received but did not act on multiple tips about shooter Nikolas Cruz, and local police had been called to his home dozens of times. Donald Trump proposed raising the federal firearms purchase age to 21 after a school shooting and later called an armed school resource officer who did not intervene a "coward" at CPAC.
