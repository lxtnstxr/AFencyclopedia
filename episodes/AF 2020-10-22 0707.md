## Summary

Donald Trump and Joe Biden participated in the final 2020 presidential debate at Belmont University in Nashville, moderated by NBC's Kristen Welker. The Commission on Presidential Debates implemented a rule muting each candidate's microphone during the other's opening statements. During the debate, Trump credited his administration's early travel restrictions and promoted Operation Warp Speed, while Biden criticized the pandemic response and outlined a plan emphasizing masks and testing. Kristen Welker presented breaking news that officials confirmed Russia and Iran were working to influence the election.
