## Summary

Jared Taylor filed a lawsuit against Twitter under California state law, alleging false advertising and unfair competition. Judge Harold Cahn allowed the lawsuit to proceed, ruling that Twitter must have clear criteria for banning accounts. Twitter banned Jared Taylor's personal account and the account of American Renaissance, which he founded and edits. Nicholas J. Fuentes cited an article in The Forward arguing against association with Donald Trump supporters.
