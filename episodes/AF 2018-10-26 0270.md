## Summary

Cesar Sayoc faced five charges for sending suspicious packages addressed to Cory Booker and James Clapper. Sheriff Scott Israel, criticized for his response to the Parkland shooting, apprehended Sayoc in Florida. Donald Trump announced the arrest during a Young Black Leadership Summit hosted by Turning Point USA.
