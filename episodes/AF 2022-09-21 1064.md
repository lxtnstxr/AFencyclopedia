## Summary

The Luhansk People's Republic and Donetsk People's Republic scheduled referendums on joining Russia from September 23rd to the 27th. A New York Times/Siena College poll found 56% of Hispanic voters planned to support Democrats in the 2022 midterms, with majorities favoring Black Lives Matter and legal abortion. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued Russia's annexation of the territories would force NATO to either escalate support for Ukraine or de-escalate by cutting it off.
