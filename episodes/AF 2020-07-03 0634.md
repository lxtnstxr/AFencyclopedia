## Summary

Donald Trump delivered a July 3rd speech at Mount Rushmore following protests and road blockades by Native American groups, including the Cheyenne River Sioux Tribe. The New York Times, Associated Press, and Reuters adopted a style change to capitalize "Black" while keeping "white" lowercase, citing a shared cultural identity for the former. The Mount Rushmore event featured military flyovers from the South Dakota Air National Guard and the Blue Angels and was hosted by Mary Hart, who welcomed Governor Kristi Noem and Interior Secretary David Bernhardt. Nicholas J. Fuentes streamed the event and expressed views linking Christian identity to an "America First" political stance.
