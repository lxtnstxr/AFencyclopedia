## Summary

Several advertisers, including The Walt Disney Company and Papa John's, withdrew from Tucker Carlson's Fox News program following his comments on Black Lives Matter. Donald Trump and the Trump campaign announced the restart of campaign rallies, coinciding with Anthony Fauci and the CDC warning of a potential second coronavirus surge. Nicholas J. Fuentes described Robert E. Lee as a great American hero and argued for a new right-wing ideology based on tradition and pragmatism. He also accused Anthony Fauci and the CDC of applying a double standard by expressing concern over Trump rallies but not mass protests.
