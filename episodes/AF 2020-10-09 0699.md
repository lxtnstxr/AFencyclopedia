## Summary

Donald Trump co-hosted the Rush Limbaugh show and appeared on the Mark Levin show. The Commission on Presidential Debates canceled the second presidential debate between Donald Trump and Joe Biden after Trump rejected a virtual format. The White House Office of Personnel considered requiring political appointees to submit resignation letters before the election, a maneuver to allow a re-elected Trump to retain loyalists. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued that purging disloyal personnel was necessary to secure the populist future of the Republican Party.
