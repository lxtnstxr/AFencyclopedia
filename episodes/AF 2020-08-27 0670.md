## Summary

His administration withdrew the United States from the Iran nuclear deal, recognized Jerusalem as Israel's capital, and brokered a peace agreement between Israel and the United Arab Emirates. Trump established the U.S. Space Force and signed the First Step Act into law. Joe Biden co-authored a campaign platform with Bernie Sanders that called for suspending deportations and supported China's entry into the World Trade Organization.
