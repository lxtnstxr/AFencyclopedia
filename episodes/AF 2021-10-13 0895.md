## Summary

A federal judge in Texas blocked and later issued an injunction against a United Airlines COVID-19 vaccine mandate, scheduling a hearing on its enforcement. Netflix employees, including transgender staff, organized a walkout protesting a Dave Chappelle comedy special, after which co-CEO Ted Sarandos defended its release and the company suspended and reinstated three employees. Governors Ron DeSantis and Greg Abbott enacted state bans on COVID-19 vaccine mandates in Florida and Texas, while the Biden administration extended an eviction moratorium and announced an OSHA rule for a federal business mandate. Nicholas J. Fuentes argued that racial double standards exist in cancel culture, citing Dave Chappelle and Cardi B, while noting Nick Cannon faced professional consequences for anti-Semitic content.
